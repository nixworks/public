##### Turki al-Hamad
# Novelist Turki al-Hamad, who was jailed in December for six months without trial over his calls for reform in Saudi Arabia, is released. 

Turki al-Hamad, who was arrested in December for tweets criticising Islamism, returns home, according to an activist.
