##### Roll-on/roll-off
# A search and rescue operation gets underway after a Roll-on-roll-off ferry carrying about 60 people capsizes off the island of Burias in the Philippines Masbate province. So far 34 people have been rescued and at least one death confirmed. 

The search for survivors continues after a ferry carrying more than 50 people capsized off the Philippines coast.
