### [2013-06-6](/news/2013/06/6/index.md)

##### Barack Obama
# It is reported that an increasing number of hunger-striking Guantanamo inmates are being force fed by military medical teams since Barack Obama's recent speech promised again to close the prison camp. 

Without clear signs that Obama will fulfill promise to close facility, more detainees are refusing food.
