### [2013-06-23](/news/2013/06/23/index.md)

##### Albania
# Voters in Albania go to the polls for a parliamentary election. Activist Gjon Pjeter Gjoni is shot dead and three other people are wounded in a shoot out near a polling station in the town of Lac. 

Shootout that killed opposition activist and dispute over who certifies ballots cast shadow over crucial vote.
