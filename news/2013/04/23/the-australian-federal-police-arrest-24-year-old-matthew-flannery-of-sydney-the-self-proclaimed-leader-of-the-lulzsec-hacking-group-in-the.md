##### Australian Federal Police
# The Australian Federal Police arrest 24-year-old Matthew Flannery of Sydney, the self-proclaimed leader of the LulzSec hacking group in the New South Wales city of Gosford. 

The AFP says the self-proclaimed leader of international hacking group LulzSec has been arrested in Sydney.
