### [2013-04-9](/news/2013/04/9/index.md)

##### Downing Street
# Downing Street announces the funeral for Margaret Thatcher is scheduled for Wednesday, 17 April. She will be afforded a ceremonial exit, ranking her alongside Diana Spencer and the Queen Mother. 

Former Prime Minister Tony Blair criticises parties held to &quot;celebrate&quot; the death of Baroness Thatcher as being in &quot;pretty poor taste&quot;.
