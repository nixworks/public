##### Velika Ivanca shooting
# Velika Ivanca shooting: At least 13 people are killed and another three injured after a man goes on a spree shooting in the village of Velika Ivanca near the Serbian capital Belgrade. 

A 60-year-old man went from house to house in his village near Belgrade, killing several of his neighbors and relatives before attempting suicide.
