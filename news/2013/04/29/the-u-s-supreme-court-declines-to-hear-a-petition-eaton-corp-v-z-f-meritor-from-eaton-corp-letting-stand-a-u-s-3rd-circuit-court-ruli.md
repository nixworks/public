### [2013-04-29](/news/2013/04/29/index.md)

##### Supreme Court of the United States
# The U.S. Supreme Court declines to hear a petition (Eaton Corp v. Z F Meritor) from Eaton Corp., letting stand a U.S. 3rd Circuit Court ruling on an antitrust issue that may limit the use of loyalty discounts in the form of rebates as a way of locking in customers.
