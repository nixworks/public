# Kuwaiti opposition leader Musallam Al-Barrak is given a five-year prison sentence for insulting Emir Sabah Al-Ahmad Al-Jaber Al-Sabah. 

Prominent Kuwaiti opposition leader, Mussallam al-Barrak, is jailed for five years for insulting Emir Sheikh Sabah al-Sabah.
