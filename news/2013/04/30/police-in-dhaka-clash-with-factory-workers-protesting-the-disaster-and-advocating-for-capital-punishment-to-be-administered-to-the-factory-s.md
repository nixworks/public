### [2013-04-30](/news/2013/04/30/index.md)

# Police in Dhaka clash with factory workers protesting the disaster and advocating for capital punishment to be administered to the factory's owner. 

Bangladeshi police use batons to disperse hundreds of angry factory workers calling for the death penalty for the owner of a collapsed building.
