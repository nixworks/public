##### Greater Manchester Police
# The Greater Manchester Police becomes the first force in the United Kingdom to record attacks on members of subcultures, such as goths and emos, as hate crime. 

Hate crimes against groups such as goths or punks are to be recorded by Greater Manchester Police in the first move of its kind by a UK force.
