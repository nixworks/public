##### South Korea
# South Korean sources say a North Korean medium range missile has been moved to the eastern coast and that it may be ready for testing or for attacking Japan or Guam. 

John Everard, former UK Ambassador, explains that North Korea &quot;believe that they are the victim, [and] they believe that they are being hounded by the United States.&quot;
