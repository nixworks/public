# Authorities warn that three fires burning out of control in Australia's Blue Mountains region near the towns of Lithgow, Springwood and Mount Victoria could merge and form a "megafire". 

NSW is in a state of emergency as authorities warn of an unparalleled fire emergency and a potential mega-fire.
