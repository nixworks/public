# The UK's Privy Council grants a Royal Charter on regulating the British press after the newspaper industry loses a last minute legal bid to seek an injunction against the plans. 

A royal charter on press regulation is granted by the Privy Council, despite a last-minute legal challenge by the newspaper industry, which says it threatens their freedom.
