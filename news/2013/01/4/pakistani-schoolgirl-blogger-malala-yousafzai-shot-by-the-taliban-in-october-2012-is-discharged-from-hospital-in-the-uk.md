##### Pakistan
# Pakistani schoolgirl blogger Malala Yousafzai, shot by the Taliban in October 2012, is discharged from hospital in the UK. 

The Pakistani schoolgirl activist shot in the head by the Taliban is discharged from a Birmingham hospital as an inpatient.
