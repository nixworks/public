##### Torre Ejecutiva Pemex explosion
# Torre Ejecutiva Pemex explosion: A blast at Torre Ejecutiva Pemex, the headquarters of Pemex in Mexico City, Mexico, kills 36 and injures 126 people. 

At least 25 people are killed and another 100 injured in Mexico City in a blast at the headquarters of the state oil company, Pemex.
