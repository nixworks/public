# The President of Syria Bashar al-Assad delivers a rare television appearance addressing the Syrian crisis and denounces the Syrian rebels as "enemies of God and puppets of the West". It is his first television appearance since June 2012. 

Syrian President Bashar al-Assad delivers a rare televised speech, denouncing the opposition as &quot;puppets of the West&quot;.
