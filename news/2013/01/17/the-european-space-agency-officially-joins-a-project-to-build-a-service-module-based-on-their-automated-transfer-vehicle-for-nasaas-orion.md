### [2013-01-17](/news/2013/01/17/index.md)

# The European Space Agency officially joins a project to build a Service Module based on their Automated Transfer Vehicle for NASA's Orion, a spacecraft to carry astronauts into deep space. 

NASA signed an agreement for the European Space Agency to provide a service module for the Orion spacecraft&#039;s Exploration Mission-1 in 2017.
