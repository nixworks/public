##### 2013 Jakarta flood
# 2013 Jakarta flood: A massive flood hits Jakarta, Indonesia, with the Indonesian Army deploying rubber boats in the financial district to rescue people trapped in this flood. Four people were killed and over 2,000 evacuated. 

At least four people die and thousands are evacuated as flooding hits parts of the Indonesian capital, Jakarta, shutting down offices and traffic.
