### [2013-01-25](/news/2013/01/25/index.md)

##### Moscow
# Scuffles occur on the streets of Moscow as supporters and opponents clash over the Russian parliament's attempts to implement anti-gay legislation. If legalised it would result in fines for those who promote events with a gay theme. 

The Russian parliament backs a ban on the promotion of homosexuality among children in its first reading amid scuffles outside.
