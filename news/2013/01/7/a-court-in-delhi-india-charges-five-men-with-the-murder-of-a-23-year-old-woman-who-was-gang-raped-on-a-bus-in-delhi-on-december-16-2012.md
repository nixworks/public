##### Delhi
# A court in Delhi, India, charges five men with the murder of a 23-year-old woman who was gang-raped on a bus in Delhi on December 16, 2012. 

Five men are formally charged with the gang rape and murder of a young woman in Delhi appear in court amid chaotic scenes, in a case that has shocked India.
