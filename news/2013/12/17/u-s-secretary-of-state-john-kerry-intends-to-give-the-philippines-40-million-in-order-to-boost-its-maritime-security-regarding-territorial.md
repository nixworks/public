### [2013-12-17](/news/2013/12/17/index.md)

##### Secretary of State
# U.S. Secretary of State John Kerry intends to give the Philippines $40 million in order to boost its maritime security regarding territorial disputes with China. 

Americans officials want to rotate more military forces through the country, concerned about mounting pressure from China on its neighbors.
