##### Mali's coup
# A mass grave is discovered near Bamako holding the remains of 21 loyalist soldiers said to have gone missing last year during Mali's coup. 

Twenty-one skulls believed to be the remains of soldiers loyal to ousted former president Amadou Toumani Toure.
