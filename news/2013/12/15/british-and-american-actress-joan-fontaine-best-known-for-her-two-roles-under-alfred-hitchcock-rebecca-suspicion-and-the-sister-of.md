# British and American actress Joan Fontaine, best known for her two roles under Alfred Hitchcock ("Rebecca", "Suspicion"), and the sister of Olivia de Havilland, dies at the age of 96. 

The star of the Hitchcock classics &quot;Suspicion&quot; and &quot;Rebecca&quot; famously won an Oscar in 1942 over her bitter rival -- her older sister, Olivia de Havilland.
