##### Israeli-Lebanese conflict
# Two rockets are launched at Israel from Lebanon. Israel fires 20 artillery shells into Lebanon in retaliation. 

Rockets launched from south Lebanon struck northern Israel on Sunday and Israel responded with artillery shells across a border that has been largely quiet since a war in 2006.
