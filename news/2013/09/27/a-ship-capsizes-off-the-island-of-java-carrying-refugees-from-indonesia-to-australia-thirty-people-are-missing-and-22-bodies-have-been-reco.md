# A ship capsizes off the island of Java carrying refugees from Indonesia to Australia. Thirty people are missing and 22 bodies have been recovered, with 31 survivors rescued. 

Authorities fear the death toll could rise after a boat loaded with asylum seekers sank off the coast of Indonesia.
