# On the first anniversary of the 2012 Benghazi attack, a powerful car bomb explodes near the Libyan Foreign Ministry building in the city. 

On the anniversary of the 9/11 bombings on the US, a car bomb causes serious damage to a foreign ministry building in Benghazi.
