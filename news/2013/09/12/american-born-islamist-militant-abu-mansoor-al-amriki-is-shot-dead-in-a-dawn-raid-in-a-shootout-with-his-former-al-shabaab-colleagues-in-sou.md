##### Abu Mansoor Al-Amriki
# American born Islamist militant Abu Mansoor Al-Amriki is shot dead in a dawn raid in a shootout with his former al-Shabaab colleagues in southern Somalia. 

A US-born Islamist with a $5 billion bounty on his head is shot dead in Somalia.
