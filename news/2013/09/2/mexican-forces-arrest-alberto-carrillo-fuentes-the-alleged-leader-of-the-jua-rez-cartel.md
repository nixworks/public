##### Mexican Drug War
# Mexican forces arrest Alberto Carrillo Fuentes, the alleged leader of the Juarez Cartel. 

Mexican security forces say they have arrested the drug lord known as &#034;Ugly Betty&#034;, the alleged leader of the notorious Juarez Cartel which has for years been fighting a bloody turf war along the country&#039;s border with the United States.
