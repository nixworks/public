### [2013-08-9](/news/2013/08/9/index.md)

##### Ethiopian Air Force
# An Ethiopian Air Force cargo plane carrying ammunition for international forces fighting Islamist militants in Somalia crashes and catches fire on landing at the Aden Adde International Airport in Mogadishu, killing four of the six crewmembers.
