##### Hundreds are reported dead
# Hundreds are reported dead - including 43 police - across Egypt as Morsi supporters clash with security forces in response to a crackdown on protests. 

At least 278 people killed and month-long state of emergency imposed after security forces storm pro-Morsi camps.
