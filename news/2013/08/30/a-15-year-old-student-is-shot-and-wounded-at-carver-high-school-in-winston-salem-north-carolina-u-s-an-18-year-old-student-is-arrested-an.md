##### Carver High School
# A 15-year-old student is shot and wounded at Carver High School in Winston-Salem, North Carolina, U.S. An 18-year-old student is arrested and charged. 

A shooting at Carver High School on Friday left one student with non-life-threatening injuries and another student in custody.
