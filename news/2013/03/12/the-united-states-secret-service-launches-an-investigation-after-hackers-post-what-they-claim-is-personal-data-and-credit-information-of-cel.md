# The United States Secret Service launches an investigation after hackers post what they claim is personal data and credit information of celebrities, including First Lady Michelle Obama, online. 

The US Secret Service has launched an investigation after hackers posted what they said was personal data and credit information of celebrities, including First Lady Michelle Obama, online.
