##### Western Australia
# Voters in the state of Western Australia go to the polls for a state election with the coalition government of Colin Barnett winning a convincing victory. 

West Australian Premier Colin Barnett has played down polling pointing to a landslide win for the Liberals, as he went to the ballot box on Saturday.
