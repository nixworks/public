##### 2013 Compagnie Africaine d'Aviation crash
# 2013 Compagnie Africaine d'Aviation crash: A Fokker 50 operated by Compagnie Africaine d'Aviation crashes in poor weather at Goma, Democratic Republic of the Congo. Nine people were in the aircraft, a crew of five and four passengers. At least three people survived the accident. 

A Congolese airplane attempting to land during a rainstorm crashed in the eastern city of Goma, carrying at least nine people, according to the airline.
