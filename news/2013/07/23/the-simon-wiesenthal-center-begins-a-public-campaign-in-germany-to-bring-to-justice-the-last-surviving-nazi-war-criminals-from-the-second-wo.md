##### Simon Wiesenthal Center
# The Simon Wiesenthal Center begins a public campaign in Germany to bring to justice the last surviving Nazi war criminals from the Second World War. 

A poster campaign is launched in Germany aimed at tracking down the last surviving Nazi war criminals not yet brought to justice.
