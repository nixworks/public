### [2013-07-23](/news/2013/07/23/index.md)

# A bomb kills eight people in Afghanistan's Wardak Province, including three United States Army soldiers, four Afghan National Army soldiers and an Afghan interpreter. 

An insurgent riding a donkey detonated a bomb in one of the most hotly contested districts in the country, officials said.
