# Mohamed Brahmi, the leader of the Tunisian People's Movement, is fatally shot outside his house in Tunis, sparking clashes between police and protesters. 

Gunmen assassinate Tunisian opposition leader Mohamed Brahmi, sparking protests in cities across the country.
