### [2013-07-24](/news/2013/07/24/index.md)

# Clashes between the Knights Templar Cartel and the Mexican federal police leave 22 dead in the state of Michoacn. According to government press reports, two of the dead were law enforcement officials, while the rest were cartel gunmen.
