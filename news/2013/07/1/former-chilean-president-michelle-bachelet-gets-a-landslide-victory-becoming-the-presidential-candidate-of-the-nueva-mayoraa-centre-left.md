##### Michelle Bachelet
# Former Chilean president Michelle Bachelet gets a landslide victory, becoming the presidential candidate of the "Nueva Mayoria" centre-left coalition for the next November elections. Pablo Longueira, former Minister of Economy, wins the centre-right primary.
