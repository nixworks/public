# 39 musicians, including The Beatles, U2, Madonna, Lady Gaga, Beyonce, Katy Perry, Lorde, One Direction, and Justin Bieber, release a charity album to help the victims. 

Stars including Beyonce and Bob Dylan are among those to donate tracks to an album that will raise money for the Philippines Red Cross.
