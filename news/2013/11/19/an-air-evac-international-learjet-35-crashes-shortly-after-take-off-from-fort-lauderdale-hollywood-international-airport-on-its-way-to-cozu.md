##### Air Evac International
# An Air Evac International Learjet 35 crashes shortly after take-off from Fort Lauderdale-Hollywood International Airport, on its way to Cozumel, Mexico, after calling mayday and during an attempt to return to the airport, possibly due to engine failure, leaving 2 persons dead and two missing as a result.
