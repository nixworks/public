### [Syrian civil war:: Syrian government air strikes around the northern Syrian city of Aleppo kill 40 people. ](/news/2013/11/23/syrian-civil-war-syrian-government-air-strikes-around-the-northern-syrian-city-of-aleppo-kill-40-people.md)

### [Iraqi insurgency (post-U.S. withdrawal):: A car bomb and a suicide bomber kills nine people and wounds 54. ](/news/2013/11/23/iraqi-insurgency-post-u-s-withdrawal-a-car-bomb-and-a-suicide-bomber-kills-nine-people-and-wounds-54.md)
A car bomb and a suicide bomber killed at least nine people and wounded 54 in northern Iraq on Saturday, police and medical sources said, in the latest of a wave of attacks in crowded public places.

### [Islamist insurgency in Nigeria:: Gunmen kill 12 people in a raid on a Northeastern Nigerian village. ](/news/2013/11/23/islamist-insurgency-in-nigeria-gunmen-kill-12-people-in-a-raid-on-a-northeastern-nigerian-village.md)
