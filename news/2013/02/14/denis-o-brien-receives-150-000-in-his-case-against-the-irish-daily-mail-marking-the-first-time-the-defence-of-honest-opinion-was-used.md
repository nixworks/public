### [2013-02-14](/news/2013/02/14/index.md)

# Denis O'Brien receives -150,000 in his case against the "Irish Daily Mail", marking the first time the defence of honest opinion was used in a defamation case in Ireland after its introduction as part of the Defamation Act 2009. 

Businessman Denis O&#39;Brien has been awarded €150,000 by a jury at the High Court after he won his defamation claim against Associated Newspapers, publishers of the Irish Daily Mail.
