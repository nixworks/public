##### 2013 BRIT Awards
# The 2013 BRIT Awards are held in London with Ben Howard winning best British male artist and British breakthrough act, Emeli Sande winning best British female act and album of the year for "Our Version of Events" and Mumford & Sons winning Best British group. 

Olympic opening ceremony singer Emeli Sand&eacute;, has won best album and best British female at this year&rsquo;s Brit Awards, as Adele scooped best single.
