### [2013-02-5](/news/2013/02/5/index.md)

##### North Korea
# A North Korean propaganda film, which featured New York in flames, is taken down by Activision due to the video using footage from Modern Warfare 3. 

A video posted by North Korea is taken offline after games maker Activision makes a copyright claim over use of footage from Call of Duty.
