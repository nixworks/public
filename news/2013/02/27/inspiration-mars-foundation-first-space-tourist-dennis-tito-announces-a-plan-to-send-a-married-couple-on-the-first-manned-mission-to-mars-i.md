##### Inspiration Mars Foundation
# Inspiration Mars Foundation: First space tourist Dennis Tito announces a plan to send a married couple on the first manned mission to Mars in January 2018 on a round-trip journey lasting 501 days. 

A new nonprofit foundation headed by the first private space traveler, Dennis Tito, seeks to take advantage of a once-in-15-years alignment of planets to beat NASA astronauts to Mars.
