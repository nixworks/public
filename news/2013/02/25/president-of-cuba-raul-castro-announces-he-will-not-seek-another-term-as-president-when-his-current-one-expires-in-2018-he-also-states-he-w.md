### [2013-02-25](/news/2013/02/25/index.md)

##### President
# President of Cuba Ral Castro announces he will not seek another term as president when his current one expires in 2018. He also states he will seek to put term limits and age caps for political offices in Cuba, including the presidency. 

Mr. Castro, whose term will end in 2018, said he hoped to establish term limits and age caps for political offices, including the presidency.
