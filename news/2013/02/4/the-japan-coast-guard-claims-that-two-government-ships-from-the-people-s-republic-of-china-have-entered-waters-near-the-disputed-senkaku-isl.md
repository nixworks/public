##### Japan Coast Guard
# The Japan Coast Guard claims that two government ships from the People's Republic of China have entered waters near the disputed Senkaku Islands. 

THE Japanese coastguard says two Chinese government ships have entered waters around Tokyo-controlled islands that Beijing claims as it own.
