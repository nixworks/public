##### China
# An international team of scientists announce the discovery in China of a new meat-eating Theropod dinosaur, "Aorun zhaoi", dating from 161 million years ago. It is the oldest Coelurosaur known to date. 

A new species of meat-eating dinosaurs have been discovered in China.
