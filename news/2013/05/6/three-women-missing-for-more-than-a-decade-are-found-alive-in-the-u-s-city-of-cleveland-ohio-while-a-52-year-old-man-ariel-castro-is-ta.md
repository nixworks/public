##### Three women missing
# Three women missing for more than a decade are found alive in the U.S. city of Cleveland, Ohio, while a 52-year-old man, Ariel Castro, is taken into custody. 

THREE women have emerged from a house in Cleveland, Ohio, claiming to have spent years in captivity.
