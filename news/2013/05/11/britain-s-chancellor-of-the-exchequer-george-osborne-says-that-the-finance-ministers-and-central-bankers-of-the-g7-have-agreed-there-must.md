### [2013-05-11](/news/2013/05/11/index.md)

##### United Kingdom
# Britain's Chancellor of the Exchequer, George Osborne, says that the finance ministers and central bankers of the G7 have agreed there must be co-ordinated action against tax evaders. 

The chancellor says the G7 group of industrialised nations have agreed there needs to be collective action to target tax evasion and avoidance.
