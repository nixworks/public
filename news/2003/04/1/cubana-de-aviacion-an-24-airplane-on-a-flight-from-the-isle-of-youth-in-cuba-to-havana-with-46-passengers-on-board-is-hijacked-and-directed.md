### [2003-04-1](/news/2003/04/1/index.md)

##### Cubana de Aviacin
#  Cubana de Aviacin AN-24 airplane on a flight from the Isle of Youth in Cuba to Havana with 46 passengers on board is hijacked and directed towards the United States. After refueling in Havana the plane flew to Key West, under escort by two US jet fighters. The plane landed safely in Key West.
