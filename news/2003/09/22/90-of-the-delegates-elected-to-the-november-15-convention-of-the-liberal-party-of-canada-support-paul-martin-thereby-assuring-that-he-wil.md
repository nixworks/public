### [2003-09-22](/news/2003/09/22/index.md)

##### Liberal Party's
#  90% of the delegates elected to the November 15 convention of the Liberal Party of Canada support Paul Martin, thereby assuring that he will win the leadership of the party and thereby become Prime Minister of Canada after Jean Chrtien retires in February 2004.
