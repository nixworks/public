##### The Lord of the Rings: The Return of the King
#  "The Lord of the Rings: The Return of the King", the final part of Peter Jackson's film adaptation of J. R. R. Tolkien's "The Lord of the Rings", goes on broad public release in the United States and much of Europe. Industry pundits predicted that it could have become the second film, after "Titanic", to earn over US$1 billion at the box office. [http:--news.bbc.co.uk-1-hi-entertainment-film-3325051.stm]
