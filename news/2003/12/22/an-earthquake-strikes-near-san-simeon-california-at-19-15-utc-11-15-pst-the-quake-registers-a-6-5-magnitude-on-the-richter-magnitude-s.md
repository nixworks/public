### [2003-12-22](/news/2003/12/22/index.md)

##### Earthquake
#  An earthquake strikes near San Simeon, California, at 19:15 UTC (11:15 PST). The quake registers a 6.5 magnitude on the Richter magnitude scale, and causes two deaths from the collapse of a building in the town of Paso Robles.
