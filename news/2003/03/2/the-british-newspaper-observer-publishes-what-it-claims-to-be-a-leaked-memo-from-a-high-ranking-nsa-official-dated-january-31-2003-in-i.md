### [2003-03-2](/news/2003/03/2/index.md)

##### The Observer
#  The British newspaper "Observer" publishes what it claims to be a leaked memo from a high-ranking NSA official dated January 31, 2003. In it are orders to spy on the domestic and official communications of the United Nations Security Council members other than the U.S. and the United Kingdom. The memo names "... members Angola, Cameroon, Chile, Bulgaria and Guinea,&nbsp;..." as candidates for special attention.
