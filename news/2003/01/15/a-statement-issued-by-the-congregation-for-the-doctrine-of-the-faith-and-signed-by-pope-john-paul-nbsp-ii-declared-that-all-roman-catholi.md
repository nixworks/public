### [2003-01-15](/news/2003/01/15/index.md)

##### Congregation for the Doctrine of the Faith
#  A statement, issued by the Congregation for the Doctrine of the Faith and signed by Pope John Paul&nbsp;II, declared that all Roman Catholic politicians must adhere to church teachings, and that they cannot be faithful to the Church if they vote against Church positions on such issues as abortion.
