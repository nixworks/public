### [2003-10-28](/news/2003/10/28/index.md)

##### Halloween Solar Storms
#  Halloween solar storms, 2003. The sun emits another coronal mass ejection directly toward the Earth, the third largest solar flare on record. The effects are projected to affect Earth on the 29th.
