### [2003-02-17](/news/2003/02/17/index.md)

##### London
#  London congestion charging begins: Motorists must pay GBP5 per day to enter central London. This attempt by the Transport for London group to reduce traffic density and pollution, and encourage use of public transport is being followed closely by cities around the world.
