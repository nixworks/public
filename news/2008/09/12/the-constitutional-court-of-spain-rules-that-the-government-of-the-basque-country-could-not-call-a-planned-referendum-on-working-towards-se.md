### [2008-09-12](/news/2008/09/12/index.md)

##### Constitutional Court
#  The Constitutional Court of Spain rules that the Government of the Basque Country could not call a planned referendum on working towards self-determination as only the Government of Spain could call such a vote; the Basque Government said it would appeal to the European Court of Human Rights.
