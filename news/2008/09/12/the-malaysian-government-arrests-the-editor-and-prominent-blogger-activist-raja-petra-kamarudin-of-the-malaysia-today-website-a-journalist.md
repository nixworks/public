### [2008-09-12](/news/2008/09/12/index.md)

##### Malaysia
#  The Malaysian government arrests the editor and prominent blogger activist Raja Petra Kamarudin of the Malaysia Today website, a journalist from Sin Chew Jit Poh and opposition politician Teresa Kok under the Internal Security Act.
