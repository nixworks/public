##### Haile Gebrselassie
#  Haile Gebrselassie of Ethiopia sets a new world record in the marathon of two hours three minutes 58 seconds at the Berlin Marathon. 

Ethiopian Haile Gebrselassie shattered his own world marathon record Sunday when he became the first man to run under two hours four minutes.
