#  Russian and Georgian forces exchange prisoners of war. [http:--www.alertnet.org-thenews-newsdesk-LJ326260.htm (Reuters)] Russia begins to withdraw troops from Georgia proper according to eyewitness accounts. [http:--www.reuters.com-article-worldNews-idINIndia-35081820080819 (Reuters)] Russia temporarily closes its borders with Georgia and Azerbaijan to prevent terrorist groups from crossing into Russia. [http:--www.delfi.lt-news-daily-world-article.php?id=18196858 (Delfi)] [http:--www.alertnet.org-thenews-newsdesk-LJ292582.htm (Reuters)] A United Nations aid convoy which entered Gori on Sunday reports evidence of large-scale looting. [http:--www.canada.com-vancouversun-news-story.html?id=27b09bd1-80b6-4921-9970-582b8dc82928 ("The Vancouver Sun")] Alexander Stubb, the current Chairman of the Organization for Security and Cooperation in Europe says that Russia has agreed to a beefed-up monitoring mission for Georgia's disputed region of South Ossetia.
