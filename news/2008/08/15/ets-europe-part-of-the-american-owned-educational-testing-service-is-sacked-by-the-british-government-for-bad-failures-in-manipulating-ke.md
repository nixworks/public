### [2008-08-15](/news/2008/08/15/index.md)

##### ETS Europe
#  ETS Europe, part of the American-owned Educational Testing Service, is sacked by the British government for bad failures in manipulating Key Stage education tests. ETS agrees to repay some GBP35 million (USD70 million).
