### [2008-08-16](/news/2008/08/16/index.md)

#  Georgian officials claim Abkhazian army, backed by the Armed Forces of the Russian Federation, takes control of 13 Georgian villages. 

Rebels from the breakaway region of Abkhazia, backed by Russian forces, have seized control of 13 Georgian villages and land surrounding a power plant on the border of the region, Georgia&#039;s foreign ministry said.
