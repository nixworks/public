### [2008-08-29](/news/2008/08/29/index.md)

##### Ha Giang Province
#  At least 11 people die this week and five are missing as a result of floods and landslides caused by heavy rain in Ha Giang Province in northern Vietnam. 

At least 11 people have been killed and five are missing in heavy rains triggered flash floods and landslides in the mountains of northern Vietnam this week.
