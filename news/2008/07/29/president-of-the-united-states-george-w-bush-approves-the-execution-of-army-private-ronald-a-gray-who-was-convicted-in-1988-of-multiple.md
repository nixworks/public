### [2008-07-29](/news/2008/07/29/index.md)

##### U.S. President
#  President of the United States George W. Bush approves the execution of Army Private Ronald A. Gray, who was convicted in 1988 of multiple rapes and murders. It is the first American military execution approved since 1961. Further appeals are expected before he is executed.
