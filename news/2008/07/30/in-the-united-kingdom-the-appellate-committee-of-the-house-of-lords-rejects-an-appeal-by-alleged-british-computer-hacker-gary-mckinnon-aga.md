### [2008-07-30](/news/2008/07/30/index.md)

##### United Kingdom
#  In the United Kingdom, the Appellate Committee of the House of Lords rejects an appeal by alleged British computer hacker Gary McKinnon against extradition to the United States to face charges of hacking into Pentagon and NASA computers.
