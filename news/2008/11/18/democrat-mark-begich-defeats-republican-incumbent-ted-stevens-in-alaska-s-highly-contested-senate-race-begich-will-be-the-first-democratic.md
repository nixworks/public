### [2008-11-18](/news/2008/11/18/index.md)

##### Democrat
#  Democrat Mark Begich defeats Republican incumbent Ted Stevens in Alaska's highly contested Senate race. Begich will be the first Democratic senator representing the state in twenty-eight years. 

The Republican held a commanding place in Alaska politics while wielding power on some of the most influential committees in Congress.
