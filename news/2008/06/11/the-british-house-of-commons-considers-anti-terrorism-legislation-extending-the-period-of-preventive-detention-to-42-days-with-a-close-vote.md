### [2008-06-11](/news/2008/06/11/index.md)

##### British House of Commons
#  The British House of Commons considers anti-terrorism legislation extending the period of preventive detention to 42 days with a close vote expected due to a backbench revolt in the Labour Party. The House eventually passes the bill by 315 votes to 306.
