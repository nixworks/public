### [2008-06-10](/news/2008/06/10/index.md)

##### Sudan Airways Flight 109
#  Sudan Airways Flight 109 crashes on landing at Khartoum International Airport in Khartoum, Sudan, with around 200 on board. The death toll is 28 with 66 people unaccounted for.

Officials say confirmed death toll from Khartoum plane crash now 28, not 120 as previously stated.
