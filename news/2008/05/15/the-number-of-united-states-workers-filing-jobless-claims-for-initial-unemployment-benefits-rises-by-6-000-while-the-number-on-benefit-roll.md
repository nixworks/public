#  The number of United States workers filing jobless claims for initial unemployment benefits rises by 6,000 while the number on benefit rolls after a first week of aid hit a four-year high. 

The number of U.S. workersfiling claims for initial jobless benefits rose by 6,000 in thelatest week while the number on benefit rolls after a firstweek of aid hit a four-year high, a government report showed onThursday.
