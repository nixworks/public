### [2008-05-16](/news/2008/05/16/index.md)

##### Court of Arbitration for Sport
#  The Court of Arbitration for Sport overturns the IAAF's decision to ban South African double-amputee runner Oscar Pistorius from its competitions, thus clearing the way for him to attempt to become the first leg amputee to participate in the Olympic Games.
