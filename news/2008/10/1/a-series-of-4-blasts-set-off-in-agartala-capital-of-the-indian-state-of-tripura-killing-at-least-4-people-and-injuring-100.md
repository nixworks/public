##### Series of 4 blasts
#  A series of 4 blasts set off in Agartala, capital of the Indian state of Tripura, killing at least 4 people and injuring 100. 

Terrorists struck Agartala for the first time as four people were killed and 76 injured in five blasts. Police suspect HuJI hand.
