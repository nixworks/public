##### Blase Cupich
# Bishop Blase Cupich of the Roman Catholic Diocese of Spokane is appointed by Pope Francis to succeed Cardinal Francis Eugene George, O.M.I., 77, who is ill with a recurrence of cancer, as Archbishop of the Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Chicago. 

Bishop Blase J. Cupich of Spokane, Wash., has been chairman of the bishops&#8217; committee responding to the sexual abuse crisis, and has been unusually forthright in criticizing the church&#8217;s record on abuse.
