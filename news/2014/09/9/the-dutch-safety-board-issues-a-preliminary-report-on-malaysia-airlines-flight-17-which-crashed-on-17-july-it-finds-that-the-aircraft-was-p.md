### [2014-09-9](/news/2014/09/9/index.md)

##### Malaysia Airlines Flight 17
# The Dutch Safety Board issues a preliminary report on Malaysia Airlines Flight 17 which crashed on 17 July. It finds that the aircraft was probably hit by multiple "high-energy objects that penetrated the aircraft from outside" indicating a missile. 

Dutch experts say Malaysia Airlines flight MH17 crashed in east Ukraine after being hit by objects that &quot;pierced the plane at high velocity&quot;.
