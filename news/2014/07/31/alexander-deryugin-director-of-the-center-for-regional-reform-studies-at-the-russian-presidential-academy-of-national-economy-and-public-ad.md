### [2014-07-31](/news/2014/07/31/index.md)

##### Russian Presidential Academy of National Economy and Public Administration
# Alexander Deryugin, director of the Center for Regional Reform Studies at the Russian Presidential Academy of National Economy and Public Administration, says that sanctions against entities investing in Crimea have generated a great level of risk even for Russian companies because they too would face problems when working abroad after being exposed in Crimea.
