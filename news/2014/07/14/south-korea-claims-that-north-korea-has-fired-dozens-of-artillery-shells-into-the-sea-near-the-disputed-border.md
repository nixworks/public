# South Korea claims that North Korea has fired dozens of artillery shells into the sea near the disputed border. 

A day after launching two ballistic missiles from a base near the border with archrival South Korea, Pyongyang on Monday fired a barrage of artillery shells into waters near its eastern sea border with the South
