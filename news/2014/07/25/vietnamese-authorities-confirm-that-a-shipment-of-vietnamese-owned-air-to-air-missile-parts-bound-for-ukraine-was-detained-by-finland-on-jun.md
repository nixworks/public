##### Vietnam
# Vietnamese authorities confirm that a shipment of Vietnamese-owned air-to-air missile parts bound for Ukraine was detained by Finland on June 24 after raising concerns the consignment of military hardware violated arms-export regulations. 

A shipment of military hardware bound for Ukraine has been impounded in Helsinki after authorities raised concerns the consignment violated arms-export regulations.
