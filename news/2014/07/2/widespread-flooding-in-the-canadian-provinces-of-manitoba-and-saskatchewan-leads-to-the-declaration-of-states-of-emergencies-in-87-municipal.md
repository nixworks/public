# Widespread flooding in the Canadian provinces of Manitoba and Saskatchewan leads to the declaration of states of emergencies in 87 municipalities. 

Widespread overland flooding in Saskatchewan and Manitoba has prompted the evacuation of some homes in western Manitoba on Tuesday night.
