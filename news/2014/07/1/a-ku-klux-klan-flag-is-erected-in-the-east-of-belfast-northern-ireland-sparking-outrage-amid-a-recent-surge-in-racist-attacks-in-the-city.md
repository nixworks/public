##### Ku Klux Klan
# A Ku Klux Klan flag is erected in the east of Belfast, Northern Ireland, sparking outrage amid a recent surge in racist attacks in the city. 

MP Naomi Long condemns those who put up the flag off Island Street, accusing them of exacerbating racist tensions in the area
