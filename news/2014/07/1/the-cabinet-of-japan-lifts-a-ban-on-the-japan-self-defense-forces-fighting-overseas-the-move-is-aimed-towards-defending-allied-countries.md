# The Cabinet of Japan lifts a ban on the Japan Self-Defense Forces fighting overseas. The move is aimed towards defending allied countries. 

Cabinet approves changes to pacifist constitution allowing Japan&#039;s military to fight overseas for the first time since WWII.
