### [2014-10-11](/news/2014/10/11/index.md)

# Cyclone Hudhud strengthens over the Bay of Bengal with one person having already died in the storm. Mass evacuations of thousands of people is occurring as the storm approaches the east coast. 

India has begun evacuating thousands of people from fishing villages as it braces for cyclone Hudhud barrelling towards its east coast.
