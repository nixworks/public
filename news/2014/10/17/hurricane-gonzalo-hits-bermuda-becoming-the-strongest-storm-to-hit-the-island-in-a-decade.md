# Hurricane Gonzalo hits Bermuda becoming the strongest storm to hit the island in a decade. 

Bermuda issued a hurricane watch for Category 4 Hurricane Gonzalo, which is expected to pass &quot;perilously close&quot; to the island on Friday.
