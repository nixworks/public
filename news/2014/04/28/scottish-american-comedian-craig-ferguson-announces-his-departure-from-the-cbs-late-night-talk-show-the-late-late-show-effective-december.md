### [2014-04-28](/news/2014/04/28/index.md)

# Scottish-American comedian Craig Ferguson announces his departure from the CBS late-night talk show "The Late Late Show", effective December 2014. 

The late-night landscape continues its upheaval. During Monday&#39;s taping of CBS&#39; The Late Late Show, host Craig Ferguson told his studio audience that he will be leaving the talk show in December.
