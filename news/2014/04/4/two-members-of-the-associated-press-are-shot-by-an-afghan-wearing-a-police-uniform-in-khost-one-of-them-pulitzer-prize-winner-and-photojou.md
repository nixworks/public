##### Associated Press
# Two members of the Associated Press are shot by an Afghan wearing a police uniform in Khost. One of them, Pulitzer Prize winner and photojournalist Anja Niedringhaus, is killed. The other is seriously injured. 

An Afghan police officer kills a veteran Associated Press photographer and wounds her colleague a day before presidential polls.
