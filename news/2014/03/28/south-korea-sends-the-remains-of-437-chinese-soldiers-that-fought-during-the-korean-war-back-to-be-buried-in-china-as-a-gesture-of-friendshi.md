##### South Korea
# South Korea sends the remains of 437 Chinese soldiers that fought during the Korean War back to be buried in China as a gesture of friendship and healing between the two countries. 

 South Korea flew the remains of 437 Chinese soldiers killed during the 1950-53 Korean War to China in a solemn ceremony on Friday for final burial in their homeland.
