##### Boeing 787
# A Boeing 787 controlled by Japan Airlines makes an emergency landing at Honolulu Airport in Honolulu, Hawaii, after oil pressure in their right engine dropped. 

The Boeing 787 had engine troubles, forcing it to land early; same plane had experienced mysterious battery issues last year, but was allowed to fly again
