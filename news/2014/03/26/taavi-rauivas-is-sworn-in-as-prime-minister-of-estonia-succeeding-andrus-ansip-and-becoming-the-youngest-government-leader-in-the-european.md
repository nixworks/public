##### Taavi Roivas
# Taavi Roivas is sworn in as Prime Minister of Estonia, succeeding Andrus Ansip and becoming the youngest government leader in the European Union at 34 years. 

Taavi Rõivas&#039;s Reform Party and Social Democrat coalition has been sworn in as the new government of Estonia.
