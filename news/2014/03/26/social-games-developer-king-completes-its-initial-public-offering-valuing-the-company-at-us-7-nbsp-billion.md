# Social games developer King completes its initial public offering, valuing the company at US$7&nbsp;billion. 

King Digital Entertainment, the Irish parent company of the mobile game Candy Crush Saga, has priced its hotly-anticipated initial public offering at the exact midpoint of the expected range.
