##### Garda Sochna Ombudsman Commission
# The Garda Sochna Ombudsman Commission (GSOC), Ireland's police Ombudsman, is revealed to have been bugged, with emails, wi-fi and phone systems at its Dublin headquarters also accessed illegally and internationally. 

A report today claimed emails, wi-fi and phone systems were hacked and a phone in a conference room at the building was bugged.
