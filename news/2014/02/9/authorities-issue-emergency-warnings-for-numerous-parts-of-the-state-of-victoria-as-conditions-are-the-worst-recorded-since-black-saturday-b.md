##### Victoria
# Authorities issue emergency warnings for numerous parts of the state of Victoria as conditions are the worst recorded since Black Saturday bushfires on February 7, 2009. 

Emergency warnings have been issued for 10 areas throughout the state as bushfires escalate in extreme conditions.
