##### US National Academy of Sciences
# The US National Academy of Sciences found that pollution from China's manufacturing industry drifts across the Pacific Ocean and affects US cities. 

Pollution from China is travelling in large quantities to the US, with a large proportion of emissions attributed to US-China trade.
