##### Syrian Civil War
# 500 people are killed in inter-factional fighting between an alliance of secular rebels and moderate Islamists against radical Islamists. 

Almost 500 people have been killed in clashes between Syrian rebels and the al-Qaeda-linked Islamist State in Iraq and the Levant, activists say.
