### [2014-11-16](/news/2014/11/16/index.md)

##### Russia
# The Russian Presidential Library announces plans to establish a Russian version of Wikipedia stating that it would provide more "detailed and accurate" information about the country. 

Russia plans to create its own Wikipedia to ensure its citizens have access to more detailed and reliable information about their country.
