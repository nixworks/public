# A vehicular attack in Jerusalem leaves one person dead and injures fourteen others as Hamas claims responsibility and calls for similar acts. 

Victim is Border Police officer from Druze village; another casualty in critical condition; police shoot and kill the attacker, a Hamas member from Shuafat
