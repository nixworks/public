### [2014-11-27](/news/2014/11/27/index.md)

##### European Parliament
# The European Parliament passes a symbolic motion calling for the break-up of American tech giant Google. 

While the vote poses no immediate threat to the company, it symbolizes the growing resentment in Europe toward the American technology titan.
