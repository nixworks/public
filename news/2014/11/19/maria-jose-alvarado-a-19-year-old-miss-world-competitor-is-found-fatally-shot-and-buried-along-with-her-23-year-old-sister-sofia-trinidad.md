### [2014-11-19](/news/2014/11/19/index.md)

##### Maria Jose Alvarado
# Maria Jose Alvarado, a 19-year-old Miss World competitor, is found fatally shot and buried along with her 23-year-old sister Sofia Trinidad in Santa Barbara, Honduras. The boyfriend of Sofia Trinidad, Plutarco Ruiz and an accomplice are arrested. 

The boyfriend of Maria Jose Alvarado&#039;s sister Sofia Trinidad is one of two men arrested since the women vanished after a party.
