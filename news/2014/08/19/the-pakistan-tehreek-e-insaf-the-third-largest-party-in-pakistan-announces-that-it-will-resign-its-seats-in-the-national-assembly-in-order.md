##### Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf
# The Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf, the third largest party in Pakistan, announces that it will resign its seats in the National Assembly in order to force Prime Minister Nawaz Sharif to resign. 

Imran Khan&#039;s opposition party will resign its seats in Pakistan&#039;s national assembly, a senior official says.
