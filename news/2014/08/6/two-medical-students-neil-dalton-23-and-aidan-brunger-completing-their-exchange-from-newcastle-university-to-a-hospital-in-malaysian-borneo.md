# Two medical students Neil Dalton, 23 and Aidan Brunger completing their exchange from Newcastle University to a hospital in Malaysian Borneo were murdered after 4 men followed them after an argument over singing in a bar and stabbed them from behind. 

Neil Dalton and Aidan Brunger were warned about the dangers of drinking in local bars just days before they were brutally knifed to death in Kuching
