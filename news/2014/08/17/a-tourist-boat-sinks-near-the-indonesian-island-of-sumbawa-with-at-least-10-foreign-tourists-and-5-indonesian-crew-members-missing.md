##### Indonesia
# A tourist boat sinks near the Indonesian island of Sumbawa, with at least 10 foreign tourists and 5 Indonesian crew members missing. 

Twenty-three people have been rescued but two remain missing after a boat carrying foreign tourists sank in eastern Indonesia.
