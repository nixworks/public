### [2014-06-18](/news/2014/06/18/index.md)

##### Wessington Springs, SD
# [http:--www.ustornadoes.com-2014-06-23-tornado-digest-after-three-big-days-in-june-will-we-finish-the-month-strong- June 16th- 18th Tornado outbreak] continues. Tornadoes rip through parts of the northern plains region again affecting mainly South Dakota. Two towns hard hit are Wessington Springs, SD, by an EF2 tornado, and Alpena, SD, by an EF4 tornado.
