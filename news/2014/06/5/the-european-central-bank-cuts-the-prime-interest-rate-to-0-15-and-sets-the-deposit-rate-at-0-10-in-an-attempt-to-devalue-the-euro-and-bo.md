### [2014-06-5](/news/2014/06/5/index.md)

##### European Central Bank
# The European Central Bank cuts the prime interest rate to 0.15% and sets the deposit rate at -0.10% in an attempt to devalue the euro and boost the stagnant Eurozone economy. 

The central bank cut its benchmark interest rate to 0.15 percent, a record low.
