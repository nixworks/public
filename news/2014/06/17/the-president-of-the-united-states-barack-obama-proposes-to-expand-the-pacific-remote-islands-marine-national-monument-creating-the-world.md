### [2014-06-17](/news/2014/06/17/index.md)

##### President
# The President of the United States, Barack Obama, proposes to expand the Pacific Remote Islands Marine National Monument, creating the world's largest marine sanctuary in the Central Pacific Ocean. 

The plan would close a broad swath of central Pacific to fishing, energy exploration and other activities.
