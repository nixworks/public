##### Giorgio Orsoni
# 35 people, including Giorgio Orsoni, the Mayor of Venice, are arrested in Italy on corruption charges in connection with the MOSE Project. 

The mayor of Venice is among 35 people arrested in Italy over alleged corruption in connection with new flood barriers being built around the city.
