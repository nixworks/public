### [2014-05-27](/news/2014/05/27/index.md)

##### Barack Obama
# Barack Obama, the President of the United States, announces that 9,800 U.S. troops will stay in Afghanistan for a year after the withdrawal of combat troops in December. 

The troops will include troops to train and advise Afghanistan&rsquo;s military and a group of Special Operations forces.
