### [2014-05-20](/news/2014/05/20/index.md)

##### Narendra Modi
# Narendra Modi is officially re-elected as the leader of the Bharatiya Janata Party and the National Democratic Alliance ahead of being sworn in as the Prime Minister of India. 

Modi will meet President Pranab Mukherjee after his election in the first formal step before his swearing-in.
