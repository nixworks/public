##### Meriam Yehya Ibrahim
# An eight month pregnant Christian woman, Meriam Yehya Ibrahim, is sentenced to death by hanging for apostasy in Sudan in a case that has drawn widespread international condemnation.

A 27-year-old heavily pregnant woman in Sudan is expected to learn today whether she will be sentenced to death for apostasy.
