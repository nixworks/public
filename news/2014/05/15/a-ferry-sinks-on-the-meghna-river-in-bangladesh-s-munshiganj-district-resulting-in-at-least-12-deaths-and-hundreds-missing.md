##### Meghna River
# A ferry sinks on the Meghna River in Bangladesh's Munshiganj District resulting in at least 12 deaths and hundreds missing. 

Twenty-five bodies have been recovered after a Bangladeshi ferry sank in a deep river, with the death toll expected to rise steeply.
