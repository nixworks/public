##### Sony Pictures Entertainment
# Sony Pictures Entertainment reportedly shuts production on several sets because of computer network issues regarding employee disbursement of pay following a massive computer hack. 

Sony Pictures has abandoned film shoots because hackers have crippled its computer network, The Times of London reported Saturday.
