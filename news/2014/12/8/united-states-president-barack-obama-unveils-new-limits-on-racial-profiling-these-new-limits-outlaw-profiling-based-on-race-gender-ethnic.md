### [2014-12-8](/news/2014/12/8/index.md)

##### Barack Obama
# United States President Barack Obama unveils new limits on racial profiling. These new limits outlaw profiling based on race, gender, ethnicity, nationality, religion, or sexual orientation. 

Attorney General Eric Holder says he hopes state and local police will adopt the new guidelines for federal law enforcers.
