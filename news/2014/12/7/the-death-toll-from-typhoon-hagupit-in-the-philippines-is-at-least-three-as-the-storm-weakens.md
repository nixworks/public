##### Typhoon Hagupit
# The death toll from Typhoon Hagupit in the Philippines is at least three as the storm weakens. 

Tropical Storm Hagupit weakens as it sweeps across the Philippines towards Manila, after leaving 21 dead and forcing a million to seek shelter.
