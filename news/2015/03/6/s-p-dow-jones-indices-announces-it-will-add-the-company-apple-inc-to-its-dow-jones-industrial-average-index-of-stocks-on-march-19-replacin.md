### [2015-03-6](/news/2015/03/6/index.md)

##### S&P Dow Jones Indices
# S&P Dow Jones Indices announces it will add the company Apple Inc. to its Dow Jones Industrial Average index of stocks on March 19, replacing AT&T. 

The step is a reminder of the soaring valuations among consumer-driven technology and social media companies.
