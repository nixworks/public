##### Brooklyn
# A fire in Brooklyn, New York kills seven children and leaves two others critically injured. 

A fire that ripped through the home of an Orthodox Jewish family, trapping the children — ages 5 to 16 — in their bedrooms, was sparked by a malfunctioning hot plate, officials said.
