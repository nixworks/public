##### Cyclone Pam
# Authorities in Vanuatu report that it faces imminent shortages of food and water in the wake of Cyclone Pam. 

Vanuatu warns it faces imminent food shortages in the wake of Cyclone Pam that struck the archipelago on Friday night.
