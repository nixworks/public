### [2015-03-17](/news/2015/03/17/index.md)

##### Likud
# The Likud party, led by incumbent Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, wins the most seats in the Knesset, winning 30 out of 120 seats. If asked by the President to form a government, Netanyahu will become the first Prime Minister to serve a fourth term. 

After a bruising campaign focused on his failings, Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu seemed all but certain to form a new government, though he offended many in the process.
