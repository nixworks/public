# A BBC investigation finds that "Top Gear" presenter Jeremy Clarkson assaulted a producer with reports that he will be sacked from the television program. 

Jeremy Clarkson is to be sacked as Top Gear presenter after a BBC investigation found he did assault a producer in a row over steak and chips
