### [2015-03-23](/news/2015/03/23/index.md)

##### North Gauteng High Court
# The South Gauteng High Court sitting in Palm Ridge, Katlehong, finds former doubles tennis champion Bob Hewitt guilty of two counts of rape and one of sexual assault of three former students that he coached in South Africa. 

Former Grand Slam tennis champion Bob Hewitt, 75, has been found guilty of two counts of rape and one of indecent assault.
