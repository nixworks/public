##### Novodevichy Convent
# A fire breaks out in the bell tower of Moscow's Novodevichy Convent, established in 1524 and added to UNESCO's World Heritage Sites. 

Reports suggest renovation work on the heritage site, involving wooden scaffolding, was conducted in violation of safety regulations
