### [2015-02-3](/news/2015/02/3/index.md)

##### Iranian parliament
# The Iranian parliament passes a new bill that requires the Rouhani administration to cancel the Geneva interim agreement on the Iranian nuclear program in case of new US sanctions, as a response to a similar action by the Congress.
