##### Yeongjong Bridge
# A 106-car pileup occurs on Yeongjong Bridge in Incheon, South Korea, leaving two men dead and more than 65 people injured. 

A pile-up involving 100 cars on a bridge leading to Incheon airport in South Korea leaves at least two people dead.
