##### Harmony Gold
# All 486 trapped by an underground fire at the Harmony Gold Kusasalethu mine near Carletonville, South Africa have been rescued. 

Harmony Gold says rescue teams have been deployed to rescue some 486 workers after an underground fire started at its Kusasalethu mine.
