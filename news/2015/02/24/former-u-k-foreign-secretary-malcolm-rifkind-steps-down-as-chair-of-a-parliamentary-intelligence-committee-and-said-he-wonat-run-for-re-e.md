# Former U.K. Foreign Secretary Malcolm Rifkind steps down as chair of a parliamentary intelligence committee and said he won't run for re-election as a Conservative member of parliament after becoming embroiled in an embarrassing cash-for-access sting. 

Tory MP will resign from Commons at May election after quitting as head of parliamentary security committee over cash-for-access claims
