### [2015-12-9](/news/2015/12/9/index.md)

##### U.S.
# A group of mayors and officials from American cities such as Philadelphia and New York City say that if they had their way Donald Trump would be banned from their cities for his comments on Muslims. 

While city officials acknowledged that they can&#x27;t legally stop Trump from traveling into their communities, many joined the backlash against him.
