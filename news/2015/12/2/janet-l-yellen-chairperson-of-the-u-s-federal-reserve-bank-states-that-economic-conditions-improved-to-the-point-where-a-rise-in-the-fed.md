### [2015-12-2](/news/2015/12/2/index.md)

##### Janet L. Yellen
# Janet L. Yellen, chairperson of the U.S. Federal Reserve Bank, states that economic conditions improved to the point where a rise in the Fed's benchmark interest rate is likely when the Federal Open Market Committee meets in mid-December. 

The Federal Reserve chairwoman cited progress on goals of maximum employment and price stability.
