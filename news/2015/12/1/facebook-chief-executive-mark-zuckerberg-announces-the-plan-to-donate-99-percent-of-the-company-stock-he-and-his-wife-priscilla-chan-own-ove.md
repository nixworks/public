### [2015-12-1](/news/2015/12/1/index.md)

##### Facebook
# Facebook chief executive Mark Zuckerberg announces the plan to donate 99 percent of the company stock he and his wife Priscilla Chan own over their lifetimes, shares today worth about $45 billion, to "advance human potential and promote equality for all children." 

The billionaires join others who are giving massive amounts away to solve big problems.
