##### Northlake Mall
# One person is shot and killed by a policeman at the Northlake Mall in Charlotte, North Carolina. 

Charlotte-Mecklenburg Police spent all Thursday night interviewing witnesses after a deadly, officer-involved shooting on Christmas Eve inside the Northlake mall.
