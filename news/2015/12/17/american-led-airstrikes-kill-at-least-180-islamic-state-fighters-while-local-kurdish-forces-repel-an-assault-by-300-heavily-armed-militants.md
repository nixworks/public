##### U.S. airstrikes
#  American-led airstrikes kill at least 180 Islamic State fighters while local Kurdish forces repel an assault by 300 heavily armed militants in northern Iraq. 

At least 180 Islamist militants were killed in U.S. and allied airstrikes to support Kurdish forces.
