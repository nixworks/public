### [2015-12-4](/news/2015/12/4/index.md)

##### Obama administration
# The Obama Administration and the International Rescue Committee ask a federal judge to reject attempts by Texas to block Syrian refugees from resettling in the state. Later, the Texas Attorney General withdraws the state's December 2, 2015 request for a temporary restraining order.
