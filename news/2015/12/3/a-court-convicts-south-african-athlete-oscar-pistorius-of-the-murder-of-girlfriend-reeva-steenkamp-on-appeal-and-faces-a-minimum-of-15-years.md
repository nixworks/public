##### Trial of Oscar Pistorius
# A court convicts South African athlete Oscar Pistorius of the murder of girlfriend Reeva Steenkamp on appeal and faces a minimum of 15 years in prison. 

Justice Eric Leach said the sprinter &quot;never offered an acceptable explanation&quot; for fatally shooting girlfriend Reeva Steenkamp through a bathroom door.
