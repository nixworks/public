##### Israeli prosecutors
# A Palestinian man from eastern Jerusalem, Muhammad Abu Kaf, is indicted by Israeli prosecutors on eight counts of incitement to violence or terror for posting a video on Facebook calling on people to stab Jews. 

Muhuammad Abu Kaf of East Jerusalem was indicted on 8 counts of incitement to violence for posting a video calling for the stabbing of Jews on Facebook.
