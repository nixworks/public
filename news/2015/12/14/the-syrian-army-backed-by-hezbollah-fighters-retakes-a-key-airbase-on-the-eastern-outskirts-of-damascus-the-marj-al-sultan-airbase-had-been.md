##### Syrian Civil War
# The Syrian Army backed by Hezbollah fighters retakes a key airbase on the eastern outskirts of Damascus. The Marj al-Sultan airbase had been under rebel control since November 2012. 

Syrian government forces have regained control of a military airbase on the eastern outskirts of Damascus, activists and military sources say.
