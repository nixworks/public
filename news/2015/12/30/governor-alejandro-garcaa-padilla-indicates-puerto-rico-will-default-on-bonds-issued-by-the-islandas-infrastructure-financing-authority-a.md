### [2015-12-30](/news/2015/12/30/index.md)

##### Alejandro Garcia Padilla
# Governor Alejandro Garcia Padilla indicates Puerto Rico will default on bonds issued by the island's Infrastructure Financing Authority and Public Finance Corporation. 

The territory’s governor said the island’s total bond default was roughly $174 million million because he had “clawed back” money from a variety of bonds.
