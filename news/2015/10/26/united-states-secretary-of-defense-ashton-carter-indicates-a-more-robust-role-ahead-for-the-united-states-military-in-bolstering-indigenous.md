##### Syrian Civil War
# United States Secretary of Defense Ashton Carter indicates a more robust role ahead for the United States military in bolstering indigenous forces countering ISIS and in response to Russia's increasing role in Middle East conflicts. 

Defense Secretary Ash Carter said Friday the U.S. is likely to step up military raids in its campaign against Islamic State.
