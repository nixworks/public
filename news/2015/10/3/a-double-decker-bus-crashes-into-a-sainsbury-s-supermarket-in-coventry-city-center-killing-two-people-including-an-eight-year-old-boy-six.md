### [2015-10-3](/news/2015/10/3/index.md)

##### Sainsbury's
# A double-decker bus crashes into a Sainsbury's supermarket in Coventry city center killing two people, including an eight-year-old boy; six others are also seriously hurt, including the bus driver according to West Midlands Police. 

A woman and an eight-year-old boy have died after a double-decker bus crashed into a supermarket in Coventry city centre, police say.
