##### Stephen Glass
# Stephen Glass, author, former journalist and attorney, will repay Harper's Magazine and other outlets because works he authored were found to have been fabricated. 

Mr. Glass, a rising star in journalism in the 1990s, was revealed to be a serial fabricator and says he wants “to make right” part of his “many transgressions.”
