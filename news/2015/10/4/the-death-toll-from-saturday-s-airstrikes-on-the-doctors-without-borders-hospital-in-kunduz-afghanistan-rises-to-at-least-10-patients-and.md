##### War in Afghanistan (2015-present)
# The death toll from Saturday's airstrikes on the Doctors Without Borders' hospital in Kunduz, Afghanistan rises to at least 10 patients and 12 hospital staff in Doctors Without Borders, also known as Medecins Sans Frontieres (MSF). Doctors without Borders issues a statement expressing its "clear assumption that a war crime has been committed," and announces that all staff have left the hospital and the city. 

Doctors Without Borders staff have left the hospital in the city of Kunduz, Afghanistan, that was bombed in airstrikes blamed on the U.S.-led NATO coalition.
