##### Lennart Anderson
# Lennart Anderson died at age 87 in Brooklyn, New York. Also a teacher at several notable institutions such as Pratt Institute, the Art Students League of New York, Yale, Princeton, Columbia, and Brooklyn College, Anderson's influence was felt by generations of artists.

Mr. Anderson brought an understanding of painters like Degas to his still lifes, portraits, landscapes and streetscapes, while lifting them from the common run of academic realism.
