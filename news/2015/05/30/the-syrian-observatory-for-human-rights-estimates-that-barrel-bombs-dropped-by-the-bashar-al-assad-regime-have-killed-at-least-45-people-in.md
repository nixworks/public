# The Syrian Observatory for Human Rights estimates that barrel bombs dropped by the Bashar al-Assad regime have killed at least 45 people in the Aleppo Governorate and injured dozens more. 

A series of barrel bombs dropped by Syria&#39;s regime killed at least 45 civilians and wounded dozens in Aleppo province Saturday, said the Syrian Observatory for Human Rights.
