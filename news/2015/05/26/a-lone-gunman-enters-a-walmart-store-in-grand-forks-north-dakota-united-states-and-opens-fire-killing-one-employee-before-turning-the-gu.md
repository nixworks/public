##### Walmart
# A lone gunman enters a Walmart store in Grand Forks, North Dakota, United States, and opens fire, killing one employee before turning the gun on himself. 

The shooting occurred about 1 a.m. Tuesday inside the store. Police said there is no ongoing public safety risk.
