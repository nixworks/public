# 2015 Valenzuela fire: At least 72 people die after a fire engulfs a rubber sandal factory in Valenzuela City, a northern suburb of Manila, Philippines. 

Government launches investigation into cause of Manila blaze which killed at least 72 and left dozens of others trapped.
