# The President of the United States Barack Obama nominates Joseph F. Dunford, Jr., the Commandant of the United States Marine Corps as the next Chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff. 

In nominating Joseph F. Dunford Jr. of the Marines, the president picked a veteran combat commander to help him wind down one war and figure out how to win another.
