# North Korea abruptly cancels an invitation for Ban Ki-moon, the Secretary-General of the United Nations, to visit. 

North Korea has cancelled an invitation to Ban Ki-moon to visit an industrial zone inside the isolated state, in an abrupt move the UN Secretary-General deplored as &#034;deeply regrettable&#034;.
