# 2015 Baltimore riots: The Governor of Maryland, Larry Hogan, rescinds a state of emergency imposed following the riots prompted by the death of Freddie Gray. 

Gov. Larry Hogan on Wednesday officially lifted the state of emergency he put on Baltimore 10 days ago.
