### [2015-11-15](/news/2015/11/15/index.md)

##### 2015 Beirut bombings
# Lebanon arrests nine people including seven Syrians over the recent Beirut bombings that killed at least 44 people. 

Seven Syrians and two Lebanese detained for allegedly planning deadly explosions and smuggling attackers into country.
