### [2015-11-27](/news/2015/11/27/index.md)

##### China
# China's Shanghai index closed down 5.5%, a drop of almost 200 points in what was its largest single-day decline in three months. 

Investors appeared to be cashing out to invest in new initial public offerings, and they were reacting to news that two brokerage firms were being investigated.
