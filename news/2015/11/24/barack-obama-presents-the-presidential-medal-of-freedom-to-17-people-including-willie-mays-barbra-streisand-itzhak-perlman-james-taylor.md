# Barack Obama presents the Presidential Medal of Freedom to 17 people including Willie Mays, Barbra Streisand, Itzhak Perlman, James Taylor, Gloria Estefan and Emilio Estefan, Stephen Sondheim and Steven Spielberg. 

Among those honored were such iconic figures as Willie Mays, Barbra Streisand, Itzhak Perlman and Steven Spielberg.
