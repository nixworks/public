##### Israeli-Palestinian conflict
# Israeli agents, disguised as Arabs escorting a woman in labor, raids a hospital in Hebron, West Bank, seizing a Palestinian suspected of stabbing and shooting dead his cousin. 

Video emerged showing Israeli forces disguised in traditional Arab outfits as they burst in to make an arrest. One man was killed.
