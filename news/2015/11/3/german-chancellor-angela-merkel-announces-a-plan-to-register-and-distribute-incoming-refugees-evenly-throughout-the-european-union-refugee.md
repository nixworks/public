### [2015-11-3](/news/2015/11/3/index.md)

##### Refugee immigration to Germany
#  German Chancellor Angela Merkel announces a plan to register and distribute incoming refugees evenly throughout the European Union. Refugees would be stopped at EU borders, have their application processed, and then, if accepted, sent to one of the Union's 28 member states. 

German Chancellor Angela Merkel announced a plan to register and distribute incoming refugees evenly throughout the European Union.
