##### Yemeni Civil War (2015)
# Two attacks on security checkpoints by ISIS militants in Yemen's historic Shibam town and Al Qatn District in the central Hadhramaut Governorate leave at least 15 pro-Hadi government soldiers dead. 

A number of soldiers and civilians were also injured in the twin attacks in Hadramawt province, large areas of which have been under the control of Al Qaeda since earlier this year.
