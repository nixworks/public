# The family of Swedish diplomat Raoul Wallenberg, who saved tens of thousands of Jews from the Holocaust only to languish in a Russian prison, have asked authorities to officially declare him dead. 

Swedish diplomat who saved thousands of Jews during the Holocaust disappeared in 1945 after being detained by Soviet officers
