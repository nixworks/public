#  Officials report three people died in the Saint-Denis raid. The body of a second woman is found at the apartment where a woman and Paris attacks ringleader Abdelhamid Abaaoud were killed. 

Prosecutors confirm the cousin of the Paris attacks plotter was killed in the raid as a second woman&#039;s body is found at the scene.
