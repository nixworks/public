# Abu Anas al-Libi, a one-time associate of Osama bin Laden, dies in New York, United States, while awaiting trial for allegedly plotting the bombings of the U.S. embassies in Kenya and Tanzania. 

Terror suspect Abu Anas al-Libi has died in New York while awaiting trial for allegedly plotting the 1998 bombings of the U.S. embassies.
