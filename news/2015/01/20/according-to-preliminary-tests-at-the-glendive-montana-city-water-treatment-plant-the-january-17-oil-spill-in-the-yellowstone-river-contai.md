### [2015-01-20](/news/2015/01/20/index.md)

##### Glendive, Montana
# According to preliminary tests at the Glendive, Montana city water treatment plant, the January 17 oil spill in the Yellowstone River contains benzene, a chemical that causes cancer. 

The latest spill may have polluted drinking water in Montana but is not expected to affect Yellowstone National Park.
