# Schools in the Midwestern and Northeast United States close for a second successive day due to bitterly cold temperatures. 

By Mary Wisniewski CHICAGO (Reuters) - Schools in large cities across the Midwest and into the Northeast announced they will close on Thursday to protect children from bitterly cold temperatures as wind chill warnings were issued for most of the eastern half of the United States.
