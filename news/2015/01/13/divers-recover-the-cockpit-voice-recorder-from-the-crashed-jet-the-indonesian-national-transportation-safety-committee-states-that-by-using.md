### [2015-01-13](/news/2015/01/13/index.md)

# Divers recover the cockpit voice recorder from the crashed jet. The Indonesian National Transportation Safety Committee states that by using the now collected recorders a preliminary report on the accident will be produced within a month and a final report after a year. 

Indonesian Navy confirms the cockpit voice recorder from the AirAsia plane that crashed last month has been recovered.
