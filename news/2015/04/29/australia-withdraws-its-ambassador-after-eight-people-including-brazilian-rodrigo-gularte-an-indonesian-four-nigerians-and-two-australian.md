##### Australia-Indonesia relations
# Australia withdraws its ambassador after eight people including Brazilian Rodrigo Gularte, an Indonesian, four Nigerians, and two Australians, Andrew Chan and Myuran Sukumaran are executed. 

France and Australia have condemned the death penalty as executions for three of their nationals looms in Indonesia.
