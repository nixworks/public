### [2015-04-29](/news/2015/04/29/index.md)

##### World Health Organization
# The Americas becomes the first World Health Organization region to be officially declared free of rubella. 

The disease, also known as German measles, is no longer a threat in the region, a scientific panel set up by global health authorities announced on Wednesday.
