##### 2015 Libya migrant shipwreck
# Up to 400 people may have died as a boat capsizes in the Mediterranean Sea between Libya and Italy with up to 550 passengers on board. 

Around 400 migrants died when their boat capsized during an attempt to reach Italy from Libya.
