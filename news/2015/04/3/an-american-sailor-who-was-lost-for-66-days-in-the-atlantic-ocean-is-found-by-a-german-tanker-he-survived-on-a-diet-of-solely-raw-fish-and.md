#  An American sailor who was lost for 66 days in the Atlantic Ocean is found by a German tanker. He survived on a diet of solely raw fish and rainwater. 

A rescued sailor who was adrift in the Atlantic Ocean for two months says he survived on rainwater he collected and fish he caught.
