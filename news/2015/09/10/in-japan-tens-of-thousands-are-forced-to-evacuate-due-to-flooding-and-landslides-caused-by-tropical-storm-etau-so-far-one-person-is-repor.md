### [2015-09-10](/news/2015/09/10/index.md)

##### 2015 Pacific typhoon season
# In Japan, tens of thousands are forced to evacuate due to flooding and landslides caused by Tropical Storm Etau. So far, one person is reported as missing. 

Authorities in central Japan have ordered tens of thousands to flee their homes after rain flooded rivers and triggered landslides.
