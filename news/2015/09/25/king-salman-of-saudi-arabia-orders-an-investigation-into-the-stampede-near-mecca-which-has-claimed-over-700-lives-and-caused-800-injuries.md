##### 2015 Hajj stampede
# King Salman of Saudi Arabia orders an investigation into the stampede near Mecca which has claimed over 700 lives and caused 800 injuries. 

Saudi Arabia&#039;s King orders an investigation into a stampede at the annual Hajj pilgrimage that killed at least 717 people and left 800 others injured.
