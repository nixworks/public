### [2015-09-25](/news/2015/09/25/index.md)

##### John Boehner
# John Boehner schedules his resignation from the position of Speaker of the United States House of Representatives and from Congress for the end of October. 

The move reduced the chance of a government shutdown, because Mr. Boehner is now free to push for a short-term funding measure.
