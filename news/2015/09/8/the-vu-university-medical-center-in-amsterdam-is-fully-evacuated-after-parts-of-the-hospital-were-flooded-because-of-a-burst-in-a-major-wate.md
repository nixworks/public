### [2015-09-8](/news/2015/09/8/index.md)

##### VU University Medical Center
# The VU University Medical Center in Amsterdam is fully evacuated after parts of the hospital were flooded because of a burst in a major water-pipeline. 

A burst water main in the south of Amsterdam caused chaos on Tuesday morning, RTL news reported.
