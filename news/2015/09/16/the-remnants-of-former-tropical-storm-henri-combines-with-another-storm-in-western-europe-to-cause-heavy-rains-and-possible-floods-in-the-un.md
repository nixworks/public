##### Tropical cyclone effects in Europe
# The remnants of former Tropical Storm Henri combines with another storm in western Europe to cause heavy rains and possible floods in the United Kingdom, France, Spain and Portugal. 

Once-Tropical Storm Henri will contribute to bring flooding rain and damaging winds to western Europe through Wednesday.
