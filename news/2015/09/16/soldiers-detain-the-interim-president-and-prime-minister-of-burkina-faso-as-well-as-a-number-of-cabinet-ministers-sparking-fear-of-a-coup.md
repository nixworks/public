### [2015-09-16](/news/2015/09/16/index.md)

##### 2015 Burkinabe coup d'etat
# Soldiers detain the interim president and prime minister of Burkina Faso, as well as a number of cabinet ministers, sparking fear of a coup d'etat. A joint statement by the United Nations, the African Union, and the Economic Community of West African States states that the presidential guard should immediately release the leaders of the interim government. 

Detention of president, prime minister and cabinet members raises fears of coup before elections.
