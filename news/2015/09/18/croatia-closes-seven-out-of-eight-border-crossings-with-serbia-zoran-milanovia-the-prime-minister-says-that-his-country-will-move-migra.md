### [2015-09-18](/news/2015/09/18/index.md)

##### Refugee immigration to Germany
# Croatia closes seven out of eight border crossings with Serbia. Zoran Milanovic, the Prime Minister, says that his country "will move migrants on". 

Croatia closes seven of its eight border crossings with Serbia as thousands of desperate migrants seek alternative routes to Germany and other EU states.
