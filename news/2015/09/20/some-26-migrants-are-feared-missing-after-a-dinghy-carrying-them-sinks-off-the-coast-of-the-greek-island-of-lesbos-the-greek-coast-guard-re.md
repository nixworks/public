##### Refugee immigration to Germany
# Some 26 migrants are feared missing after a dinghy carrying them sinks off the coast of the Greek island of Lesbos. The Greek Coast Guard rescued 20 people who were spotted in the water by Frontex, EU's border agency. 

The Italian coastguard has said nearly 4,700 migrants were rescued from boats in the Mediterranean yesterday, releasing video of the rescue operations at sea.
