##### Libya
# A prominent Libyan Zuwarah militia leader and migrant smuggler, Salah Al-Maskhout, is reportedly killed in Tripoli by Italian special forces. The EU recently approved military action against people smuggling networks operating out of Libya. 

Libyan militia leader and alleged human smuggler reportedly killed by Italian special forces but relatives claim he is still alive and has nothing to do with smuggling
