# The death toll from flooding in Japan caused by Tropical Storm Etau rises to seven. 

TOKYO (AFP) - The number of people who died in floods which devastated Japan this week reached five on Sunday (Sept 13) as rescuers continued their search for more than a dozen still missing, local media said.. Read more at straitstimes.com.
