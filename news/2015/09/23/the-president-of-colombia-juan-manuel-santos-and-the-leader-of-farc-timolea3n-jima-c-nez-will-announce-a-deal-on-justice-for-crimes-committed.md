##### Colombian conflict (1964-present)
# The President of Colombia Juan Manuel Santos and the leader of FARC Timoleon Jimenez will announce a deal on justice for crimes committed in the fifty-year-long conflict. 

Colombian President Juan Manuel Santos and Farc rebel leader Timochenko agree to sign a peace deal within six months to end a five-decade long conflict.
