### [2015-09-28](/news/2015/09/28/index.md)

##### Saudi Arabian-led intervention in Yemen
# An Arab coalition air-strike on a wedding party in Wahijah, a village near the Red Sea port city of Mocha in southern Yemen, reportedly leaves as many as 131 people dead, making it the deadliest coalition attack on Yemeni civilians in the conflict so far. The UN general secretary condemns the attack. 

At least 38 people are reported to have been killed in an air strike on a wedding party in south-western Yemen.
