# Thousands of people in the Chilean town of Illapel sleep outside following yesterday's 8.3 magnitude earthquake as the death toll rises to 11. 

At least 10 dead from powerful temblor that cuts off power to a quarter-million homes; tsunami advisories for southern California, Hawaii
