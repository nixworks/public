### [2015-09-17](/news/2015/09/17/index.md)

##### American Airlines
# American Airlines halts flights for 90 minutes at its major hubs in Chicago, Dallas, and Miami because of a computer glitch. The incident produces a cascading effect of delays throughout all US airlines. 

Flights on American Airlines were halted at three major U.S. airports Thursday due to a computer glitch that created connectivity problems, officials said.
