### [2015-07-6](/news/2015/07/6/index.md)

##### Ban Ki-Moon
# Ban Ki-Moon, the Secretary-General of the United Nations, calls for a new investigation into the death of former Secretary-General Dag Hammarskjold in a plane crash after a panel he appointed uncovers new evidence. 

A panel appointed by Secretary General Ban Ki-moon reported new information suggesting that an attack had brought down the flight over southern Africa.
