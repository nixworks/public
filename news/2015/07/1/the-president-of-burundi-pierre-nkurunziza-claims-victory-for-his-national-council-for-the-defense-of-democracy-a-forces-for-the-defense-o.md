##### Burundian legislative election, 2015
# The President of Burundi Pierre Nkurunziza claims victory for his National Council for the Defense of Democracy - Forces for the Defense of Democracy coalition despite the fact that opposition parties did not contest the election. 

Pierre Nkurunziza claims an early victory for his ruling CNDD-FDD party, despite the opposition of domestic civic groups, AU and the UN.
