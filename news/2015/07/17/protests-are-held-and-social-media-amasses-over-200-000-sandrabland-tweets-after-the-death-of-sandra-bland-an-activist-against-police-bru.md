### [2015-07-17](/news/2015/07/17/index.md)

##### Death of Sandra Bland
# Protests are held, and social media amasses over 200,000 #SandraBland tweets after the Death of Sandra Bland, an activist against police brutality, while she is imprisoned in Waller County, Texas. 

Days after a Chicago-area woman, Sandra Bland, was found dead in a Texas jail cell and officials ruled suicide, her friends and family say they want a thorough investigation.
