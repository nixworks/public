### [2015-07-24](/news/2015/07/24/index.md)

##### Zhou Benshun
# Zhou Benshun, the Communist Party chief of China's Hebei province, is detained for investigation by the party's anti-graft body. He is first incumbent provincial leader to be investigated since the anti-corruption campaign began in 2013. 

Zhou Benshun, the head of a major Chinese province and a former aide to disgraced official Zhou Yongkang, has been placed under investigation.
