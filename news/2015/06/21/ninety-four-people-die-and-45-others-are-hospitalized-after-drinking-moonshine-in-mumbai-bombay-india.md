# Ninety-four people die, and 45 others are hospitalized, after drinking moonshine in Mumbai (Bombay), India. 

Toxic homemade liquor has killed nearly 100 slum dwellers in India&#39;s financial capital Mumbai, with more than 45 in hospital, police say.
