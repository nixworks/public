# Kuwait declares a day of mourning for the victims of the bombing of a Shia mosque yesterday. 

Kuwait has declared an &#034;all-out confrontation&#034; with terrorism after an Islamic State suicide bomber struck a Shi&#039;ite mosque in the Gulf state&#039;s capital during Friday prayers, killing 27 people.
