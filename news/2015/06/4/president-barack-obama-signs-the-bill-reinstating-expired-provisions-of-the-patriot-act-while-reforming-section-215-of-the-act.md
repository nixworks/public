### [2015-06-4](/news/2015/06/4/index.md)

##### USA Freedom Act
#  President Barack Obama signs the bill, reinstating expired provisions of the Patriot Act while reforming Section 215 of the Act. 

The bill moves the storage of bulk telephone metadata used by the NSA to telecom companies.
