##### Syrian Civil War
# The Southern Front of the Free Syrian Army claims to have captured a major Syrian Army base known as Brigade 52 in Daraa Governorate. 

Rebels in Syria say they have captured a major army base in the south of the country, the latest in a string of defeats suffered by the government.
