##### Hong Kong electoral reform
# The Legislative Council of Hong Kong rejects a Beijing-backed electoral reform which sparked the Occupy Central with Love and Peace movement. 

Hong Kong’s legislature rejected a Beijing-backed election-reform plan, dealing a setback to the former British colony’s relationship with mainland China.
