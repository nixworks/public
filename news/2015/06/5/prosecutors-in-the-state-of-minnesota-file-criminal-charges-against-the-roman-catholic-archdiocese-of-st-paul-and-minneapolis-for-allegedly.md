# Prosecutors in the state of Minnesota file criminal charges against the Roman Catholic Archdiocese of St. Paul and Minneapolis for allegedly mishandling sexual abuse allegations by a priest. 

A criminal case accuses Catholic leaders of St. Paul and Minneapolis of misdemeanors in the handling of complaints about a priest who is now in prison.
