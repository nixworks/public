### [2015-08-31](/news/2015/08/31/index.md)

##### Denali
# Denali, the highest mountain peak in North America, officially regains its original Alaska Native name through an executive order issued by American President Barack Obama. Previously, since 1917, it had been known as Mount McKinley.
