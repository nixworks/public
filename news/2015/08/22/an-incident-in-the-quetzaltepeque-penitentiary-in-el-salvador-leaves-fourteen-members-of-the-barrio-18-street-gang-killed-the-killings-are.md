# An incident in the Quetzaltepeque penitentiary in El Salvador leaves fourteen members of the Barrio 18 street gang killed; the killings are believed to be a result of a power struggle within the gang. 

Fourteen members of one of El Salvador&#x27;s most powerful street gangs were killed in a prison on Saturday in what appeared to be an internal gang purge, authorities said.
