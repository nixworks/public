##### South Sudanese Civil War
# Fighting resumes in South Sudan after the failure to reach a deal between tribes loyal to the president and tribes loyal to the former vice-president. 

A military spokesman says fighting has resumed between South Sudanese troops and rebels two days after the president declined to sign a peace deal.
