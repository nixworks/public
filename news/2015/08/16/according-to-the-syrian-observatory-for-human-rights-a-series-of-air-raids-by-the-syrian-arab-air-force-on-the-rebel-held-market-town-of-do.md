### [2015-08-16](/news/2015/08/16/index.md)

##### 2015 Douma market air strikes
# According to the Syrian Observatory for Human Rights, a series of air-raids by the Syrian Arab Air Force on the rebel-held market town of Douma kills at least 110 people and leaves hundreds injured. 

At least 80 people have died after government air strikes on a marketplace in the rebel-held town of Douma, near Damascus, activists say.
