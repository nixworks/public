##### North Korea
# North Korea will change its time zone to UTC+08:30 on August 15, returning to the time used by the Korean Empire until Japanese occupation in 1910. 

North Korea will put its standard time back by half an hour on 15 August to break from the time zone imposed by &quot;wicked&quot; imperialist Japan.
