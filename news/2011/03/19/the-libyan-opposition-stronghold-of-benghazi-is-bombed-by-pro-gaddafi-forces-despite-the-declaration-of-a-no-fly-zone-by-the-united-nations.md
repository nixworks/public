##### Libya
# The Libyan opposition stronghold of Benghazi is bombed by pro-Gaddafi forces despite the declaration of a no-fly zone by the United Nations. 

A French plane has fired the first shots on a military vehicle in Libya, as the UK and US also join enforcement of the UN-mandated no-fly zone.
