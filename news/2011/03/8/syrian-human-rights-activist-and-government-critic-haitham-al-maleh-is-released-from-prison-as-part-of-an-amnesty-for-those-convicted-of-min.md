### [2011-03-8](/news/2011/03/8/index.md)

##### Syria
# Syrian human rights activist and government critic Haitham al-Maleh is released from prison as part of an amnesty for those convicted of minor crimes and those aged over 70. 

The prominent Syrian human rights activist and critic of the government Haitham al-Maleh was been released from jail.
