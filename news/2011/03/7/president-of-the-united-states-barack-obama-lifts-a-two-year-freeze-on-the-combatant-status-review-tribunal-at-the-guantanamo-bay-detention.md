##### U.S. President
# President of the United States Barack Obama lifts a two year freeze on the Combatant Status Review Tribunal at the Guantanamo Bay detention camp. 

US President Barack Obama is lifting the two-year freeze on new military trials for detainees at the Guantanamo Bay prison.
