# A U.S. federal district court in Delaware rules in favor of Cephalon, a phamaceutical company, in a patent infringement lawsuit it had brought against a manufacturer of generic drugs. 

A U.S. Court upheld aCephalon Inc CEPH.O patent protecting its Fentora painmedicine in a dispute with generic drugmaker WatsonPharmaceuticals Inc WPI.N, Cephalon said on Thursday.
