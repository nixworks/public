### [2011-03-24](/news/2011/03/24/index.md)

##### Tasmania
# Thousands of people are stranded by flash flooding in Tasmania, amid record falls of rain. Roads are closed, blackouts occur and people are flown away in helicopters. 

Thousands of people in eastern Tasmania have been stranded by flash flooding, following record rainfalls in the past 24 hours.
