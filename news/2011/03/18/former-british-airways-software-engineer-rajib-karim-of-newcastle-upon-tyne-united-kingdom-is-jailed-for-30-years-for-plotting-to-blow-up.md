# Former British Airways software engineer Rajib Karim, of Newcastle upon Tyne, United Kingdom is jailed for 30 years for plotting to blow up a plane. 

A British Airways computer expert is jailed for 30 years for plotting to blow up a plane after contacting an al-Qaeda preacher based in Yemen.
