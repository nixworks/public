##### Ukraine
# After a gag order is lifted and Sunday publication is permitted, Israel's security service admits it has arrested Gazan engineer Dirar Abu Sisi who disappeared in Ukraine almost two weeks ago and whose wife thought he had been abducted by Mossad. 

State says 42-year old Palestinian engineer abducted in Ukraine being interrogated in prison
