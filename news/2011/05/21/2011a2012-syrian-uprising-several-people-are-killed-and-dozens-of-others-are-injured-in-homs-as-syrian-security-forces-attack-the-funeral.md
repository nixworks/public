# 2011-2012 Syrian uprising:Several people are killed and dozens of others are injured in Homs as Syrian security forces attack the funeral of protesters killed in yesterday's protest events during the country's uprising against the regime. 

At least 11 dead and 27 injured in Homs, activists say, as thousands attend funerals for those killed on Friday.
