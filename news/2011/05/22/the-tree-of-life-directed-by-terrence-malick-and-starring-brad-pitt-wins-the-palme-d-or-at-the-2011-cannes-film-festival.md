##### The Tree of Life
# "The Tree of Life", directed by Terrence Malick and starring Brad Pitt, wins the Palme d'Or at the 2011 Cannes Film Festival. 

Two directors who made headlines at the Cannes Film Festival for their conduct off screen saw their movies win top awards last night.
