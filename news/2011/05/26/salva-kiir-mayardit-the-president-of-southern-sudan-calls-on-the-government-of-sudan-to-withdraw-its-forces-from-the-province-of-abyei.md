### [2011-05-26](/news/2011/05/26/index.md)

##### Salva Kiir Mayardit
# Salva Kiir Mayardit, the President of Southern Sudan, calls on the government of Sudan to withdraw its forces from the province of Abyei. 

Salva Kiir calls on Khartoum to withdraw its troops from the oil-rich region to avoid further violence.
