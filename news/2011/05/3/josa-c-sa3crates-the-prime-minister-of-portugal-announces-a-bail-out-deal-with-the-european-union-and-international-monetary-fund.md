### [2011-05-3](/news/2011/05/3/index.md)

# Jose Socrates, the Prime Minister of Portugal, announces a bail out deal with the European Union and International Monetary Fund. 

Portugal has reached a deal on a bail-out from the EU and IMF, its caretaker prime minister announces.
