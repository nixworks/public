# Ali Abdullah Saleh refuses to sign a Gulf Arab states-brokered agreement intended to resolve the situation, and the deal has collapsed. The opposition in Yemen promises to escalate the protests. 

A deal to end the unrest in Yemen appears to be unravelling after President Ali Abdullah Saleh refuses to turn up to sign it.
