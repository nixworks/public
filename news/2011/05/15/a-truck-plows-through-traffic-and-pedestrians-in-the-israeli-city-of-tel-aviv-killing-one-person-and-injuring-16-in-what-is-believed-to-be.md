##### Israel
# A truck plows through traffic and pedestrians in the Israeli city of Tel Aviv, killing one person and injuring 16 in what is believed to be a terrorist attack, as Palestinians mark Nakba Day.

Police spokesman: Truck rampage &quot;looks deliberate,&quot; but probe ongoing; driver, a Kfar Qasm resident, says he hit cars by accident.
