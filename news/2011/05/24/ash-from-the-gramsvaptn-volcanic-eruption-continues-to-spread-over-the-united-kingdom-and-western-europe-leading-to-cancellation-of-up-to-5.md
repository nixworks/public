##### Grimsvotn
# Ash from the Grimsvotn volcanic eruption continues to spread over the United Kingdom and Western Europe leading to cancellation of up to 500 airline flights. 

Airlines canceled about 500 European flights as the ash cloud from the Grimsvotn volcano in Iceland drifted into northern Britain and Ireland.
