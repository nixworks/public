### [2011-05-7](/news/2011/05/7/index.md)

# Sources claim that the Syrian Army storms into the city of Baniyas attacking Sunni districts that have opposed President Bashar al-Assad during the 2011 Syrian protests. 

Residents form a human chain in a bid to halt troops from sweeping into the coastal town of Baniyas.
