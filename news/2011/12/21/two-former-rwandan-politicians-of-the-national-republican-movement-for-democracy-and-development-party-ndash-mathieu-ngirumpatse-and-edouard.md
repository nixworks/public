### [2011-12-21](/news/2011/12/21/index.md)

##### Rwanda
# Two former Rwandan politicians of the National Republican Movement for Democracy and Development party&ndash;Mathieu Ngirumpatse and douard Karemera&ndash;are jailed for their role in the 1994 Rwandan Genocide. 

Two men, who headed MRND party when up to 800,000 were massacred by Hutu extremists in Rwanda, sentenced for life.
