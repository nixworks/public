### [2011-12-8](/news/2011/12/8/index.md)

##### A storm
# A storm causes damage, flooding and power cuts across the north of the British Isles, particularly Scotland with winds up to 165&nbsp;mph (264&nbsp;km-h). 

Police tell people in Scotland not to travel, as severe winds of up to 165mph hit the country, leaving more than 50,000 people without power.
