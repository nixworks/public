# The President of Nigeria Goodluck Jonathan declares a state of emergency in the states of Borno, Yobe, Niger and Plateau due to violence by the Islamist sect Boko Haram. 

Nigeria has declared a state of emergency and closed parts of the country&#039;s borders in areas hit hard by violence blamed on an Islamist sect.
