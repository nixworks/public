##### David Cameron
# David Cameron, the Prime Minister of the United Kingdom, refuses to join a European Union financial crisis accord after negotiations in Brussels. 

The PM effectively vetoes an EU-wide treaty change, after making what French President Nicolas Sarkozy called &quot;unacceptable&quot; demands.
