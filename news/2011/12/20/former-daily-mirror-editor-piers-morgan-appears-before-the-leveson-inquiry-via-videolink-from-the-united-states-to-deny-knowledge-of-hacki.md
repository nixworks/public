# Former "Daily Mirror" editor Piers Morgan appears before the Leveson Inquiry via videolink from the United States to deny knowledge of hacking at the paper. 

Former Daily Mirror editor Piers Morgan tells the Leveson Inquiry he does not believe phone hacking took place at the paper while he was in charge.
