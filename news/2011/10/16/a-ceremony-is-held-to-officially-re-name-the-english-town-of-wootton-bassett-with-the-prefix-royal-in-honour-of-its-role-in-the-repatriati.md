##### English
# A ceremony is held to officially re-name the English town of Wootton Bassett with the prefix "Royal" in honour of its role in the repatriation of British military personnel killed in action in Iraq and Afghanistan. It is the first town to receive such an honour since 1909. 

A ceremony to rename Wootton Bassett with the prefix &quot;Royal&quot; takes place in the Wiltshire town.
