### [2011-10-19](/news/2011/10/19/index.md)

##### Hellenic Parliament
# The Hellenic Parliament gives approval in principle to further austerity measures, as street protests in Athens turn violent amid Greece's worsening economic crisis. 

One man dies after fighting erupts between protesters in Athens, as the Greek parliament passes its latest round of austerity measures.
