# Sir Hugh Orde, President of the UK's Association of Chief Police Officers, announces that key policing services are being left "unfunded" as a result of cuts. 

Key policing services are being left &quot;unfunded&quot; by budget cuts, the president of the Association of Chief Police Officers, Sir Hugh Orde, has said.
