##### Uzbekistan
# A 6.1 magnitude earthquake occurs near the Uzbekistan city of Fergana killing 13 people in Uzbekistan and one person in Tajikistan. 

A powerful earthquake killed at least 14 people in a heavily populated valley in Central Asia early Wednesday, emergency officials said.
