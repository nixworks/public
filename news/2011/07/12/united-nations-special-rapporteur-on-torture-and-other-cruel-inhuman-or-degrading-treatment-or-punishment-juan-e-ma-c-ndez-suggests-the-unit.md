### [2011-07-12](/news/2011/07/12/index.md)

##### United Nations
# United Nations special rapporteur on torture and other cruel, inhuman or degrading treatment or punishment Juan E. Mendez suggests the United States is violating UN rules by refusing unmonitored access to imprisoned United States Army private Bradley Manning, the young serviceman the Obama administration accuses of passing classified information on secret U.S. activities to the WikiLeaks whistleblower website. 

The UN torture investigator accuses the US of violating long-established rules by refusing unmonitored access to detained Wikileaks suspect Bradley Manning.
