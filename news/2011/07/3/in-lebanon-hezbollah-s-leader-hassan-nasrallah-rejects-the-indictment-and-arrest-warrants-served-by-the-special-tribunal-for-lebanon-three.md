### [2011-07-3](/news/2011/07/3/index.md)

##### Lebanon
# In Lebanon, Hezbollah's leader Hassan Nasrallah rejects the indictment and arrest warrants served by the Special Tribunal for Lebanon three days earlier against the party's four senior members. 

Hezbollah leader says UN tribunal investigating death of former Lebanese PM should be asking questions of Israel.
