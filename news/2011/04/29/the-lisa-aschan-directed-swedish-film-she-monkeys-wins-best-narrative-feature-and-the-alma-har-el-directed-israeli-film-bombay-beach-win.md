# The Lisa Aschan-directed Swedish film "She Monkeys" wins best narrative feature and the Alma Har'el-directed Israeli film "Bombay Beach" wins best documentary prize at New York's Tribeca Film Festival. 

A Swedish film about two teenage girls whose friendship turns to rivalry wins a top award at the Tribeca film festival in New York.
