# At least 15 people are killed and at least 20 others are injured after a bomb tears through the Argana cafe in Marrakesh's main Djemaa el-Fna square. 

A bomb attack in the main square in the Moroccan city of Marrakesh kills 15 people and injures 20, with foreigners among the casualties.
