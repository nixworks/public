# Five months after his expulsion from the Shas party, Israeli rabbi Chaim Amsellem forms the Whole Nation party, and announces plans to run for the 19th Knesset on a secular-religious unity platform. 

Rabbi Chaim Amsellem announces intentions to run for 19th Knesset with Am Shalem, his new party which aims to bring secular, religious closer together
