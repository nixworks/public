##### African Union
# An African Union mission arrives in Libya to negotiate a settlement in the 2011 Libyan civil war between Muammar Gaddafi and his opponents. 

Libya accepts an African Union peace proposal, which will now be put to rebel leaders in Benghazi, says South African President Jacob Zuma.
