### [2011-04-17](/news/2011/04/17/index.md)

##### Secretary of State
# The United States Secretary of State Hillary Clinton visits Japan to announce US assistance for the recovery effort following the 2011 Tohoku earthquake and tsunami. 

Hillary Clinton announces joint reconstruction plans as she visits Japan to show US solidarity five weeks after the quake disaster.
