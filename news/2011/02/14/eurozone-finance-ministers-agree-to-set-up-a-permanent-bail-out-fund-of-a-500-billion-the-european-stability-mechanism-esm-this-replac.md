### [2011-02-14](/news/2011/02/14/index.md)

# Eurozone finance ministers agree to set up a permanent bail-out fund of EUR500 billion - the European Stability Mechanism (ESM). This replaces the European Financial Stability Facility (EFSF). 

Eurozone leaders agree to set up a new bail-out fund of 500bn euros - almost double the current amount that can be lent.
