##### Allison Transmission
# Allison Transmission, a maker of transmissions for trucks and buses, is planning an initial public offering expected to raise between US$500 million and $1 billion, according to Reuters, which cites "four sources familiar with the situation." 

Private equity-backed Allison Transmission has hired banks and is planning an initial public offering that could raise around $1 billion in the third quarter of the year, four sources familiar with the situation said.
