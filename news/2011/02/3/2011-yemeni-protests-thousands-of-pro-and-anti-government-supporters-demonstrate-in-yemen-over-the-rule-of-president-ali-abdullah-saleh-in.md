# 2011 Yemeni protests: Thousands of pro- and anti-government supporters demonstrate in Yemen over the rule of President Ali Abdullah Saleh in a planned "day of rage". 

Anti-government demonstrators reiterate calls for president to stand down while his supporters stage a counter rally.
