##### The Guardian
# "The Guardian" reports that Raymond Davis, charged with murder in Pakistan after shooting dead two men in Lahore, worked for the U.S. Central Intelligence Agency (CIA). 

An American official held in Pakistan after shooting dead two men is a CIA agent who was on assignment at the time of the incident, according to reports.
