# The British Ministry of Defence suspends training flights in aircraft with ejector seats similar to that which killed a Red Arrows pilot on Tuesday. 

The MoD suspends training flights in aircraft with ejector seats like the one in which Red Arrows pilot Flt Lt Sean Cunningham died.
