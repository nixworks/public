# 2011 Syrian uprising: 250 Syrians, mostly civilians, have been killed so far this month, as the violence escalates in Syria's eight-month-old crackdown on pro-democracy protests and attacks on security forces increase. 

Activists say more than 250 Syrians have been killed so far this month as the political unrest escalates dramatically.
