# A Congressional panel fails to agree on proposals to cut the United States' national budget deficit by $1.2 trillion, leading to automatic budget cuts. 

U.S. president Barak Obama promised Monday to veto any efforts to get rid of automatic spending cuts that are to follow the failure of a congressional &quot;supercommittee&quot; to agree on how to cut the budget deficit by $1.2 trillion US.
