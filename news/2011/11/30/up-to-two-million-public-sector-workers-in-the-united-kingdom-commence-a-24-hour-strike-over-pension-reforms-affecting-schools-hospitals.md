##### United Kingdom
# Up to two million public sector workers in the United Kingdom commence a 24-hour strike over pension reforms, affecting schools, hospitals, airports, ports and government offices nationwide. 

Tens of thousands attended rallies around the UK as a public sector strike over pensions disrupted schools, hospitals and other services.
