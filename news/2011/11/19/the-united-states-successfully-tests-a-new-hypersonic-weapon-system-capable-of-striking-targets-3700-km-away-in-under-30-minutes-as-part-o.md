### [2011-11-19](/news/2011/11/19/index.md)

##### U.S.
# The United States successfully tests a new hypersonic weapon system, capable of striking targets 3700 km away in under 30 minutes, as part of its Prompt Global Strike program. 

The US test-fires a weapon that can travel at five times the speed of sound, as part of plans to develop a new long-range rapid strike force.
