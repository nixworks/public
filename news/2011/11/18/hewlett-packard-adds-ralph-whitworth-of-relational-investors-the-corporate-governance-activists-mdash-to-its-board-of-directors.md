# Hewlett-Packard adds Ralph Whitworth of Relational Investors, the corporate-governance activists&mdash;to its board of directors. 

Hewlett-Packard Co., the largest computer maker, is adding activist shareholder Ralph Whitworth to its board to help shore up investor confidence shaken by strategy shifts and slashed sales forecasts.
