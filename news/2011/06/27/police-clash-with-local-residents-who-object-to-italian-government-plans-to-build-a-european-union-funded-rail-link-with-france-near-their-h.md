### [2011-06-27](/news/2011/06/27/index.md)

##### European Union
# Police clash with local residents who object to Italian government plans to build a European Union-funded rail link with France near their homes in the Italian Alps. The police intervention follows a peaceful candlelight nighttime prayer vigil featuring thousands of people. 

Police break down barricades erected by opponents of a new tunnel being bored through the Italian Alps for a high-speed rail link with France.
