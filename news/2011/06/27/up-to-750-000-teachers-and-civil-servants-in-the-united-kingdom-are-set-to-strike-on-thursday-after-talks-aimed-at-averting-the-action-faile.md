##### British
# Up to 750,000 teachers and civil servants in the United Kingdom are set to strike on Thursday after talks aimed at averting the action failed to broker an agreement. 

Leader of the TUC Brendan Barber says unions will continue talks with ministers over plans for public sector pensions, but the two sides are still at odds.
