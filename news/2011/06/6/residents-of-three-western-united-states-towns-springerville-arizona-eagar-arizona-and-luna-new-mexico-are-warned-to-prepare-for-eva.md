### [2011-06-6](/news/2011/06/6/index.md)

##### Western U.S.
# Residents of three Western United States towns - Springerville, Arizona, Eagar, Arizona and Luna, New Mexico - are warned to prepare for evacuation ahead of the Wallow Fire. one of the biggest in Arizona history. 

The Apache County Sheriff&rsquo;s Office has issued a mandatory evacuation order for Greer, Arizona.
