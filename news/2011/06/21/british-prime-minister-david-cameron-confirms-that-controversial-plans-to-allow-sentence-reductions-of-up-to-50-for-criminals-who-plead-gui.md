##### David Cameron
# British Prime Minister David Cameron confirms that controversial plans to allow sentence reductions of up to 50% for criminals who plead guilty in England and Wales have been scrapped. 

David Cameron defends his decision to scrap controversial plans to give prisoners who plead guilty at the earliest stage 50% off their jail terms.
