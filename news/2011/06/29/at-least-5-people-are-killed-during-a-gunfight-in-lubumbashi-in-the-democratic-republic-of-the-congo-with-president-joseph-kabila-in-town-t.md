### [2011-06-29](/news/2011/06/29/index.md)

##### Lubumbashi
# At least 5 people are killed during a gunfight in Lubumbashi in the Democratic Republic of the Congo, with President Joseph Kabila in town to view Independence Day ceremonies on Thursday. 

A gunfight in the Democratic Republic of Congo mining capital of Lubumbashi leaves two gunmen and three civilians dead, as the president visits the city.
