### [2011-06-29](/news/2011/06/29/index.md)

##### Sarfaraz Shah
# Six members of a paramilitary force and a civilian are charged with murder in relation to the death of unarmed Sarfaraz Shah in Karachi, whose 8 June shooting was filmed and later broadcast on television. 

Paramilitary soldiers accused of killing an unarmed man in the Pakistani city of Karachi are charged with murder and terrorism.
