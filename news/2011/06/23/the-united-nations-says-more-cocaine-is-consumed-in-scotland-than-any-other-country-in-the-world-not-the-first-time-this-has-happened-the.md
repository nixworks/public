### [2011-06-23](/news/2011/06/23/index.md)

##### United Nations
# The United Nations says more cocaine is consumed in Scotland than any other country in the world, not the first time this has happened. The country also consumes more heroin than anywhere else in the United Kingdom. 

Scotland is again placed at the top of the United Nations league for cocaine consumption, well ahead of England and Wales.
