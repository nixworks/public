### [2011-06-2](/news/2011/06/2/index.md)

##### PlayStation Network outage
# Following the PlayStation Network outage, the Sony website SonyPictures.com has been hacked, with unencrypted passwords and personal information of Sony customers within the website's database being discovered by the hackers. 

A hacker group says it has attacked SonyPictures.com and stolen more than one million passwords, e-mail addresses and other information.
