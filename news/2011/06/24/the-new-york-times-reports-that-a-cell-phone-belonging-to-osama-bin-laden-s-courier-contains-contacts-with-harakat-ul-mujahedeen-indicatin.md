### [2011-06-24](/news/2011/06/24/index.md)

##### The New York Times
# "The New York Times" reports that a cell phone belonging to Osama bin Laden's courier contains contacts with Harakat-ul-Mujahedeen indicating possible ties with Pakistan's intelligence agencies. 

Osama bin Laden used the militant group Harakat-ul-Mujahedeen, which has links to Pakistani intelligence, as part of his support network.
