##### Surgut-1 Power Station
# At least 12 people are injured in a gas explosion and fire at the Surgut-1 Power Station in the Russian city of Surgut, Tyumen Oblast. 

At least 12 people were injuredon Tuesday at the GRES-1 power station in the Siberian city ofSurgut, a spokesman for the local emergency ministry said.
