# The United Nations-backed Special Tribunal for Lebanon investigating the killing of Lebanon's former prime minister and business tycoon Rafic Hariri in 2005 issues 4 arrest warrants. 

Lebanese media says UN-backed court has charged four Hezbollah members for 2005 killing of former prime minister
