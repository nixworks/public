# Bosnian Serb genocide suspect Ratko Mladic makes his first appearance at the war crimes tribunal in The Hague, Netherlands, and dismisses charges against him as "obnoxious." 

Bosnian Serb genocide suspect Ratko Mladic dismissed as &quot;obnoxious&quot; charges against him Friday during his first appearance at a war crimes tribunal.
