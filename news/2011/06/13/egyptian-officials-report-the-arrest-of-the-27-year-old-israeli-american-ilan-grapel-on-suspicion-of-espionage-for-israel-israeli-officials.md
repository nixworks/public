### [2011-06-13](/news/2011/06/13/index.md)

##### Egypt
# Egyptian officials report the arrest of the 27-year-old Israeli-American Ilan Grapel on suspicion of espionage for Israel. Israeli officials have rejected the Egyptian accusations.

Israeli officials dismiss Egyptian reports that a suspected Mossad spy has been arrested in Cairo.
