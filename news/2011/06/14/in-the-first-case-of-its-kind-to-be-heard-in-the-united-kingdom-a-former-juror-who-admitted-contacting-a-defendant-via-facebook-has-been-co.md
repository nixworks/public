# In the first case of its kind to be heard in the United Kingdom, a former juror who admitted contacting a defendant via Facebook has been convicted of contempt of court. 

A former juror who admitted contacting a defendant on Facebook, in the first contempt of court case of its kind to be heard in the UK, is facing jail.
