##### Mid North Coast
# More than 16,000 people remain isolated and 3,350 are waiting to return to their homes due to continued flooding on the Mid North Coast of the Australian state of New South Wales. 

More than 16,000 residents remain isolated and 3350 people are waiting to return to their homes as floods gradually ease on the NSW mid north coast.
