### [2011-06-4](/news/2011/06/4/index.md)

##### Islamists
# Senior Islamic militant Ilyas Kashmiri, the leader of Harkat-ul-Jihad al-Islami, is killed in a US drone attack in South Waziristan in Pakistan. 

Ilyas Kashmiri was on a list which the US gave Pakistan of senior al-Qaeda members it wanted killed or captured.
