##### Wazir Akbar Khan
# 8 people are killed in a suicide attack at a Finest supermarket near the British embassy in the wealthy Wazir Akbar Khan suburb of Kabul. 

Eight people have died in a suicide attack at a supermarket popular with foreigners in the Afghan capital Kabul, officials say.
