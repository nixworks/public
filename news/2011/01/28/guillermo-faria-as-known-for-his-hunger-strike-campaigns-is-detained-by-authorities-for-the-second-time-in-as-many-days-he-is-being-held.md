##### Guillermo Farinas
# Guillermo Farinas, known for his hunger strike campaigns, is detained by authorities for the second time in as many days. He is being held in the Cuban city of Santa Clara. 

Cuban dissident Guillermo Farinas is released after being arrested three times in 48 hours, as he defied official warnings to keep a low profile.
