### [2011-01-15](/news/2011/01/15/index.md)

##### Rafael Ramirez
# Rafael Ramirez, energy minister for Venezuela, a member of the Organization of Petroleum Exporting Countries, played down concern that rising oil prices may choke off the world's still-tentative recovery, and said there is no need of an emergency meeting of OPEC.
