##### Daily Nation
# The "Daily Nation" publishes a picture of a police officer apparently shooting a male at point-blank range on a main road in Nairobi; Amnesty condemns the incident and three police officers are suspended. 

Kenyan authorities suspend three policemen after a newspaper published a picture of an officer apparently shooting a man dead.
