##### Hepatitis B
# As many as 430,000 people infected with Hepatitis B from reused needles settle their legal action and are to receive payments in Japan's biggest medical dispute. 

Up to 430,000 people infected with Hepatitis B from repeated use of needles, to receive payments over a 30-year period.
