### [2011-01-31](/news/2011/01/31/index.md)

##### Roger Vinson
# Roger Vinson of the United States District Court for the Northern District of Florida rules that the Health insurance mandate of President Barack Obama's health care reforms are unconstitutional. 

A judge said the insurance mandate in the health law made it unconstitutional, going further than a judge in Virginia had.
