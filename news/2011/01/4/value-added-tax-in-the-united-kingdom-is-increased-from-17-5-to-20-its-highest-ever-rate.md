### [2011-01-4](/news/2011/01/4/index.md)

##### Value added tax
# Value added tax in the United Kingdom is increased from 17.5% to 20%, its highest ever rate. 

The top rate of VAT rises from 17.5% to 20% as the government looks to boost tax revenue to cut the budget deficit.
