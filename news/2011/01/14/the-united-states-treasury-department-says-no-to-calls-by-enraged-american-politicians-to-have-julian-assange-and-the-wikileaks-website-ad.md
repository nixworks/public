### [2011-01-14](/news/2011/01/14/index.md)

##### Julian Assange
# The United States Treasury Department says "no" to calls by enraged American politicians to have Julian Assange and the WikiLeaks website added to its economic blacklist or sanctions list like so-called "terrorist groups". The Treasury Department cites a lack of "evidence at this time". 

WikiLeaks and Founder Julian Assange Will Not Be Added to Economic Terrorist Blacklist, Treasury Dept. Says, Despite Pressure
