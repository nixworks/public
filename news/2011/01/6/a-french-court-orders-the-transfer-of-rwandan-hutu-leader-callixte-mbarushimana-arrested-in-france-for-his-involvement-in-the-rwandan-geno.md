### [2011-01-6](/news/2011/01/6/index.md)

# A French court orders the transfer of Rwandan Hutu leader, Callixte Mbarushimana, arrested in France for his involvement in the Rwandan Genocide, to the International Criminal Court in The Hague. 

A Rwandan Hutu leader arrested in France must be transferred to the International Criminal Court for a war crimes trial, a French court has ruled.
