### [2011-09-7](/news/2011/09/7/index.md)

##### Broome County
# Ten thousand people are ordered to evacuate in Broome County in the US state of New York due to flooding of the Chenango River and the Susquehanna River. 

Dozens of districts closed schools early due to flooding, power outages and concerns about bus transportation.
