### [2011-09-15](/news/2011/09/15/index.md)

##### Australia
# Researchers at Australia's Monash University have identified a previously unknown species of bottlenose dolphin living in waters in and around Southeastern Australia, which they have named "Tursiops australis". 

Australian researchers find that a species of dolphin thought to be a common bottlenose is in fact a new species - and may already be endangered.
