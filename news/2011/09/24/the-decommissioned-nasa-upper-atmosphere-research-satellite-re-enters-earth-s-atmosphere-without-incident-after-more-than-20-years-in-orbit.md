##### NASA
# The decommissioned NASA Upper Atmosphere Research Satellite re-enters Earth's atmosphere without incident, after more than 20 years in orbit. 

Lazily falling from space, the 12,500-pound satellite UARS appeared Friday to be headed toward a early-morning, debris-spewing return to Earth.
