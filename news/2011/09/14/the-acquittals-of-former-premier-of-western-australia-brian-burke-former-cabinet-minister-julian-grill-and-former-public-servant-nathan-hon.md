### [2011-09-14](/news/2011/09/14/index.md)

##### Premier
# The acquittals of former Premier of Western Australia Brian Burke, former Cabinet Minister Julian Grill and former public servant Nathan Hondros on corruption charges are overturned by the Court of Appeal in Perth, with the three set to face a retrial. 

Former West Australian premier Brian Burke is set to face a new corruption trial after a previous acquittal was overturned on appeal.
