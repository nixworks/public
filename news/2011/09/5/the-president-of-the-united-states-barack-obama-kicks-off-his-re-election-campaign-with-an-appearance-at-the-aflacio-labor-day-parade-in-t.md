# The President of the United States Barack Obama kicks off his re-election campaign with an appearance at the AFL-CIO Labor Day parade in the city of Detroit. 

He won the state in 2008 by nearly 18 percentage points and pumped billions of tax dollars into the effort to save its flagship industry.
