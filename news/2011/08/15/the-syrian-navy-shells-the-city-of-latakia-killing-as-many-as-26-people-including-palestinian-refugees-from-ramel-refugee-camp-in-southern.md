### [2011-08-15](/news/2011/08/15/index.md)

##### Latakia
# The Syrian navy shells the city of Latakia, killing as many as 26 people, including Palestinian refugees from Ramel refugee camp in southern Latakia. 

Activists say 25 people killed as warships and tanks reportedly shell the northern port in an ongoing assault.
