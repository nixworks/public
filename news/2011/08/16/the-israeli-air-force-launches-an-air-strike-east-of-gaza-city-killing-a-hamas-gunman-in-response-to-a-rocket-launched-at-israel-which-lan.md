### [2011-08-16](/news/2011/08/16/index.md)

##### Israeli Air Force
# The Israeli Air Force launches an air strike east of Gaza City, killing a Hamas gunman, in response to a rocket launched at Israel which landed in the Negev Desert. 

Palestinian sources: Air strike wounds 3 gunmen; separate strike targets smuggling tunnel; rocket lands in open territory outside Beersheba.
