### [2011-08-23](/news/2011/08/23/index.md)

##### U.S. President
# President Obama provides temporary relief for illegal immigrants who are students, veterans, the elderly, crime victims and those with family, including same-sex partners, as part of immigration reform in the United States. 

Immigration authorities will use powers of prosecutorial discretion in existing law to suspend the deportations of most immigrants who have not been convicted of crimes.
