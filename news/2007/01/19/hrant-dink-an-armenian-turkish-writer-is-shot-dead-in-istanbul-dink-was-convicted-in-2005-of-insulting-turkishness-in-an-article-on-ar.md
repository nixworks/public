### [2007-01-19](/news/2007/01/19/index.md)

##### Hrant Dink
#  Hrant Dink, an Armenian-Turkish writer, is shot dead in Istanbul. Dink was convicted in 2005 of 'insulting Turkishness' in an article on Armenia-Turkey relations.
