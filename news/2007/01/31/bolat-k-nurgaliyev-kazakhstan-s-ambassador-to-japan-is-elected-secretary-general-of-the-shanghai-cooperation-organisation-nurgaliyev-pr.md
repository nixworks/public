### [2007-01-31](/news/2007/01/31/index.md)

##### Kazakhstan
#  Bolat K. Nurgaliyev, Kazakhstan's Ambassador to Japan, is elected Secretary-General of the Shanghai Cooperation Organisation. Nurgaliyev previously served as Ambassador to the United States and to the Republic of Korea. He stressed the need for the SCO to fight against the Three Evils.
