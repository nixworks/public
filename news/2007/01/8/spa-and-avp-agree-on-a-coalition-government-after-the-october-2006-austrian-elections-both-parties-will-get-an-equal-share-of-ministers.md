### [2007-01-8](/news/2007/01/8/index.md)

##### SPO
#  SPO and OVP agree on a coalition government after the October 2006 Austrian elections. Both parties will get an equal share of ministers with the SPO's Alfred Gusenbauer set to become Chancellor of Austria, and the OVP has had more success in implementing their demands in the coalition agreement. The government will be sworn in on 11 January.
