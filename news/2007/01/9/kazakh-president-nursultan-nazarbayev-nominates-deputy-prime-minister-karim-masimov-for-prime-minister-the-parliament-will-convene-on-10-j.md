### [2007-01-9](/news/2007/01/9/index.md)

##### Kazakh President
#  Kazakh President Nursultan Nazarbayev nominates Deputy Prime Minister Karim Masimov for Prime Minister. The Parliament will convene on 10 January to vote on the nomination.
