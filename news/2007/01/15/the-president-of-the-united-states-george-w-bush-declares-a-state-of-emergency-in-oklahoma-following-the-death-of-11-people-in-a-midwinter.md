### [2007-01-15](/news/2007/01/15/index.md)

##### U.S. President
#  The President of the United States George W. Bush declares a state of emergency in Oklahoma following the death of 11 people in a midwinter storm. Twenty-five people have died in the midwestern United States as a result of the storm.
