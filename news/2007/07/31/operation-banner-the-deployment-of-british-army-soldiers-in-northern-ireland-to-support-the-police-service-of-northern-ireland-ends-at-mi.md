### [2007-07-31](/news/2007/07/31/index.md)

##### British Army
#  Operation Banner, the deployment of British Army soldiers in Northern Ireland to support the Police Service of Northern Ireland, ends at midnight marking the conclusion of the Northern Ireland peace process. Operation Banner has been the longest British Army operation in history, lasting 38 years. 

The Minister for Foreign Affairs, Dermot Ahern, has welcomed the ending of British Army operations in Northern Ireland after 38 years.
