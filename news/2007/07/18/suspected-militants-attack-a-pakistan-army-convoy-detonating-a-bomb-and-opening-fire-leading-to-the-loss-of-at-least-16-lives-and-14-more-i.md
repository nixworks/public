##### Pakistan
#  Suspected militants attack a Pakistan army convoy detonating a bomb and opening fire leading to the loss of at least 16 lives and 14 more injuries. 

An attack on a convoy in northwestern Pakistan killed 16 soliders and wounded 14 others, Pakistani military officials said on Wednesday.
