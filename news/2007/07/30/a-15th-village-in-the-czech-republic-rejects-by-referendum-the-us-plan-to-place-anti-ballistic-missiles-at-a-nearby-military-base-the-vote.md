### [2007-07-30](/news/2007/07/30/index.md)

##### Czech Republic
#  A 15th village in the Czech Republic rejects by referendum the US plan to place anti-ballistic missiles at a nearby military base. The vote was 97% against the plan, however, the vote is non-binding on the national government.
