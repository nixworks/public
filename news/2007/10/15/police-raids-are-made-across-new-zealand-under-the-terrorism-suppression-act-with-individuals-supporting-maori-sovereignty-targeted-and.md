### [2007-10-15](/news/2007/10/15/index.md)

##### Police raids
#  Police raids are made across New Zealand under the Terrorism Suppression Act, with individuals supporting Maori sovereignty targeted, and arrested, for their alleged involvement in paramilitary activities. Activist Tame Iti is among those arrested.
