### [2007-10-21](/news/2007/10/21/index.md)

##### 2007 Slovenian presidential elections
#  Voters go to the polls in Slovenia to elect a new President, with Lojze Peterle winning most votes but not a majority. A runoff between him and Danilo Trk will be held on November 11.
