### [2007-08-28](/news/2007/08/28/index.md)

##### Cockpit voice recorder
#  The Cockpit voice recorder is retrieved from the wreckage of Adam Air Flight 574, 24 hours after the recovery of the Flight data recorder. 

Black box data recorders from an Adam Air jet have been found, eight months after the plane crashed in Indonesia killing all 102 people aboard.
