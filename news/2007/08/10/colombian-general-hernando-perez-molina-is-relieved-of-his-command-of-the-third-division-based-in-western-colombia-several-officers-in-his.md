### [2007-08-10](/news/2007/08/10/index.md)

##### Colombia
#  Colombian general Hernando Perez Molina is relieved of his command of the Third Division based in western Colombia. Several officers in his command are accused of collaborating with the Norte del Valle cocaine cartel.
