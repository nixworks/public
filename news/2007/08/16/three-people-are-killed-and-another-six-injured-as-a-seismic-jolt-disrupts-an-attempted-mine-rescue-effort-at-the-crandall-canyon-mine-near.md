### [2007-08-16](/news/2007/08/16/index.md)

##### Seismic
#  Three people are killed and another six injured as a seismic jolt disrupts an attempted mine rescue effort at the Crandall Canyon Mine near Huntington, Utah, United States. 

Three rescue workers are dead and six others were injured during an attempt to reach six men trapped at Crandall Canyon Mine, officials said.
