### [2007-08-2](/news/2007/08/2/index.md)

##### DR Congo
#  100 people are killed in the Democratic Republic of the Congo when a train derails. Government officials attribute the accident to faulty brakes. 

About 100 people are dead after an overnight train accident in central Congo, government officials said Thursday.
