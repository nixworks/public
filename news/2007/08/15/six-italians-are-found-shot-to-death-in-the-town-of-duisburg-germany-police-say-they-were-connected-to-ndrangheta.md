### [2007-08-15](/news/2007/08/15/index.md)

##### Italians
#  Six Italians are found shot to death in the town of Duisburg, Germany. Police say they were connected to 'Ndrangheta. 

Six people were found fatally shot in the town of Duisburg in western Germany, police said Wednesday.
