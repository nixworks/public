### [2007-08-6](/news/2007/08/6/index.md)

##### Trinidad
#  Trinidad Chief Magistrate Sherman McNicolls orders the extradition of three men to the United States to face charges of involvement in a terrorist attack on John F. Kennedy Airport. 

A ruling by a judge in Trinidad brings three men one step closer to Brooklyn and the charges that they conspired to attack Kennedy International Airport.
