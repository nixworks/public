### [2007-08-24](/news/2007/08/24/index.md)

##### United States District Court
#  United States District Court judge William Hoeveler rules against former Panamanian leader Manuel Noriega returning to Panama after he completes his sentence in a United States prison stating that there was no reason why he shouldn't be extradited to France to face a prison term there.
