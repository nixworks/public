### [2007-08-29](/news/2007/08/29/index.md)

##### Bishop's Stortford
#  Three people are killed - including a father and son - in a "targeted incident" involving firearms at a house in Bishop's Stortford. Two others are injured, but a 3 year-old girl is unharmed. Police are hunting "two Asian men" in connection with the attack.
