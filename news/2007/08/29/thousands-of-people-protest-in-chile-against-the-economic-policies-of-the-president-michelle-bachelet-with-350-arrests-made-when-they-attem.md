### [2007-08-29](/news/2007/08/29/index.md)

##### Chile
#  Thousands of people protest in Chile against the economic policies of the President Michelle Bachelet with 350 arrests made when they attempt to enter the grounds of the presidential palace. 

Thousands of workers have marched in Santiago against the economic policies of President Michelle Bachelet, arguing for more social equality.
