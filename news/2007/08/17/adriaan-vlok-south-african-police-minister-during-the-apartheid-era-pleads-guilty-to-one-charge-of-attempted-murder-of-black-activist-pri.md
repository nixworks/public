### [2007-08-17](/news/2007/08/17/index.md)

##### Adriaan Vlok
#  Adriaan Vlok, South African Police Minister during the apartheid era, pleads guilty to one charge of attempted murder of black activist priest Frank Chikane by poisoning his underwear. He is given a suspended sentence of ten years in jail.
