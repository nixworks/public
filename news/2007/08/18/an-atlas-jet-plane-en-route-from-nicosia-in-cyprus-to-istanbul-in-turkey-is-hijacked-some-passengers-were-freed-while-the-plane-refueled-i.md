### [2007-08-18](/news/2007/08/18/index.md)

#  An Atlas Jet plane en route from Nicosia in Cyprus to Istanbul in Turkey is hijacked. Some passengers were freed while the plane refueled in Antalya in southern Turkey. All 142 people escape unhurt while the hijackers surrender.
