### [2007-05-10](/news/2007/05/10/index.md)

##### Pope Benedict XVI
#  Pope Benedict XVI urges tens of thousands of young Brazilian Catholics packing the Pacaembu stadium in So Paulo to resist the temptations of wealth, power and other "snares of evil," and tells them to promote life from "its beginning to natural end."
