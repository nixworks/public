### [2007-09-23](/news/2007/09/23/index.md)

##### Yasuo Fukuda
#  Yasuo Fukuda, a political moderate, is elected by Japan's governing Liberal Democratic Party to become the country's next prime minister. 

Yasuo Fukuda, a mild-mannered political moderate, is known for his ability to build consensus behind the scenes.
