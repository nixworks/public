### [2007-09-6](/news/2007/09/6/index.md)

##### Czech Republic
#  A study published by United States and Czech researchers claims that there is a 90% chance that the object that caused the Chicxulub crater in Mexico's Yucatn Peninsula and probably led to the extinction of the dinosaurs came from the Baptistina family of asteroids.
