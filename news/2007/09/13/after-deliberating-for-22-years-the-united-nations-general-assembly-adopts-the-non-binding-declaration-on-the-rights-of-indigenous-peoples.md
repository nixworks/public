### [2007-09-13](/news/2007/09/13/index.md)

##### United Nations General Assembly
#  After deliberating for 22 years, the United Nations General Assembly adopts the non-binding Declaration on the Rights of Indigenous Peoples; 143 countries vote in favour, while 11 abstain and Australia, Canada, New Zealand and the United States vote against.
