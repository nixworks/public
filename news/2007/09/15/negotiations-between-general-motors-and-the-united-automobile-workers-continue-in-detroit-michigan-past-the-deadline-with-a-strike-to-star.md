### [2007-09-15](/news/2007/09/15/index.md)

##### General Motors
#  Negotiations between General Motors and the United Automobile Workers continue in Detroit, Michigan past the deadline with a strike to start if negotiations fail. 

On Saturday, bargainers for G.M. and the union did not repeat the marathon sessions they held on Thursday and Friday nights.
