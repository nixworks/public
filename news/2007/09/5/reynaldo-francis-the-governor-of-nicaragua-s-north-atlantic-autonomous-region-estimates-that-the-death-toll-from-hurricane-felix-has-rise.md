### [2007-09-5](/news/2007/09/5/index.md)

##### Reynaldo Francis
#  Reynaldo Francis, the Governor of Nicaragua's North Atlantic Autonomous Region, estimates that the death toll from Hurricane Felix has risen to at least 21. Since his statement, the death toll has risen to 38, with 80 people missing. 

Hurricane Felix killed at least 38 people on Nicaragua&#039;s Caribbean coast and more than 80 people were missing after the storm destroyed thousands of flimsy homes, the Government said.
