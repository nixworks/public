### [2007-03-24](/news/2007/03/24/index.md)

##### Xi Jinping
#  Xi Jinping is appointed the new CPC Shanghai Committee Secretary, thus becoming the number one figure in Shanghai, replacing acting party chief Han Zheng. He leaves his post as Party chief in Zhejiang. 

The ruling Communist Party appointed Xi Jinping as the top leader of Shanghai.
