### [2007-04-17](/news/2007/04/17/index.md)

##### Iccho Ito
#  Iccho Ito, the mayor of Nagasaki, Japan, is shot at least twice outside his re-election campaign headquarters. The assassin, Tetsuya Shiroo, is alleged to be a senior member of a local gang affiliated to the Yamaguchi-gumi crime syndicate. 

The mayor of the Japanese city of Nagasaki died early on Wednesday after being shot by a gangster, police said.
