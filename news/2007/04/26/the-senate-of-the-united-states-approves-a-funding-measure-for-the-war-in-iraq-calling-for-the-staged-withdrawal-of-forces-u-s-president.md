### [2007-04-26](/news/2007/04/26/index.md)

##### Senator
#  The Senate of the United States approves a funding measure for the War in Iraq calling for the staged withdrawal of forces. U.S. President George W. Bush responded by stating that his reaction to the bill will be to veto it.
