### [2007-11-30](/news/2007/11/30/index.md)

##### Colombia
#  Colombian authorities release seized videos of 16 hostages being held by the FARC rebel group; these include former senator and presidential candidate ngrid Betancourt, last heard from in 2002, and three U.S. defence contractors abducted in 2003.
