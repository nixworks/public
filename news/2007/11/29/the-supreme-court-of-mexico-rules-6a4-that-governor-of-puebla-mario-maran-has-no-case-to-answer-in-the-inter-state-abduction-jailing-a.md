### [2007-11-29](/news/2007/11/29/index.md)

##### Supreme Court
#  The Supreme Court of Mexico rules 6-4 that Governor of Puebla Mario Marin has no case to answer in the inter-state abduction, jailing, and harassment of journalist Lydia Cacho. 

In a setback for journalistic freedom in Mexico, the judges ruled 6 to 4 against the journalist, Lydia Cacho, despite claims that public officials conspired to harass her.
