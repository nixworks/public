### [2007-02-13](/news/2007/02/13/index.md)

##### Japan
#  Japan holds an international conference to push for the resumption of commercial whaling. Anti-whaling nations in the International Whaling Commission such as the United States, United Kingdom and Australia have boycotted the conference.
