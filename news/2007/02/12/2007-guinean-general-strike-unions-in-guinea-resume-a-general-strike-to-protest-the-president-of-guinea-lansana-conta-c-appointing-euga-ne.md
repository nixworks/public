##### 2007 Guinean general strike
#  2007 Guinean general strike: Unions in Guinea resume a general strike to protest the President of Guinea Lansana Conte appointing Eugene Camara as Prime Minister of Guinea. At least 17 people have died in protests over the weekend. The protest started on the morning of the 12th with a march from the centre of Conakry to the palace. Widespread problems with armed bandits taking advantage of the insecurity have also been reported. Lansana Conte has now declared a state of emergency.
