### [2007-02-6](/news/2007/02/6/index.md)

##### Human Rights Watch
#  A Human Rights Watch report concludes that European Union sanctions on the Government of Uzbekistan, which came in response to the "Andijan massacre" in May 2005, have failed due to a lack of commitment in enforcing them.
