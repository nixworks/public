### [2007-02-24](/news/2007/02/24/index.md)

##### Virginia General Assembly
#  The Virginia General Assembly votes unanimously in favor of a motion expressing "profound regret" for Virginia's role in promoting slavery, which is the first apology for slavery passed by a U.S. state legislature.
