### [2007-02-20](/news/2007/02/20/index.md)

##### Felix Kulov
#  Felix Kulov, former Prime Minister of Kyrgyzstan, breaks his political alliance with President Kurmanbek Bakiyev. Kulov forms the United Front for a Worthy Future for Kyrgyzstan with Ar-Namys and other political parties.
