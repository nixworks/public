### [2007-12-15](/news/2007/12/15/index.md)

##### 2007 United Nations Climate Change Conference
#  The 2007 United Nations Climate Change Conference concludes with an agreement for a two-year process to develop a new treaty on climate change. 

An agreement reached Saturday pushes debates on U.S. participation into the administration of a new president.
