### [2007-12-21](/news/2007/12/21/index.md)

##### Belgian authorities
#  Belgian authorities arrest 14 people the government calls Islamic extremists, who they say were plotting to free an al-Qaeda member from prison. 

The suspects were planning to use explosives to free an Al Qaeda sympathizer from prison, officials said.
