### [2007-12-10](/news/2007/12/10/index.md)

##### U.S.
#  The United States Supreme Court decides that federal district judges have broad discretion to impose what they think are reasonable sentences, even if federal guidelines call for different sentences. The case that they ruled on involved crack cocaine. 

The Supreme Court said that federal district judges are free to disagree with sentencing guidelines.
