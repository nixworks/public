##### Parliamentary elections
# Parliamentary elections are held in Cote d'Ivoire. 28 seats are excluded because of a boycott by the Rally of the Republicans. The result is a victory for the Ivorian Popular Front, which wins 96 of the 225 seats in the National Assembly of Cote d'Ivoire.
