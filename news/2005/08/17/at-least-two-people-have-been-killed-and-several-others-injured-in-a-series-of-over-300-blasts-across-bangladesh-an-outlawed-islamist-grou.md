### [2005-08-17](/news/2005/08/17/index.md)

##### Bangladesh
#  At least two people have been killed and several others injured in a series of over 300 blasts across Bangladesh. An outlawed Islamist group, Jamaat-ul-Mujahideen Bangladesh, has claimed responsibility.
