##### Hurricane Katrina
#  Hurricane Katrina as a Category 3 storm makes landfall at 6:10 a.m. CDT (11:10 a.m. UTC). The eye is just east of New Orleans. This date is now known as Black Monday to New Orleanians and many residents of the Gulf Coast.
