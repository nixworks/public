### [2005-08-28](/news/2005/08/28/index.md)

##### Saffir-Simpson Hurricane Scale
#  Hurricane Katrina continues to rapidly strengthen reaching Category Five status. It has the second lowest pressure for an American hurricane in recorded history, with only the Labor Day Hurricane of 1935 having a lower measurement. Katrina is at 908 mb. and dropping; the Labor Day event of 1935 had 892 mb.
