### [2005-08-10](/news/2005/08/10/index.md)

##### Sikorsky S-76
#  A Sikorsky S-76 helicopter of the Finnish company Copterline has crashed into the Gulf of Finland near Estonia's capital Tallinn with 14 on board; eight Finns, four Estonians and two Americans. There are no survivors.
