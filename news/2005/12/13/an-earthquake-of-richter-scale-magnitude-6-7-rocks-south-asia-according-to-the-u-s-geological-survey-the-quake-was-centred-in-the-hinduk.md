### [2005-12-13](/news/2005/12/13/index.md)

##### An earthquake
#  An earthquake of Richter scale magnitude 6.7 rocks South Asia. According to the U.S. Geological Survey, the quake was centred in the Hindukush region of northeastern Afghanistan. 

Although there were no reports of casualties, many houses in Uri, Fafiabad and Baramulla were damaged.
