### [2005-12-16](/news/2005/12/16/index.md)

##### Liaoyuan
#  At least 39 people, 33 of whom were patients, die after a fire breaks out in a hospital in the city of Liaoyuan, near Changchun in China's Jilin Province. The cause of the fire is unknown.
