### [2005-07-14](/news/2005/07/14/index.md)

##### New York City
#  In the US, customs in New York airport have refused entry of British leading moderate Muslim theologian Zaki Badawi. Bawadi had been invited to a talk in Chautauqua Institution. He had publicly condemned the bombings in London
