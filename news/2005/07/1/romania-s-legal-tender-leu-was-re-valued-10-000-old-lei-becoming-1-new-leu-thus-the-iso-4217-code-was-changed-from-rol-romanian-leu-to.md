### [2005-07-1](/news/2005/07/1/index.md)

##### Romania
#  Romania's legal tender, leu was re-valued, 10,000 old lei becoming 1 new leu. Thus the ISO 4217 code was changed from ROL (Romanian leu) to RON (Romanian New leu).
