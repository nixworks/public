### [2005-04-11](/news/2005/04/11/index.md)

##### Australia
#  Australian Liberal MP David Tollner urges people to kill poisonous cane toads with cricket bats and golf clubs. The toads have become a nuisance in the Northern Territory. Animal rights groups prefer freezing them to death.
