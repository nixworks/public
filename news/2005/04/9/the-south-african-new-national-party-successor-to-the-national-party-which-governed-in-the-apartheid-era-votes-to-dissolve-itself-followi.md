### [2005-04-9](/news/2005/04/9/index.md)

##### South Africa
#  The South African New National Party, successor to the National Party which governed in the apartheid era, votes to dissolve itself following poor results in last year's elections. Its elected representatives are expected to join the governing African National Congress.
