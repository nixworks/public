### [2005-04-20](/news/2005/04/20/index.md)

##### Alfredo Palacio
#  Vice President Alfredo Palacio is sworn in as new interim President of Ecuador, after Congress removes President Lucio Gutierrez from office after a week of escalating street protests demanding his ouster. Riot police fire tear gas at protesters who demand the resignation of president Gutierrez. One man has died of a heart attack during the demonstration.
