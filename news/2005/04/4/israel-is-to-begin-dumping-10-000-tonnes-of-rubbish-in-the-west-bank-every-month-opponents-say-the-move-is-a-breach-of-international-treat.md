### [2005-04-4](/news/2005/04/4/index.md)

##### Israel
#  Israel is to begin dumping 10,000 tonnes of rubbish in the West Bank every month. Opponents say the move is a breach of international treaties and may also pollute the main Palestinian water supply, an assertion denied by the Israelis.
