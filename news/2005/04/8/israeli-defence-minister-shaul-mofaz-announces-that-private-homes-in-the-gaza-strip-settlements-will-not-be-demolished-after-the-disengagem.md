### [2005-04-8](/news/2005/04/8/index.md)

##### Israel
#  Israeli Defence Minister Shaul Mofaz announces that private homes in the Gaza strip settlements will not be demolished after the disengagement plan. Religious structures such as synagogues, Mikveh baths and cemeteries will be dismantled and transferred inside the "Green line". 

Defense, Internal Security Ministers recommend transferring Gush Katif houses to Palestinians; synagogues, cemeteries to move to Israel
