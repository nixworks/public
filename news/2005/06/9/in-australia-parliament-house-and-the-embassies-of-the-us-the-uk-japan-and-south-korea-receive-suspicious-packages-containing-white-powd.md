### [2005-06-9](/news/2005/06/9/index.md)

##### Parliament House
#  In Australia, Parliament House and the embassies of the US, the UK, Japan and South Korea receive suspicious packages containing white powder. Federal Police later state that analysis showed the powder to be "harmless", and was sent as a hoax, following similar events in the past week.
