### [2005-06-15](/news/2005/06/15/index.md)

##### Abu Musab al-Zarqawi
#  Spanish police arrest 17 suspected Islamic extremists in a series of raids around the country. Eleven are alleged to be associated with Abu Musab al-Zarqawi, and 5 are alleged to be connected to the Madrid train bombings of 11 March 2004.
