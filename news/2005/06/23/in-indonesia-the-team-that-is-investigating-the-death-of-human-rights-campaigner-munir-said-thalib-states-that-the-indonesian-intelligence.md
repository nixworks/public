### [2005-06-23](/news/2005/06/23/index.md)

##### Indonesia
#  In Indonesia, the team that is investigating the death of human rights campaigner Munir Said Thalib states that the Indonesian intelligence agency BIN may be involved. Munir died of arsenic poisoning en route to the Netherlands on September 7, 2004.
