### [2005-06-5](/news/2005/06/5/index.md)

##### Kuwait
#  In Kuwait, two women become the first females to be appointed to municipal council. They are Sheikha Fatima al-Sabah of the ruling Sabah family and engineer Fawziya al-Bahar
