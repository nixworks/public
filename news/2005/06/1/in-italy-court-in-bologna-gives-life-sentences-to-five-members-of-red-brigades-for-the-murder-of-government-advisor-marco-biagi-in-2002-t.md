### [2005-06-1](/news/2005/06/1/index.md)

##### Italy
#  In Italy, court in Bologna gives life sentences to five members of Red Brigades for the murder of government advisor Marco Biagi in 2002. They are suspected members of the Communist Combatant faction of the Brigades
