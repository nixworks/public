### [2005-06-2](/news/2005/06/2/index.md)

##### Brazil
#  In Brazil, police have issued 124 arrest warrants from people suspected of illegal logging in the Amazon Rainforest over the last 15 years. 89 people are arrested, many of them from government agencies
