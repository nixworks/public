### [2005-06-6](/news/2005/06/6/index.md)

##### Condoleezza Rice
#  U.S. Secretary of State Condoleezza Rice opened the 35th General Assembly meeting of the Organization of American States Sunday in Fort Lauderdale, Florida. Secretary Rice called on member states to do more to protect democracy in the region, and strengthen civil society.
