### [2005-01-28](/news/2005/01/28/index.md)

#  Thailand's prime minister Thaksin Shinawatra demands that Malaysia extradite separatist leader of Gerakan Mujahidin Islam Patani party known as 'Jehkumir Kuteh' or 'Abdul Rahman Ahmad' (in addition to other names). Malaysian government refuses because he is Malaysian but they would cooperate the best they can
