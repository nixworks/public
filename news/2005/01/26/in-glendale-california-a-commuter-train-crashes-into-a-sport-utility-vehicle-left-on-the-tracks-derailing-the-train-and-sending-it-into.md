### [2005-01-26](/news/2005/01/26/index.md)

##### Glendale, California
#  In Glendale, California, a commuter train crashes into a sport utility vehicle left on the tracks, derailing the train and sending it into another commuter train, killing 11 and injuring at least 100. Juan Manuel lvarez, who allegedly drove the car to the railway in an attempt to commit suicide, is accused of eleven murders.
