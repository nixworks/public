### [2005-01-24](/news/2005/01/24/index.md)

##### North Korea
#  North Korea has cut its food rations to half the amount that the United Nations World Food Program recommends. U.N. officials say the cut appears temporary and is not unprecedented in a country where fluctuations in public food distribution are regular.
