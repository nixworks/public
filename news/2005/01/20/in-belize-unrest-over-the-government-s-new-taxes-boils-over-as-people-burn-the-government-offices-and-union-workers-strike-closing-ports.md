### [2005-01-20](/news/2005/01/20/index.md)

##### Belize
#  In Belize, unrest over the government's new taxes boils over as people burn the government offices and union workers strike, closing ports and shutting down water services. There are reports that the United Kingdom says it will send in 1500 soldiers to maintain peace.
