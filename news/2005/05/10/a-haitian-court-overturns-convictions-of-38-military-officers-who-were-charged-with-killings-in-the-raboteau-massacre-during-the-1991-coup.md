### [2005-05-10](/news/2005/05/10/index.md)

##### Haiti
#  A Haitian court overturns convictions of 38 military officers who were charged with killings in the Raboteau Massacre during the 1991 coup against Jean-Bertrand Aristide. The Ecumenical Center for Human Rights states that the trial was annulled on technicality.
