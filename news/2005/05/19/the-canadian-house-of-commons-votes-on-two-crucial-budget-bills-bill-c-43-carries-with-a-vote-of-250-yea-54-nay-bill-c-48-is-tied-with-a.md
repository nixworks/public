### [2005-05-19](/news/2005/05/19/index.md)

##### Canadian House of Commons
#  The Canadian House of Commons votes on two crucial budget bills. Bill C-43 carries with a vote of 250 yea, 54 nay. Bill C-48 is tied with a vote of 152 yea, 152 nay, with the Speaker voting in favour, breaking the tie and carrying the bill. The Canadian government survives the Motion of no confidence.
