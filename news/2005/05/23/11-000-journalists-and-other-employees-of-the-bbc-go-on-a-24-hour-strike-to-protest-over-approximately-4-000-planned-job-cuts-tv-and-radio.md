### [2005-05-23](/news/2005/05/23/index.md)

##### BBC
#  11,000 journalists and other employees of the BBC go on a 24-hour strike to protest over approximately 4,000 planned job cuts. TV and Radio news output has been the worst affected, with few new stories materialising and only a skeleton service being offered.
