### [2005-05-23](/news/2005/05/23/index.md)

##### Channel 10
#  Channel 10, an Israeli television station, broadcasts footage of what it claims is Israeli Defence Forces using a Palestinian youth as a human shield against rock-throwers in the West Bank. The IDF denies the allegation.
