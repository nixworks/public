### [2005-05-3](/news/2005/05/3/index.md)

##### Mogadishu
#  An explosion in a football stadium in Mogadishu, Somalia kills 15 people when new prime minister Ali Mohammed Ghedi begins his speech. Authorities later state that a security guard accidentally set off a grenade.
