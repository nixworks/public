### [2005-05-1](/news/2005/05/1/index.md)

##### Iraq
#  Iraqi and American militaries hold several suspects for questioning in the Margaret Hassan kidnapping case. Hassan, director of CARE's Iraq division, was kidnapped by insurgents in late October 2004 and subsequently believed to be killed. 

Items belonging to abducted British aid worker Margaret Hassan have been found during a raid to round up suspects.
