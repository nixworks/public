### [2005-05-9](/news/2005/05/9/index.md)

##### Democratic Republic of Congo
#  In the Democratic Republic of Congo, president Joseph Kabila visits Lumumbashi in Katanga province. At least 30 people have been arrested for an attempted separatist coup in the province.
