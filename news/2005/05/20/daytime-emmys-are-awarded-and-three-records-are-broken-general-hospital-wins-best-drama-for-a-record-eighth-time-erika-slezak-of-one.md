##### Daytime Emmys
#  Daytime Emmys are awarded, and three records are broken. "General Hospital" wins Best Drama for a record eighth time, Erika Slezak of "One Life to Live" wins her sixth Daytime Emmy for Best Actress, and "The Ellen DeGeneres Show" wins five Daytime Emmys, including Best Talk Show Host, and Best Talk Show.
