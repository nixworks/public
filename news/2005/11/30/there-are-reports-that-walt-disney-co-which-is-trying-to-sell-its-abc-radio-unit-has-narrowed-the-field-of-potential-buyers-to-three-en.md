### [2005-11-30](/news/2005/11/30/index.md)

##### Walt Disney Co.
#  There are reports that Walt Disney Co., which is trying to sell its ABC Radio unit, has narrowed the field of potential buyers to three: Entercom Communications Corp., Cumulus Media Inc. and a private equity firm Kohlberg Kravis Roberts.
