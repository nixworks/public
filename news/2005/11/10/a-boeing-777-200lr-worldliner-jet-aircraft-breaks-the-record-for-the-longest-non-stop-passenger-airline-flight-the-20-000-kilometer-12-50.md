### [2005-11-10](/news/2005/11/10/index.md)

##### Boeing 777
#  A Boeing 777-200LR Worldliner jet aircraft breaks the record for the longest non-stop passenger airline flight. The 20,000-kilometer (12,500-mile) flight from Hong Kong to London lasted 23 hours.
