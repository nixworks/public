### [2005-03-23](/news/2005/03/23/index.md)

##### Central African Republic
#  In the Central African Republic, the spokesman of Andr Kolingba, former military ruler, says that yesterday's shootout outside his house was an assassination attempt. Kolingba has called for an annulment of the results of the presidential elections.
