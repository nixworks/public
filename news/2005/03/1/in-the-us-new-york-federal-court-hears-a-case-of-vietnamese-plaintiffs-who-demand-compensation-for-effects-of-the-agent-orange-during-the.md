### [2005-03-1](/news/2005/03/1/index.md)

##### New York City
#  In the US, New York Federal court hears a case of Vietnamese plaintiffs who demand compensation for effects of the Agent Orange during the Vietnam War. United States Justice Department and companies that manufactured Agent Orange demand that the federal judge dismiss the case.
