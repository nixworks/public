### [2005-03-2](/news/2005/03/2/index.md)

##### Titan Corporation
#  US company Titan Corporation agrees to pay a fine equivalent to US$28.5 million after they admit attempting bribery to get a military communications contract in Benin. The corporation allegedly gave US$2 million to the re-election campaign of president Mathieu Krkou.
