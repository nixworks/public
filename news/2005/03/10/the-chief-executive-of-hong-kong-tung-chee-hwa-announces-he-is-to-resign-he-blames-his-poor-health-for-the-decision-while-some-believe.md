### [2005-03-10](/news/2005/03/10/index.md)

##### Chief Executive
#  The Chief Executive of Hong Kong, Tung Chee Hwa, announces he is to resign. He blames his poor health for the decision, while some believe that he may have been dismissed by the Chinese government.
