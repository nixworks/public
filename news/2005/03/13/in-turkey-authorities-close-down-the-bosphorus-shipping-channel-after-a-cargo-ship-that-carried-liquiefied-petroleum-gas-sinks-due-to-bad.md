### [2005-03-13](/news/2005/03/13/index.md)

##### Turkey
#  In Turkey, authorities close down the Bosphorus shipping channel after a cargo ship that carried liquiefied petroleum gas sinks due to bad weather. It takes hours for the shipping authorities to collect gas tanks.
