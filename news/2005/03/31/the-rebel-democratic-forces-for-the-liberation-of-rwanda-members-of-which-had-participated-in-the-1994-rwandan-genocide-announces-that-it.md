### [2005-03-31](/news/2005/03/31/index.md)

##### Democratic Forces for the Liberation of Rwanda
#  The rebel Democratic Forces for the Liberation of Rwanda, members of which had participated in the 1994 Rwandan Genocide, announces that it is giving up its armed struggle. The FDLR has been a key source of instability in the aftermath of the Second Congo War.
