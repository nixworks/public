### [2005-03-31](/news/2005/03/31/index.md)

##### Neil Young
#  Two days after surgery to repair a brain aneurysm, Neil Young collapses on a New York street, bleeding from a rupture in a femoral artery. He cancels a scheduled appearance at the Juno Awards and reveals his surgery to the press for the first time.
