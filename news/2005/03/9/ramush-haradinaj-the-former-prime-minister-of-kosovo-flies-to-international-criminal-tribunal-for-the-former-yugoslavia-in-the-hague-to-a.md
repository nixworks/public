### [2005-03-9](/news/2005/03/9/index.md)

##### Ramush Haradinaj
#  Ramush Haradinaj, the former prime minister of Kosovo, flies to International Criminal Tribunal for the Former Yugoslavia in The Hague to answer for charges for his role as a former commander of Kosovo Liberation Army in 1998-1999. He goes there voluntarily.
