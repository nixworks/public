### [2005-03-9](/news/2005/03/9/index.md)

##### Israeli settlement
#  In Israel, an official report has revealed that Israeli state bodies have been diverting funds from state projects to fund the establishment of illegal Israeli settlements in the West Bank. Former state prosecutor Talia Sasson has recommended that criminal investigations be launched.
