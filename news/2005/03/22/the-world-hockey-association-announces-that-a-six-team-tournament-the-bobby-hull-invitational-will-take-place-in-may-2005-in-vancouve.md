### [2005-03-22](/news/2005/03/22/index.md)

#  The World Hockey Association announces that a six-team tournament, the ""Bobby Hull Invitational"", will take place in May 2005 in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada. 

The WHA will hold a tournament at the home of the Vancouver Canucks and may land a European investment group.
