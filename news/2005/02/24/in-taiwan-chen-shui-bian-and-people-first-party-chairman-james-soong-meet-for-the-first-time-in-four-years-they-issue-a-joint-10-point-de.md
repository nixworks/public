### [2005-02-24](/news/2005/02/24/index.md)

##### Taiwan
#  In Taiwan, Chen Shui-bian and People First Party Chairman James Soong meet for the first time in four years. They issue a joint 10-point declaration emphasizing their agreement on preserving the "status quo" in cross strait relations.
