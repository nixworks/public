### [2005-02-1](/news/2005/02/1/index.md)

##### Kenya
#  Ex-president of Kenya and chairman of KANU party, Daniel arap Moi, is due to step down. His successor will be Uhuru Kenyatta, son of Jomo Kenyatta.
