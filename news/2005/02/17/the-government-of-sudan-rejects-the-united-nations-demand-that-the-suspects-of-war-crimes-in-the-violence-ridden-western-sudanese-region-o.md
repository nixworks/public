### [2005-02-17](/news/2005/02/17/index.md)

##### Sudanese cabinet
#  The government of Sudan rejects the United Nations' demand that the suspects of war crimes in the violence ridden western Sudanese region of Darfur would be put before the International Criminal Court in The Hague.
