### [2005-02-26](/news/2005/02/26/index.md)

##### Canadian
#  Canadian Prime Minister Paul Martin announces that the US must get permission first before launching missiles over Canadian airspace, after recently announcing Canada's non-involvement in the controversial US National Missile Defence plan.
