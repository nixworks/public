### [2005-02-2](/news/2005/02/2/index.md)

##### Robert Mugabe
#  Robert Mugabe, president of Zimbabwe, announces that a general election will be held March 31. The country's main opposition party, Movement for Democratic Change, has not yet decided whether to participate.
