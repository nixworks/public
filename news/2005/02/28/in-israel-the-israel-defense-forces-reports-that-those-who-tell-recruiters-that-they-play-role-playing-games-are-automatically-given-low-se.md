### [2005-02-28](/news/2005/02/28/index.md)

##### Israel
#  In Israel the Israel Defense Forces reports that those who tell recruiters that they play role-playing games are automatically given low security clearance and are sent directly to a professional for an evaluation, usually a psychologist. 

IDF says players are detached from reality and automatically given a low security clearance
