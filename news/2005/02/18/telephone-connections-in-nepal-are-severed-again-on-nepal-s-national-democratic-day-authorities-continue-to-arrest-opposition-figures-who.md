### [2005-02-18](/news/2005/02/18/index.md)

##### Telephone
#  Telephone connections in Nepal are severed again, on Nepal's National Democratic Day. Authorities continue to arrest opposition figures who had planned demonstrations against the new government of King Gyanendra.
