### [2005-09-7](/news/2005/09/7/index.md)

##### Independent Inquiry Committee
#  A report by the Independent Inquiry Committee criticizes U.N. Secretary General Kofi Annan and the U.N. Security Council's role in the Oil-for-Food Programme. 

According to an independent investigatory committee, U.N. officials allowed corruption to overrun the Oil-for-Food program.
