### [2005-09-25](/news/2005/09/25/index.md)

##### Swiss
#  Swiss voters approve a referendum by 56% to 44% to allow citizens from the 10 newest European Union member countries to travel and work in Switzerland. Quotas will be applied until 2011 on the number of people allowed to settle.
