### [2005-09-1](/news/2005/09/1/index.md)

##### Tibet Autonomous Region
#  As part of celebrations for the 40th Anniversary for the founding of the Tibet Autonomous Region in the People's Republic of China, 20,000 people gather at the Potala Palace Square for a cultural performance. CPC Politburo's Jia Qinglin attended.
