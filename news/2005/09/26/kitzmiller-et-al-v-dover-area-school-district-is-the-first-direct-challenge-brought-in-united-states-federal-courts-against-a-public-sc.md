##### Kitzmiller v. Dover Area School District
#  "Kitzmiller et al. v. Dover Area School District" is the first direct challenge brought in United States federal courts against a public school district curriculum mandating the teaching of intelligent design as an alternative to evolution. Opening arguments are set for today in Harrisburg, Pennsylvania District Court.
