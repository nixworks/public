### [2009-12-14](/news/2009/12/14/index.md)

##### Bangkok
#  A Bangkok court extends the detention of the crew of the seized Ilyushin Il-76, a cargo aircraft loaded with 35 tonnes of arms from North Korea, charging them with the illegal possession of weapons.
