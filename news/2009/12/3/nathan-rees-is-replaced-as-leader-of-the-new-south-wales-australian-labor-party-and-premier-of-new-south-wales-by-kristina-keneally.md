### [2009-12-3](/news/2009/12/3/index.md)

##### Nathan Rees
#  Nathan Rees is replaced as leader of the New South Wales Australian Labor Party and Premier of New South Wales by Kristina Keneally. 

Kristina Keneally will become the first female Premier of NSW after Nathan Rees resigned as the leader.
