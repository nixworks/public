### [2009-12-18](/news/2009/12/18/index.md)

##### Castlegregory
#  Irish priest Father Sen Sheehy withdraws from work in his parish of Castlegregory over a controversy which followed his shaking the hand of a convicted sex offender in court days earlier. Bishop of Kerry William Murphy disassociates himself from Sheehy and his actions.
