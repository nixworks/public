### [2009-12-18](/news/2009/12/18/index.md)

##### Iran
#  The Iraqi government demands the withdrawal of Iranian soldiers that it claims seized an oil well in Fakkah, in the Maysan Governorate in southern Iraq. 

Baghdad says Iranian troops crossed border to seize disputed southern oil well.
