##### Arbeit macht frei
#  The "Arbeit macht frei" sign is stolen from Auschwitz concentration camp. 

Thieves have stolen the infamous &#39;Arbeit macht frei&#39; sign at the entrance to the Nazi concentration camp in Auschwitz.
