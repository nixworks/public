### [2009-12-24](/news/2009/12/24/index.md)

##### Pope Benedict XVI
#  Pope Benedict XVI is knocked down by a woman during a procession before the Christmas Eve Mass at St. Peter's Basilica in the Vatican City. He is uninjured, but Roger Cardinal Etchegaray suffers a hip fracture.
