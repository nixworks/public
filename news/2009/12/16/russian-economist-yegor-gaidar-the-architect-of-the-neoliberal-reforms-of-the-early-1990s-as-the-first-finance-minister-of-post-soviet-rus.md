### [2009-12-16](/news/2009/12/16/index.md)

##### Yegor Gaidar
#  Russian economist Yegor Gaidar, the architect of the neoliberal reforms of the early 1990s as the first finance minister of post-Soviet Russia, dies unexpectedly in his home. 

Yegor T. Gaidar oversaw the transition from Communism to capitalism as the first finance minister of post-Soviet Russia.
