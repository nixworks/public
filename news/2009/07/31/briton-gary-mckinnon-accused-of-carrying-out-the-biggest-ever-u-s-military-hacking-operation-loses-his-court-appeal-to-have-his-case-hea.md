### [2009-07-31](/news/2009/07/31/index.md)

##### British
#  Briton Gary McKinnon, accused of carrying out the biggest ever U.S. military hacking operation, loses his court appeal to have his case heard in Britain, and faces extradition to the United States. 

The British High Court has ruled against a last-ditch bid by Gary McKinnon to avoid extradition to the US where he faces trial for hacking into military networks.
