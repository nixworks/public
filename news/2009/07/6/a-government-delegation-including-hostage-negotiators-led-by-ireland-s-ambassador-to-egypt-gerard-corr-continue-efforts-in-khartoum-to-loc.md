### [2009-07-6](/news/2009/07/6/index.md)

##### Egypt
#  A Government delegation including hostage negotiators, led by Ireland's Ambassador to Egypt Gerard Corr continue efforts in Khartoum to locate two Irish and Ugandan aid workers who were kidnapped in Sudan. 

A team of diplomats and negotiators are seeking to locate an Irish aid worker and her Ugandan colleague who have been kidnapped in Darfur.
