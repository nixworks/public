##### Iran
#  Iran says it will hold the US responsible for any Israeli attack against their country, in response to US Vice President Joe Biden's claim that the US will not interfere in an Israeli attack on Iranian nuclear facilities. 

US vice-president Joe Biden says Iran nuclear issue for Israel to decide how to fix.
