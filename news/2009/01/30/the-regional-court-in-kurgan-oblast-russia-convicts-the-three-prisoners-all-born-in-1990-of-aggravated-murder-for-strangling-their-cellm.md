### [2009-01-30](/news/2009/01/30/index.md)

##### Kurgan Oblast
#  The regional Court in Kurgan Oblast, Russia convicts the three prisoners, all born in 1990, of aggravated murder for strangling their cellmate, who had asked them to end his life in this fashion.
