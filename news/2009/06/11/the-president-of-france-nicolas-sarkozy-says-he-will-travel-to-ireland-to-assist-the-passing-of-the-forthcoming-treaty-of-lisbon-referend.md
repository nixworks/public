### [2009-06-11](/news/2009/06/11/index.md)

##### President of France
#  The President of France, Nicolas Sarkozy, says he will travel to Ireland to assist the passing of the forthcoming Treaty of Lisbon referendum at a joint news conference with the Chancellor of Germany, Angela Merkel, in Paris. 

French President Nicolas Sarkozy has said he would be prepared to travel to Ireland to help gather support for the Lisbon Treaty.
