### [2009-06-15](/news/2009/06/15/index.md)

##### European Union
#  The European Union announces it will help resettle detainees released from the Guantanamo Bay detention camp. 

European Union member states are ready to help resettle detainees freed from the U.S. detention center at Guantanamo Bay in Cuba, the EU said on Monday.
