### [2009-02-2](/news/2009/02/2/index.md)

##### Chairman
#  Chairman Mahmoud Abbas says he will not deliberate with any group that does not recognize the Palestine Liberation Organization. 

Palestinian president condemns PLO criticism as factions prepare to meet Egyptian mediators.
