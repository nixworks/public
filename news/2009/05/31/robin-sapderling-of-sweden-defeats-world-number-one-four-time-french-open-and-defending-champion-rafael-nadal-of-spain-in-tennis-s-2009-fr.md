#  Robin Soderling of Sweden defeats world-number-one, four-time French Open and defending champion Rafael Nadal of Spain in tennis's 2009 French Open. 

Four-time French Open champion Rafael Nadals reign as the king of clay has come to an end with defeat to Robin Soderling.
