### [2009-10-31](/news/2009/10/31/index.md)

##### Irish
#  A video of the kidnapped Irish priest Father Michael Sinnott is released by his captors in the Philippines. The kidnappers want a $2 million (EUR1.36 million) ransom. It is the first evidence that Sinnott is still alive since his abduction on 11 October. 

Kidnappers holding Fr Michael Sinnott hostage in the Philippines have released a video in which he says $2m must be paid for his release.
