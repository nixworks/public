### [2009-10-13](/news/2009/10/13/index.md)

##### Russia
#  A Russian court rejects a libel suit by Yevgeny Dzhugashvili, the grandson of Joseph Stalin, who claimed the "Novaya Gazeta" newspaper lied about Stalin personally signing death warrants. 

Josef Stalin&#039;s grandson has lost a bizarre legal case he hoped would rehabilitate the Soviet dictator&#039;s murderous reputation posthumously.
