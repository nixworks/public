##### Irish
#  Ambassadors from Ireland and Uganda, along with the Sudanese minister overseeing negotiations, visit Al-Fashir in a renewed effort to win the freedom of aid workers Sharon Cummins and Hilda Kawuki, kidnapped since 3 July. 

A diplomatic team, including Ireland&#39;s ambassador to Sudan, have renewed efforts to secure the release of the two GOAL workers kidnapped in Darfur.
