##### United States
#  Former United States President Bill Clinton arrives in Pyongyang, North Korea, and secures the freedom of two detained American journalists, Laura Ling and Euna Lee. 

Ex-US president is in Pyongyang in bid to negotiate release of jailed US reporters.
