##### England
#  English youth Michael Perham, aged 17 years, 5 months, becomes the youngest person to complete a solo circumnavigation of the world by sailboat, breaking the previous record by two months. 

A 17-year-old Briton became the youngest person to sail round the globe single-handed on Thursday after nine months at sea.
