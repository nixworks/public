##### Sayano-Shushenskaya hydroelectric power station
#  An accident at the Sayano-Shushenskaya hydroelectric power station in Russia kills seven and causes power shortages in Siberia. 

Eight people are dead and 54 still unaccounted for after an accident at a hydroelectric power station in Siberia.
