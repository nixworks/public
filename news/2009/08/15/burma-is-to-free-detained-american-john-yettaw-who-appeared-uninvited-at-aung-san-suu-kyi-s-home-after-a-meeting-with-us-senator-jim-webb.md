### [2009-08-15](/news/2009/08/15/index.md)

#  Burma is to free detained American John Yettaw, who appeared uninvited at Aung San Suu Kyi's home, after a meeting with US Senator Jim Webb. 

John Yettaw, the American who was jailed for breaching the house arrest of Aung San Suu Kyi, the Burmese democracy leader, is likely to be deported, it emerged today.
