### [2009-08-25](/news/2009/08/25/index.md)

##### Prime Minister
#  British Prime Minister Gordon Brown repeats that the British Government played no role in the release from prison of Lockerbie bomber Abdelbaset al-Megrahi, it being a matter for the Scottish Government. 

Gordon Brown, the prime minister broke his silence on the release of the Lockerbie bomber to say he &#034;had no role&#034; in the decision to release Abdelbaset Ali Mohmed al Megrahi.
