### [2009-08-25](/news/2009/08/25/index.md)

##### Narathiwat
#  42 people are injured after a car bomb explodes at a crowded restaurant with government officials in Narathiwat, southern Thailand. 

A car bomb has ripped through a restaurant packed with government officials, injuring at least 42 people.
