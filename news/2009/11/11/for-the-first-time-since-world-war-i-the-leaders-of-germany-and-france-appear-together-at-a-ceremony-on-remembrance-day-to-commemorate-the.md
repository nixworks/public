### [2009-11-11](/news/2009/11/11/index.md)

##### First World War
#  For the first time since World War I, the leaders of Germany and France appear together at a ceremony on Remembrance Day to commemorate the end of the conflict. 

Solemn services were held in Belgium, France, Britain, Australia and other countries around the world to commemorate the end of World War I .
