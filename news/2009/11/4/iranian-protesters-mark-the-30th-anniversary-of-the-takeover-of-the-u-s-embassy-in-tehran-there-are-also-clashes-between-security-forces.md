### [2009-11-4](/news/2009/11/4/index.md)

#  Iranian protesters mark the 30th anniversary of the takeover of the U.S. embassy in Tehran. There are also clashes between security forces and anti-government demonstrators. 

Opposition protesters dispersed with batons and tear gas as Iran marks capture of US embassy.
