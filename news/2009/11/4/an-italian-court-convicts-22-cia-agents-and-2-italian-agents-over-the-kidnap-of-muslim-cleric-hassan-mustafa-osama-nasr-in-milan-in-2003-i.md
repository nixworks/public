### [2009-11-4](/news/2009/11/4/index.md)

##### Italy
#  An Italian court convicts 22 CIA agents and 2 Italian agents over the kidnap of Muslim cleric Hassan Mustafa Osama Nasr in Milan in 2003, in the first court case challenging the CIA's "extraordinary rendition" programme. 

Twenty-three US secret agents, accused of seizing imam, sentenced to jail in absentia.
