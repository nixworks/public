### [2009-11-8](/news/2009/11/8/index.md)

##### Sharm el-Sheikh
#  Speaking at Sharm el-Sheikh's Forum on China-Africa Cooperation, China's Premier Wen Jiabao, pledges $10 billion (GBP6 billion) loans to Africa over a three-year period, in order to increase Sino-african cooperation. 

Chinese has offered Africa $10bn in concessional loans over the next three years.
