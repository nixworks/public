### [2009-09-28](/news/2009/09/28/index.md)

##### G20
#  A police officer who served at the G20 demonstrations in London in April 2009 is to face a charge of assaulting a woman with a baton after becoming involved in a confrontation at a vigil for Ian Tomlinson. 

A police sergeant who allegedly struck a woman, Nicola Fisher, during G20 protests will be charged with assault, the Crown Prosecution Service (CPS) said.
