### [2009-09-27](/news/2009/09/27/index.md)

##### Germany
#  In Germany the Christian Democrats and the Liberals win the federal election and announce their intention to form a new government with Angela Merkel as Chancellor. The Social Democrats concede defeat. 

Angela Merkel appears to have swept to victory in the German elections, with exit polls showing a clear win for her coalition.
