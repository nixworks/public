### [2009-09-23](/news/2009/09/23/index.md)

##### Tanzania
#  A court in Tanzania sentences three men to death by hanging for killing a 14-year-old albino boy, Matatizo Dunia, to steal parts of his anatomy. It is the country's first conviction for this offence.
