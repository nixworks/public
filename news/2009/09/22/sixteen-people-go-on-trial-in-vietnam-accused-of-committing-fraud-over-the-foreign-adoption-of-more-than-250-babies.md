##### Vietnam
#  Sixteen people go on trial in Vietnam accused of committing fraud over the foreign adoption of more than 250 babies. 

Sixteen Vietnamese doctors, nurses and officials sold 266 babies for overseas adoptions, a court heard on Tuesday.
