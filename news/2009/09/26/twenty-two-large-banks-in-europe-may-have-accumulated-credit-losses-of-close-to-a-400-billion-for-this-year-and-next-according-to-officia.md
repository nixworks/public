#  Twenty-two large banks in Europe may have accumulated credit losses of close to EUR400 billion for this year and next, according to officials who have seen a draft of conclusions of "stress tests" conducted by European regulators. 

Europe’s 22 biggest banks may have accumulated about 400 billion euros in credit losses for 2009 and 2010, say officials who have seen the results of “stress tests” conducted by European regulators.
