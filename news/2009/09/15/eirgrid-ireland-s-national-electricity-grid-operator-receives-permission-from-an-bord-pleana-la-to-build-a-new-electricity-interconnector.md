##### EirGrid
#  EirGrid, Ireland's national electricity grid operator, receives permission from An Bord Pleanala to build a new electricity interconnector between Ireland and Wales. 

An Bord Pleanála has given EirGrid permission to build a new electricity interconnector between Ireland and Wales.
