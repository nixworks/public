##### J. R. R. Tolkien
#  New documents disclose that novelist J. R. R. Tolkien secretly trained as a spy for His Majesty's Government in the run up to World War II. 

The novelist JRR Tolkien secretly trained as a Government spy in the run up to the Second World War, new documents have disclosed.
