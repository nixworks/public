### [2009-03-1](/news/2009/03/1/index.md)

##### United States
#  The United States Coast Guard searches Florida's west coast for a fishing vessel carrying National Football League players Marquis Cooper and Corey Smith. 

The boat departed from a ramp in Clearwater Pass early Saturday morning with four people in board and was supposed to return sometimes after dark, but never did.
