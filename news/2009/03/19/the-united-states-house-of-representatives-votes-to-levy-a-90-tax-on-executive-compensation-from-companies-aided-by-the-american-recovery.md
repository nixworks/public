### [2009-03-19](/news/2009/03/19/index.md)

##### United States
#  The United States House of Representatives votes to levy a 90% tax on executive compensation from companies aided by the American Recovery and Reinvestment Act. 

Spurred by anger over A.I.G., the House voted 328 to 93 to tax bonuses paid by any company owing more than $5 billion in bailout money.
