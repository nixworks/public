### [2009-03-21](/news/2009/03/21/index.md)

##### Anna Bligh
#  Anna Bligh of the Labor Party is reelected Premier of Queensland, Australia. 

Liberal National Party leader Lawrence Springborg has conceded defeat in the Queensland State Election.
