### [1999-01-2](/news/1999/01/2/index.md)

##### Snowstorm
#  A brutal snowstorm smashes into the Midwestern United States, causing 14 inches (359 mm) of snow at Milwaukee, Wisconsin and 19 inches (487 mm) at Chicago, Illinois. In Chicago, temperatures plunge to -13F (-25C), and 68 deaths are reported.
