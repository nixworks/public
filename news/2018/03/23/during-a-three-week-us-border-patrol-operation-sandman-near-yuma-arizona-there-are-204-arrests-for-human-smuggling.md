##### US Border Patrol
# During a three-week US Border Patrol Operation Sandman near Yuma, Arizona, there are 204 arrests for human smuggling. 

During a three-week Border Patrol operation, special agents apprehended 204 suspects west of Yuma in the Imperial Sand Dunes. It took place between February and March. 175 of them were foreign nationals who illegally entered the United States and 29 of them were American citizens who were arrested for coordinating the smuggling. Several of the people tried running away from law enforcement...
