# Anti-fascist protesters disrupt a libertarian event at King's College London featuring speakers such as Yaron Brook and Carl Benjamin, reportedly causing injuries to security guards and property damage. The neo-conservative Henry Jackson Society protests, saying university campuses are becoming "intolerant to free speech." 

Protesters stormed a King&rsquo;s College London event featuring a controversial anti-feminist YouTube star, with the university accused of prioritising &ldquo;safe space&rdquo; policies over protecting students from violence.
