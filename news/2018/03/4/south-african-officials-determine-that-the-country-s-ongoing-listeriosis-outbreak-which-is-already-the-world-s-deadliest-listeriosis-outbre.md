### [2018-03-4](/news/2018/03/4/index.md)

##### 2017 South African listeriosis outbreak
# South African officials determine that the country's ongoing listeriosis outbreak, which is already the world's deadliest listeriosis outbreak, killing over 180 people and sickening 1000 more, was caused by contaminated sausage meat. The Health Ministry issues recalls of contaminated products.
