##### China-United States relations
# A senior Chinese diplomat says that China does not want a trade war with the United States after U.S. President Donald Trump announced a plan to put tariffs on steel and aluminium imports. 

China does not want a trade war with the United States but will defend its interests, a senior Chinese diplomat said on Sunday.
