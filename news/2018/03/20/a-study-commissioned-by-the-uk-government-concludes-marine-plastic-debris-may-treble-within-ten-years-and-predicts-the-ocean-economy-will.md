##### Marine debris
# A study commissioned by the UK government concludes marine plastic debris may treble within ten years, and predicts the "ocean economy" will double to US$3 trillion (GBP2 trillion) by 2030. 

But there are opportunities to cash in on the &quot;ocean economy&quot;, a major report for the UK government says.
