### [2018-03-3](/news/2018/03/3/index.md)

##### "Polish death camp" controversy
# Hours after the Polish so-called Holocaust Law about discussing of historical facts takes effect, the Polish League Against Defamation sues Argentinian newspaper "Pagina-12", which it accuses of suggesting Poland was complicit in the Holocaust. 

A Polish campaign group is suing an Argentinian newspaper it says breached a new law that makes it a criminal offense to suggest Poland was complicit in the Holocaust.
