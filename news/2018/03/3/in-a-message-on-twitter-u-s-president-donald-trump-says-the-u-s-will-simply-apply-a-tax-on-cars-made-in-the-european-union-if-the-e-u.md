##### Protectionism in the United States
# In a message on Twitter, U.S. President Donald Trump says the U.S. will "simply" apply a tax on cars made in the European Union if the E.U. "further" increase tariffs. 

In a Twitter post, President Trump threatened to hit car exports from the European Union with a retaliatory tax.
