### [2018-03-13](/news/2018/03/13/index.md)

##### Cabinet of Donald Trump
# U.S. President Donald Trump fires Rex Tillerson as United States Secretary of State, moving to replace him with CIA director Mike Pompeo. Trump selects CIA Deputy Director Gina Haspel to replace Pompeo as CIA director. 

The move brings major changes as the United States deals with delicate foreign policy efforts such as possible outreach to North Korea.
