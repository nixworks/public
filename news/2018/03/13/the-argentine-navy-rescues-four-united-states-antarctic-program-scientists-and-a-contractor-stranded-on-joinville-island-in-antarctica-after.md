### [2018-03-13](/news/2018/03/13/index.md)

##### Argentine Navy
# The Argentine Navy rescues four United States Antarctic Program scientists and a contractor stranded on Joinville Island in Antarctica after their icebreaker RV "Laurence M. Gould" encountered thick sea-ice. 

The scientists and a contractor were carrying out research on Joinville Island off Antarctica.
