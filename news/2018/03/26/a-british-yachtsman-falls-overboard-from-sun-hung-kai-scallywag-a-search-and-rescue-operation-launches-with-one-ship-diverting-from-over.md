##### 2017-18 Volvo Ocean Race
# A British yachtsman falls overboard from "Sun Hung Kai-Scallywag". A search and rescue operation launches, with one ship diverting from over 400 nautical miles away. 

John Fisher is missing after falling overboard off the tip of South America during a round-the-world race.
