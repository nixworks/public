##### 2018 Kemerovo fire
# Witnesses and Russia's Investigative Committee say the Winter Cherry complex's exits were blocked and there were no alarms during the disaster, which killed more than 60. 

Many of the 64 people killed in the leisure complex fire in Siberia were children.
