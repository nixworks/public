##### Harasta
# Evacuations of 7,500 people from defeated rebel-held town Harasta, Easter Ghouta begin. Rebels agreed with Syria and Russia for the displaced people to be exiled in Idlib. 

Thousands of fighters to be deported after deal with Russian and Syrian officials
