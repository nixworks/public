##### Environmental issues in Chile
# Outgoing Chilean President Michelle Bachelet signs a new law which creates nine marine reserves. The new legislation will increase the area of sea under state protection from 4.3% to 42.4%. 

President Michele Bachelet has signed a decree creating a new protectionist policy towards the sea.
