### [2018-03-17](/news/2018/03/17/index.md)

##### Russia-United Kingdom relations
#  In response to the UK's decision to expel 23 Russian diplomats following the poisoning of Sergei and Yulia Skripal, Russia expels 23 British diplomats and closes the Consulate-General of the United Kingdom in Saint Petersburg. 

It also closes the British Council in Russia - amid tensions over the nerve agent attack on an ex-spy.
