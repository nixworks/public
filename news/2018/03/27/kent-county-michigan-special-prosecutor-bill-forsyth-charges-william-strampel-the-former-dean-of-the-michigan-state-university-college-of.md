### [2018-03-27](/news/2018/03/27/index.md)

##### Kent County, Michigan
# Kent County, Michigan special prosecutor Bill Forsyth charges William Strampel, the former dean of the Michigan State University College of Osteopathic Medicine, in the United States District Court for the Western District of Michigan in East Lansing, Michigan, with misconduct in office, fourth-degree criminal sexual conduct, and two counts of willful neglect of duty in connection with the Larry Nassar scandal. As Nassar's boss, Strampel appears to have willfully ignored years of sexual misconduct involving Nassar, the sports medicine doctor who abused hundreds of student athletes and Olympic gymnasts under the subterfuge of being appropriate medical care. Among the evidence it is reported that there was the discovery on Strampel's computer of a pornographic video of Nassar performing a "so-called" treatment on a young female patient.
