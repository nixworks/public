##### Censorship in Spain
# A court issues an order shutting down a website that uses extracts from "Don Quixote" to recreate the book "Farina". "Farina", an investigation of drug trafficking, is the subject of a freedom of speech debate after a court halted sales ahead of a libel case. 

When Miguel de Cervantes wrote &ldquo;Don Quixote&rdquo; in the 17th century, he couldn&rsquo;t have imagined that the novel would be used as a weapon in a modern battle over freedom of speech in Spain.
