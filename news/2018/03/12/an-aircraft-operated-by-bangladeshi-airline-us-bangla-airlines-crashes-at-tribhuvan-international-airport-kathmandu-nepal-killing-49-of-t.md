### [2018-03-12](/news/2018/03/12/index.md)

##### US-Bangla Airlines Flight 211
# An aircraft operated by Bangladeshi airline US-Bangla Airlines crashes at Tribhuvan International Airport, Kathmandu, Nepal, killing 49 of the 71 people on board. 

There were about 67 people on the US-Bangla aircraft and there are casualties, officials say.
