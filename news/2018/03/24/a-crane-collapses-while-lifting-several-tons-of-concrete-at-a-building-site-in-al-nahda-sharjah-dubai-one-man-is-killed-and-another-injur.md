### [2018-03-24](/news/2018/03/24/index.md)

##### Al Nahda
# A crane collapses while lifting several tons of concrete at a building site in Al Nahda, Sharjah, Dubai. One man is killed and another injured. 

A team of rescue units, police patrols and ambulances arrived at the site in less than five minutes.
