##### Poland in the European Union
# Polish Foreign Minister Jacek Czaputowicz accuses the European Union of "double standards" and of suffering "a deficit of democracy". Poland and the European Commission are engaged in disputes over migrant quotas and proposed reform of Poland's judiciary. 

There is a &#39;deficit of democracy in the European Union&#39;, the foreign minister said.
