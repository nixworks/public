### [2018-03-21](/news/2018/03/21/index.md)

##### Censorship in Singapore
# The Singaporean Parliament approves the Public Order and Safety (Special Powers) Act, which makes it a crime to take pictures and relay information during terror attacks. 

The Public Order and Safety (Special Powers) Act aims to reduce the risk of security operations being compromised by unauthorised communications.
