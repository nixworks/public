### [2018-03-21](/news/2018/03/21/index.md)

##### Economy of the African Union
# The leaders of 44 African Union states sign an agreement in Kigali, Rwanda, to create the African Continental Free Trade Area (CFTA). 

It is hoped the deal could boost industry and make more than a billion people more prosperous.
