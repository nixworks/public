##### Valencia
# An overnight riot and fire at a police station in Valencia, Venezuela, leaves at least 68 people dead. Prosecutor General Tarek Saab says an investigation into what has happened would begin immediately. 

Rioting and a fire at a police station in Valencia, Carabobo State, leave 68 people dead.
