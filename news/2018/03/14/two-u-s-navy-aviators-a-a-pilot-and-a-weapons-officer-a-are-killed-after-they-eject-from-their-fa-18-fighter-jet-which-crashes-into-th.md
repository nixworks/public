# Two U.S. Navy aviators - a pilot and a weapons officer - are killed after they eject from their FA-18 fighter jet, which crashes into the sea off Key West, Florida. 

A U.S. Navy fighter jet crashed Wednesday off the coast of Key West, Florida, killing its two crew members.
