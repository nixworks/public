### [2018-03-14](/news/2018/03/14/index.md)

##### Economy of the United States
# Shortly after the announcement of the closure of Toys "R" Us stores in the United Kingdom, the company announced that they will close down all 800 stores in the United States, resulting in the loss of about 33,000 jobs.

The retailer informed employees of plans on Wednesday.
