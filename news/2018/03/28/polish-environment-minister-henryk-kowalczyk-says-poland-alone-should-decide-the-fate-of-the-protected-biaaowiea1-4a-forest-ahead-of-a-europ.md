##### Poland in the European Union
# Polish environment minister Henryk Kowalczyk says Poland alone should decide the fate of the protected Bialowieza Forest, ahead of a European Court of Justice ruling on whether additional logging allowed by the Polish government at the UNESCO world heritage site breaches European Union rules.
