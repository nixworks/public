### [2018-03-28](/news/2018/03/28/index.md)

##### Durban
# A partially-constructed building collapses onto a truck in Jacobs, Durban, South Africa, killing three people. Six more are hospitalised and search and rescue dogs are looking for further victims. 

Two people have died after being trapped under a building that collapsed on a truck in the Wentworth area, south of Durban, paramedic services have said.
