##### Search and rescue
# Search and rescue operations continue off the Californian coast for three children missing following a crash which killed five of their relatives. Their car fell into the sea on Monday. 

It&#39;s not clear if the children were in the SUV crash that left five other family members dead, but a search is on as part of the ongoing investigation.
