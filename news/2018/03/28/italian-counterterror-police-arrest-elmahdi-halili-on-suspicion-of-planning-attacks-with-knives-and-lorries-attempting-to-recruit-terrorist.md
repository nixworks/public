### [2018-03-28](/news/2018/03/28/index.md)

##### Terrorism in Italy
# Italian counterterror police arrest Elmahdi Halili on suspicion of planning attacks with knives and lorries, attempting to recruit terrorist attackers, and creating the first piece of ISIL literature in Italian. 

Anti-terrorism police carried out searches across Italy on Wednesday morning, arresting a man in Turin who is accused of plotting attacks for the violent Islamist group Isis.
