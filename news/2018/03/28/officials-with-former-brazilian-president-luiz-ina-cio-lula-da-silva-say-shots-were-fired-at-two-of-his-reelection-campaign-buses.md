### [2018-03-28](/news/2018/03/28/index.md)

##### Brazil
# Officials with former Brazilian President Luiz Inacio Lula da Silva say shots were fired at two of his reelection campaign buses. 

No one was hurt and the former president was not in either of the two buses, which were traveling in southern Brazil
