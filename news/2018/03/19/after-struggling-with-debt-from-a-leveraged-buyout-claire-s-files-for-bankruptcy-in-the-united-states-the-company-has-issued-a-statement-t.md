### [2018-03-19](/news/2018/03/19/index.md)

##### Economy of the United States
# After struggling with debt from a leveraged buyout, Claire's files for bankruptcy in the United States. The company has issued a statement that it expects its stores will remain operational while it restructures its debt. 

The hub for ear piercings at malls across America is the latest retailer to succumb to its massive pile of debt.
