### [2018-03-9](/news/2018/03/9/index.md)

##### Australia-North Korea relations
#  Australia Prime Minister Malcolm Turnbull urges US President Donald Trump to be cautious in North Korea talks. Furthermore, Turnbull stated that Australia must retain sanctions on North Korea. 

PM says an unprecedented meeting between Donald Trump and Kim Jong-un should be welcomed cautiously.
