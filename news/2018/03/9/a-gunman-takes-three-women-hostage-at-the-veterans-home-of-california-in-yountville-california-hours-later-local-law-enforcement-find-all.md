### [2018-03-9](/news/2018/03/9/index.md)

##### Crime in California
# A gunman takes three women hostage at the Veterans Home of California in Yountville, California. Hours later, local law enforcement find all four of them dead. 

The bodies were found at the home in Northern California hours after the gunman had fired at a deputy, the authorities said.
