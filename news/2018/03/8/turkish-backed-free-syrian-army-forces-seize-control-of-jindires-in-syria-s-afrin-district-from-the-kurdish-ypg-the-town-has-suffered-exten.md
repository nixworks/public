### [2018-03-8](/news/2018/03/8/index.md)

##### Turkish military operation in Afrin
# Turkish-backed Free Syrian Army forces seize control of Jindires in Syria's Afrin District from the Kurdish YPG. The town has suffered extensive destruction due to Turkish airstrikes, according to the Syrian Observatory for Human Rights.
