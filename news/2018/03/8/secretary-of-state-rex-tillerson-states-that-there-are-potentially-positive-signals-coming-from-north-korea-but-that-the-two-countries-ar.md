##### 2017-18 North Korea crisis
# Secretary of State Rex Tillerson states that there are "potentially positive signals" coming from North Korea, but that the two countries are "still a long way" from direct negotiations. 

Secretary of State Rex Tillerson cautioned, however, that the two countries are still &quot;a long way&quot; from direct negotiations about Pyongyang&#x27;s nuclear program.
