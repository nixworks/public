##### Neutron stars
# Astronomers discover a neutron star brought back to life. They observed IGR J17329-2731, a red giant star, "revive its dead companion star with a flash of X-rays", describing it as cosmic dawn. 

Astronomers discovered an unusual moment in the birth of a rare binary star system, made up of a red giant star and a neutron star.
