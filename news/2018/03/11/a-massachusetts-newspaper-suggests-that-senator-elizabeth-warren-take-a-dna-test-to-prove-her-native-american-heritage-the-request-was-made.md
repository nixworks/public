##### 115th United States Congress
# A Massachusetts newspaper suggests that Senator Elizabeth Warren take a DNA test to prove her Native American heritage. The request was made to put the issue at rest as it has been a heated political talking point. 

A Massachusetts newspaper is calling on Sen. Elizabeth Warren (D-Mass.) to take a DNA test to resolve once and for all if she has Native American ancestry, as she has claimed.
