# U.S. President Donald Trump's former chief strategist Steve Bannon is interviewed over two days this week as part of the investigation into alleged Russian meddling in the 2016 presidential election. 

Steve Bannon, a former top adviser to Donald Trump, reportedly spent 20 hours with investigators.
