##### 2018 Oaxaca earthquake
# A military helicopter surveying the damage over Jamiltepec crashes, killing 13 people on the ground, the only deaths related to the earthquake reported so far. Among the passengers are Governor of Oaxaca Alejandro Murat Hinojosa and Mexico's Secretary of the Interior Alfonso Navarrete Prida.
