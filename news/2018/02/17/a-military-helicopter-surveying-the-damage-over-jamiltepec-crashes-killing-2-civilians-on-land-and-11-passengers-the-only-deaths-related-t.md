### [2018-02-17](/news/2018/02/17/index.md)

##### 2018 Oaxaca earthquake
# A military helicopter surveying the damage over Jamiltepec crashes, killing 2 civilians on land and 11 passengers, the only deaths related to the earthquake reported so far. Among the surviving passengers are Oaxaca governor Alejandro Murat Hinojosa and Mexico's Secretary of the Interior Alfonso Navarrete Prida.
