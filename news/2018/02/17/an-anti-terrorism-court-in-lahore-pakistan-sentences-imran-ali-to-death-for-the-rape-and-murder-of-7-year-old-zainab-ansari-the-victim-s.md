##### Murder of Zainab Ansari
# An Anti-Terrorism Court in Lahore, Pakistan, sentences Imran Ali to death, for the rape and murder of 7-year-old Zainab Ansari. The victim's family demands Ali's hanging to be conducted in public. 

Eight-year-old victim&#039;s mother and uncle say the demand is just and in accordance with the teachings of Islam
