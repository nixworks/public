# Barnaby Joyce announces his resignation as Deputy Prime Minister and leader of the National Party, following scandals involving an affair with a staffer and a sexual harassment allegation. The party, a junior partner in the Coalition government, will vote for his successor on February 26. 

• Deputy PM announces he will formally resign at Monday’s party room meeting • Joyce says he has referred allegation of sexual harassment to police
