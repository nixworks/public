### [2018-02-6](/news/2018/02/6/index.md)

##### Politics of the Maldives
# Former President of the Maldives Mohamed Nasheed has called from exile for other countries to intervene in a political crisis engulfing the island nation. Nasheed asked India to help release prisoners and the United States to curb leaders' financial transactions. 

Mohamed Nasheed asks for India to help release political prisoners amid a worsening crisis.
