##### ozone layer
# Scientists warn that the ozone layer that protects people from the sun's ultraviolet radiation is recovering only over less populated regions where the most of ozone depletion occured. 

While the hole over Antarctica has been closing, the protective ozone is thinning at the lower latitudes, where the sunlight is stronger and billions of people live
