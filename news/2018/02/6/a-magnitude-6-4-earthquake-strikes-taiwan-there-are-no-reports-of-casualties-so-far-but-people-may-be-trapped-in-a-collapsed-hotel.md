# A Magnitude 6.4 earthquake strikes Taiwan. There are no reports of casualties so far, but people may be trapped in a collapsed hotel. 

Taiwan&#039;s official Central News Agency says the ground floor of the Marshal Hotel has caved in and people are trapped inside.
