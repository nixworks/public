### [2018-02-22](/news/2018/02/22/index.md)

##### National debt
# In exchange for some of its national debt being written off, the Seychelles designates two new marine protected areas in the Indian Ocean, covering about 210000 km2. It is the result of a financial deal, brokered in 2016 by The Nature Conservancy. 

A novel deal with donors including Leonardo DiCaprio turned public debt into conservation funding.
