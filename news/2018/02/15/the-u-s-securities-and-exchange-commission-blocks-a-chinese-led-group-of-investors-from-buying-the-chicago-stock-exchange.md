### [2018-02-15](/news/2018/02/15/index.md)

##### U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission
# The U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission blocks a Chinese-led group of investors from buying the Chicago Stock Exchange. 

The top U.S. market regulator said it was concerned about whether the deal would allow it to supervise the exchange properly.
