### [2018-02-15](/news/2018/02/15/index.md)

##### Mexican general election, 2018
# Maria de Jesus Patricio Martinez "Marichuy", the National Indigenous Congress's independent pre-candidate for President of Mexico, is involved in a road accident while campaigning in Baja California Sur. Marichuy is reported as injured while a passenger of the vehicle is dead. 

The first indigenous woman to run for the Mexican presidency was injured and at least one other person killed when a van in which she was traveling was involved in an accident in western Baja California state on Wednesday, campaign social media said.
