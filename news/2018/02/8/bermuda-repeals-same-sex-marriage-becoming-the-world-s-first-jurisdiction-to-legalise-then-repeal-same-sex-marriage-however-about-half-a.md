### [2018-02-8](/news/2018/02/8/index.md)

##### LGBT rights in Bermuda
# Bermuda repeals same-sex marriage, becoming the world's first jurisdiction to legalise, then repeal same-sex marriage. However, about half a dozen same-sex marriages that took place in Bermuda between the Supreme Court ruling in May 2017 and the repeal will continue to be recognised.
