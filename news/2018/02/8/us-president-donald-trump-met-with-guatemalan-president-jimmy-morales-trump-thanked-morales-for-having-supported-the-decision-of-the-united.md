### [2018-02-8](/news/2018/02/8/index.md)

##### United States-Guatemala relations
# US President Donald Trump met with Guatemalan President Jimmy Morales. Trump thanked Morales for having supported the decision of the United States on the transfer of the Embassy of Tel Aviv to Jerusalem. They dealt with various issues about the crisis in Venezuela, drug trafficking and the UN anti-corruption commission. 

At Washington meeting, US president praises Jimmy Morales for following the US example on Israel
