##### Crime in Toronto
# The remains of six people found on a property in East York are linked to alleged serial killer Bruce McArthur. This brings the total body count to eleven. 

McArthur, 66, a self-employed landscaper, was charged last month with five counts of first-degree murder in the deaths of missing men from the city&#039;s Gay Village.
