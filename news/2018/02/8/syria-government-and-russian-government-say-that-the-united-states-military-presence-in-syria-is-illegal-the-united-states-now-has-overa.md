### [2018-02-8](/news/2018/02/8/index.md)

##### Syrian Civil War
# Syria government and Russian government say that the United States' military presence in Syria is "illegal". The United States now has overall control of about a quarter of the Syrian territory. 

The overnight clash came after pro-government forces attacked a U.S.-backed base in the eastern Syrian desert, prompting the U.S. to retaliate in &ldquo;self defense.&rdquo;
