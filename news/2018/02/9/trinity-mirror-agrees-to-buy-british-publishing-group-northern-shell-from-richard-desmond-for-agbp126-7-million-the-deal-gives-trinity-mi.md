##### Trinity Mirror
# Trinity Mirror agrees to buy British publishing group Northern & Shell from Richard Desmond for GBP126.7 million. The deal gives Trinity Mirror ownership of the "Daily Express" and "Daily Star" newspapers. 

Richard Desmond agrees to sell newspapers along with celebrity magazine OK!
