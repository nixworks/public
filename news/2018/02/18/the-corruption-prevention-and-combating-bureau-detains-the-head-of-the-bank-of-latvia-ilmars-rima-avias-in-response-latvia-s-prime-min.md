##### Corruption in Latvia
# The Corruption Prevention and Combating Bureau detains the head of the Bank of Latvia, Ilmars Rimsevics. In response, Latvia's Prime Minister Maris Kucinskis calls an emergency cabinet meeting but added there was no apparent threat to the Latvian financial system. 

Ilmars Rimsevics has been detained after the anti-corruption agency raided his home and office.
