### [2018-02-20](/news/2018/02/20/index.md)

##### Thailand
# A court in Thailand awards a Japanese man paternity rights over 13 of his children from Thai surrogate mothers. The man had rented an apartment in Bangkok and run it as a baby factory. 

The Japanese man can now pursue custody of 13 children he fathered through Thai surrogate mothers.
