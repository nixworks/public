##### Politics of Sri Lanka
# In provisional local elections results with most votes in, the opposition SLPP (Rajapaksa) is projected to win 44.05% of the vote, the ruling coalition parties UNP (Wickremesinghe) and SLFP (Sirisena) gather 31.65% and 9.52% respectively. Rosy Senanayake (UNP) is set to become the first female mayor of the capital Colombo. 

Party backed by former President Mahinda Rajapaksa set to score landslide victory in key local elections.
