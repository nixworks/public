##### Disappearance of Maelys de Araujo
# The remains of Maelys de Araujo, a girl who disappeared in the early hours of 27 August 2017 at a wedding in France, are found after suspect Nordahl Lelandais guides investigators to the site. 

French police have found the remains of an eight-year-old French girl who vanished from a wedding in the Alps last August.
