##### Disappearance of Maelys de Araujo
# The remains of Maelys de Araujo, a nine-year-old girl who disappeared from a wedding in France in August 2017, are found after suspect Nordahl Lelandais guides investigators to the site. 

French police have found the remains of an eight-year-old French girl who vanished from a wedding in the Alps last August.
