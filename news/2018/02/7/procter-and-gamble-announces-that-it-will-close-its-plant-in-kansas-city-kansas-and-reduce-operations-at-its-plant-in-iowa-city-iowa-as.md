##### Procter and Gamble
# Procter and Gamble announces that it will close its plant in Kansas City, Kansas, and reduce operations at its plant in Iowa City, Iowa, as the company plans to shift production to factories in Tablers Station, West Virginia, and Cincinnati, Ohio, by 2020. 

“My grandfather started down there in 1935,” said the head of the plant’s union, who has logged 28 years there himself.
