# Philippine President Rodrigo Duterte calls for female communist rebels to be shot in the genitals. Duterte's comments are described as "a funny joke" by his spokesman, while drawing heavy criticism from others. 

Rights groups slam Philippine president for the latest in a series of derogatory and demeaning comments about women.
