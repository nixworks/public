### [2018-02-12](/news/2018/02/12/index.md)

##### Ukrainian crisis
# Mikheil Saakashvili, leader of the opposition Movement of New Forces party who've been stripped of his Ukrainian citizenship by Ukrainian president Petro Poroshenko on 26 July 2017, is detained by the Ministry of Internal Affairs in Kiev and deported from Ukraine to Poland. 

Last year Georgia’s former President Mikheil Saakashvili, who is well known for his extravagant behavior, warned that Ukraine’s President Petro Poroshenko wanted to kidnap him. Well, it might just have happened…
