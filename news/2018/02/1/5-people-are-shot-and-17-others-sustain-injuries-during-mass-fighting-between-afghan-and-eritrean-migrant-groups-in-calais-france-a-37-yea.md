##### 2018 Calais migrant violence
# 5 people are shot and 17 others sustain injuries during mass fighting between Afghan and Eritrean migrant groups in Calais, France. A 37-year old Afghan man is suspected of having critically wounded four Eritrean teenagers at a queue for food handouts. 

An Afghan is suspected of shooting at a queue for food handouts, critically injuring four Eritreans.
