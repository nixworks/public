##### Kivu conflict
# The death toll from clashes between militias in the Rutshuru Territory of the North Kivu province, DR Congo, rises to 23 (16 civilians, 7 militiamen). The renewed violence has been ongoing since 25 February 2018. 

We have recorded the death of 16 civilians and seven militiamen since Sunday, Francois Bakundakabo, an aide to the provincial governor, told AFP.
