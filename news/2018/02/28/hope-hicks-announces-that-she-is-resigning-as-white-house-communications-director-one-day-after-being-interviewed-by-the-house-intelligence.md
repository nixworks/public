##### Russian interference in the 2016 United States elections
# Hope Hicks announces that she is resigning as White House Communications Director, one day after being interviewed by the House Intelligence Committee. 

The resignation comes one day after Hicks testified before the House intelligence committee for nine hours as part of the Russia investigation
