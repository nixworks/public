### [2018-02-28](/news/2018/02/28/index.md)

##### Kivu conflict
# The death toll from clashes between militias in the Rutshuru Territory of the North Kivu province, DR Congo, rises to 23 people. The renewed violence has been ongoing since February 25. 

We have recorded the death of 16 civilians and seven militiamen since Sunday, Francois Bakundakabo, an aide to the provincial governor, told AFP.
