##### Terrorism in Pakistan
# A suicide bombing, revindicated in an email to journalists by the Pakistani Taliban, kills at least 11 soldiers and injures 13 at an army unit camp's sports area in the Kabal area of the Swat Valley, in Northwestern Pakistan, according to Pakistan Army figures. 

A suicide bomber killed at least 11 soldiers and injured 13 at an army camp in northwestern Pakistan on Saturday, the military said.
