### [2018-01-28](/news/2018/01/28/index.md)

# Voters in Finland go to the polls to elect the President of Finland for term 2018-2024. 

Sunday 28 January is election day in Finland. Citizens of the country will cast their ballots for the next Finnish President, who will serve from 1 March 2018 to 2024.
