# The bodies of 10 Syrian men are found after a snowstorm on the Lebanon-Syria border. The Lebanese Army says they tried to enter Lebanon illegally. Two presumed people smugglers are arrested. 

The bodies of nine people were found Friday along a snow-covered smuggling route near the Masnaa border crossing between Lebanon and Syria, a Lebanese Army statement said.
