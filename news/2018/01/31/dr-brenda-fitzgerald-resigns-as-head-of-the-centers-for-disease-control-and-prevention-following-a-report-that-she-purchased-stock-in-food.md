# Dr. Brenda Fitzgerald resigns as head of the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention following a report that she purchased stock in food, health insurance, and tobacco companies shortly after taking up her federal government position. 

Fitzgerald owned stocks in tobacco companies despite being in charge of smoking cessation efforts.
