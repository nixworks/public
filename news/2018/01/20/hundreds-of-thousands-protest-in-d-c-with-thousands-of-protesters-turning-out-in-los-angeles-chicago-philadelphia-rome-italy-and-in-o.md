# Hundreds of thousands protest in D.C., with thousands of protesters turning out in Los Angeles, Chicago, Philadelphia, Rome, Italy, and in other cities throughout the United States and the world, in support of women's rights. 

Thousands of protesters gathered at rallies on Saturday across the country and around the world. The #MeToo moment has galvanized the movement in the last year.
