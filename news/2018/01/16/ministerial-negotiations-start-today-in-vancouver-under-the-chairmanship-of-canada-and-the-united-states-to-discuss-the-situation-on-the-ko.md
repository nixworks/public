### [2018-01-16](/news/2018/01/16/index.md)

# Ministerial negotiations start today in Vancouver under the chairmanship of Canada and the United States, to discuss the situation on the Korean Peninsula. According to Russian Foreign Minister Sergey Lavrov, the negotiations are "void of representativeness and a legal value" and so are not effective. A Chinese Foreign Ministry spokesperson approves of the statements. 

Russian Foreign Minister Sergey Lavrov said earlier that Russia considers the ministerial meeting in Vancouver on North Korea harmful
