### [2018-01-16](/news/2018/01/16/index.md)

# A cable bridge under construction in Colombia collapses, killing 10 construction workers. The victims fell more than 900 feet in the accident. 

A 1,500-foot-long structure over a gorge in the Andes threw the workers about 900 feet to their deaths.
