### [2018-01-14](/news/2018/01/14/index.md)

# The U.S.-led coalition confrmed reports that it is to form a new 30,000-strong Syrian Border Security Force (BSF). Half of the forces will be Syrian Democratic Forces veterans. Another 15,000 will be recruited and trained in the near future.
