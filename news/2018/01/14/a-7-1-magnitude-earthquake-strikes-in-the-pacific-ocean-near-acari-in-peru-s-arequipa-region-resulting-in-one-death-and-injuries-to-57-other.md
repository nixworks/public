# A 7.1 magnitude earthquake strikes in the Pacific Ocean near Acar in Peru's Arequipa Region resulting in one death and injuries to 57 others. Tsunami warnings are issued for coastal Peru and Chile. 

The U.S. Geological survey reported 1 known death and 57 known injuries due to the magnitude 7.1 quake in Peru that happened early Sunday morning.
