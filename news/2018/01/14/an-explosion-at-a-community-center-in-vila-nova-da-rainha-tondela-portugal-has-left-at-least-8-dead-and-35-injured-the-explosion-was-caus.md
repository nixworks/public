# An explosion at a community center in Vila Nova da Rainha, Tondela, Portugal has left at least 8 dead and 35 injured. The explosion was caused by a heat boiler exploding. 

An explosion started the blaze while the centre at Vila Nova da Rainha hosted a card tournament.
