#  U.S. President Donald Trump threatens nuclear war against North Korea, boasting that the size of his missile launch button on his desk is larger and more powerful than Kim Jong-un's. 

The president’s tweet that he has a “much bigger” arsenal of nuclear weapons was met with dread and humor, of both the gallows and locker-room varieties.
