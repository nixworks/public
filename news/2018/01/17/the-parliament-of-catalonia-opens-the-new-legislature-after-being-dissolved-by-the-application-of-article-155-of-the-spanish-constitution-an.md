### [2018-01-17](/news/2018/01/17/index.md)

# The Parliament of Catalonia opens the new legislature after being dissolved by the application of Article 155 of the Spanish constitution and the direct rule and the call for snap elections. Roger Torrent, separatist from ERC, is named speaker of the Chamber. 

The choice of Roger Torrent is the first step to reinstating local government.
