### [2018-01-17](/news/2018/01/17/index.md)

# Donald Trump tweets a link to the Republican National Committee's "Fake News Awards", won by ten stories and tweets he considers false. CNN is the night's big winner, with four. The link also honors ten ways the President "has been getting results" thus far. 

The president highlighted negative coverage he said showed bias.
