# The Bayeux Tapestry, which depicts the Norman conquest of England, is to be displayed in the United Kingdom for the first time after French President Emmanuel Macron agreed to loan it out for the first time in 950 years. 

President Macron is understood to have agreed to let it leave France for the first time in 950 years.
