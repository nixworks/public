### [2018-01-27](/news/2018/01/27/index.md)

# The U.S. will pay Boeing nearly $24 million to replace two of the five 27-year-old chiller units on Air Force One, customized to carry 70 cubic feet of refrigerated storage for about 3,000 meals. 

The improvement is just the latest example of the bespoke requirements for the presidential jets.
