### [2018-01-15](/news/2018/01/15/index.md)

# Mohamed Anwar al-Sadat, the nephew of the assassinated Egyptian president Mohamed Anwar al-Sadat, announces that he will not run in March's presidential election, blaming an environment of fear surrounding the vote. 

Mohamed Anwar al-Sadat says he doubts there will be genuine competition and indicates he may wait until 2022
