### [2018-01-22](/news/2018/01/22/index.md)

# George Weah takes office as President of Liberia, Jewel Taylor as Vice President. It is the first peaceful transition between two Liberian presidents for 74 years. 

Mr. Weah was inaugurated to the cheers of thousands at a stadium. He faces big challenges lifting Liberia out of poverty.
