### [2018-01-30](/news/2018/01/30/index.md)

# President of Russia Vladimir Putin jokes that he feels "offended" not being included on a list of Russian oligarchs that was released by the United States Department of the Treasury on January 29. He adds that the list is "indisputably an unfriendly act." 

President Vladimir Putin reportedly joked that his exclusion from a U.S. Treasury Department list of Russian oligarchs was “offensive.”
