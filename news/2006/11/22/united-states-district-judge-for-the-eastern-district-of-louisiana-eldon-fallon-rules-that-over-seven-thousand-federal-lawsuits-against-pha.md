### [2006-11-22](/news/2006/11/22/index.md)

##### Federal District Judge
#  United States district judge for the Eastern District of Louisiana Eldon Fallon rules that over seven thousand federal lawsuits against pharmaceutical company Merck claiming that its drug Vioxx caused heart problems could not be combined into one class action suit.
