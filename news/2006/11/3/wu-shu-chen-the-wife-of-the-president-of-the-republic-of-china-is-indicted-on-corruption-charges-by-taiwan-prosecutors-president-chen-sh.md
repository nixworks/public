### [2006-11-3](/news/2006/11/3/index.md)

##### Wu Shu-chen
#  Wu Shu-chen, the wife of the President of the Republic of China, is indicted on corruption charges by Taiwan prosecutors. President Chen Shui-bian is not indicted due to presidential immunity, but prosecutors say he will be charged once he is no longer president.
