### [2006-08-17](/news/2006/08/17/index.md)

##### U.S. federal court
#  US District judge Anna Diggs Taylor orders the NSA warrantless surveillance program be shut down as unconstitutional. 

A federal judge ruled Thursday that the government&#8217;s warrantless wiretapping program is unconstitutional and ordered an immediate halt to it. The White House said it could not disagree more with the ruling.
