##### EuroMillions
#  Queues build up at vendors as the EuroMillions lottery offers a jackpot of EUR180 million after 11 successive rollovers (statistically expected once in 25 years). Some British vendors report a 1200% increase in sales. EuroMillions tickets are sold in Austria, Belgium. France, Ireland, Luxembourg, Portugal, Spain, Switzerland and the United Kingdom. An Irish woman won EUR115,436,126 last July.
