### [2006-02-9](/news/2006/02/9/index.md)

##### Shiite
#  A suicide bombing occurs during a Shiite Muslim procession in Hangu, Pakistan, resulting in riots during the Muslim branch's most important holiday, Ashura. At least 27 people were killed and dozens injured in the result violence.
