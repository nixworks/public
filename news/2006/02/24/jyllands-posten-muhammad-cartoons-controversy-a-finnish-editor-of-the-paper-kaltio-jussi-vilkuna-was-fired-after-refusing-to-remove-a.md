##### Jyllands-Posten Muhammad cartoons controversy
#  Jyllands-Posten Muhammad cartoons controversy: A Finnish editor of the paper "Kaltio", Jussi Vilkuna, was fired after refusing to remove a Muhammed-cartoon on the online version of the paper. This cartoon featured a westerner in the grips of Muhammad (who was masked), and Finnish politicians burning Danish flags.
