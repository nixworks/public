### [2006-03-19](/news/2006/03/19/index.md)

##### Iraqi Prime Minister
#  Former Prime Minister of Iraq Iyad Allawi says that he believes that Iraq is engaged in a civil war, although the country has not passed "the point of no return." British and American officials dispute calling the conflict a civil war.
