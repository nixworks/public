### [2006-03-2](/news/2006/03/2/index.md)

##### George W. Bush
#  President George W. Bush, on his first visit to India, issues a joint statement with Indian Prime Minister on their growing strategic partnership, emphasising their agreement on civil nuclear cooperation. An estimated crowd of 100,000 people protest against Bush in Delhi.
