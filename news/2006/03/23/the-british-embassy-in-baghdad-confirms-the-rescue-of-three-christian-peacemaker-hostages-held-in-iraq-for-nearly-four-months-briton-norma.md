### [2006-03-23](/news/2006/03/23/index.md)

##### Baghdad
#  The British Embassy in Baghdad confirms the rescue of three Christian Peacemaker hostages held in Iraq for nearly four months; Briton Norman Kember and Canadians Harmeet Singh Sooden and James Loney. They were freed during a British led multinational military operation involving American, British, Canadian and Iraqi forces.
