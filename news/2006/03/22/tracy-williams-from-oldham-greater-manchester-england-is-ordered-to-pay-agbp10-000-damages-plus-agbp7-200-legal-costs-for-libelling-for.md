### [2006-03-22](/news/2006/03/22/index.md)

##### Tracy Williams
#  Tracy Williams from Oldham, Greater Manchester, England, is ordered to pay GBP10,000 damages, plus GBP7,200 legal costs for libelling former parliamentary candidate Michael Keith Smith in a Yahoo chat room and in her blog, making history in respect of legal actions involving the Internet. She had accused Smith of being a sex offender and a racist bigot. Williams did not file a defence to the libel writ.
