### [2006-06-22](/news/2006/06/22/index.md)

##### International Committee of the Red Cross
#  The International Committee of the Red Cross admits the Israeli Magen David Adom and the Palestine Red Crescent Society. The resolution, ending 58 years of struggle, was passed by a 237-54 vote, over Muslim objections.
