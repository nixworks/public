#  War in Afghanistan: Six Canadian soldiers are wounded following two separate attacks in the southern provinces of Afghanistan. The six casualties are the first coalition casualties since Operation Mountain Thrust began on June 12, 2006. 

Two separate attacks, within hours of each other, have wounded six Canadian soldiers in Afghanistan
