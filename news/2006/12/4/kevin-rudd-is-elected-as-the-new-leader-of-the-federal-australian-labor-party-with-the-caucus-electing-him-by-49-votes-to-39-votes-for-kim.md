### [2006-12-4](/news/2006/12/4/index.md)

##### Kevin Rudd
#  Kevin Rudd is elected as the new leader of the Federal Australian Labor Party with the caucus electing him by 49 votes to 39 votes for Kim Beazley. Julia Gillard is the new deputy leader.
