### [2006-12-29](/news/2006/12/29/index.md)

##### Foreign Minister
#  Kazakh Foreign Minister Kassym-Jomart Tokayev says that in the event of a war in Turkmenistan over succession to Turkmenbashi Saparmurat Niyazov, "Kazakhstan is not going to get involved."
