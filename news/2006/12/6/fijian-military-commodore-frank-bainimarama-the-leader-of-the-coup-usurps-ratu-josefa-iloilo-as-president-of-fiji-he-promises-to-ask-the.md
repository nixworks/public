### [2006-12-6](/news/2006/12/6/index.md)

##### Voreqe Bainimarama
#  Fijian military Commodore Frank Bainimarama, the leader of the coup, usurps Ratu Josefa Iloilo as President of Fiji. He promises to ask the Great Council of Chiefs to reappoint Iloilo to the office in one week's time.
