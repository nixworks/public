### [2006-12-6](/news/2006/12/6/index.md)

##### Philippe Douste-Blazy
#  Philippe Douste-Blazy, the Foreign Minister of France, says that the United Nations Security Council will impose sanctions on Iran for its nuclear program. However, the five permanent members of the Security Council as well as Germany have failed to agree on what the sanctions should be.
