### [2006-12-8](/news/2006/12/8/index.md)

##### Bushfire
#  Bushfires in the Australian state of Victoria threaten at least six townships in the Gippsland region with extreme weather conditions over the weekend expected to increase the danger. The Victorian Department of Education closes 24 schools due to threat of fire.
