### [2006-01-5](/news/2006/01/5/index.md)

##### Mecca
#  At least 76 people have died following the collapse of a five-story hotel in Mecca. The death toll is expected to rise. Most of the dead are foreign Muslim pilgrims who had made their way there for the Hajj.
