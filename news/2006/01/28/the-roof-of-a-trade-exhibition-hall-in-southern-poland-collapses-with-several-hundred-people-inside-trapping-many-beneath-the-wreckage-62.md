### [2006-01-28](/news/2006/01/28/index.md)

##### The roof of a trade-exhibition hall
#  The roof of a trade-exhibition hall in southern Poland collapses with several hundred people inside, trapping many beneath the wreckage, 62 people are killed and over 160 injured. Poland declares a day of national mourning.
