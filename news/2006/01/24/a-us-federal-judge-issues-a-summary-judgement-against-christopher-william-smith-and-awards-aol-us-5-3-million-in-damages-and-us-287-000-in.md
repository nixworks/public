### [2006-01-24](/news/2006/01/24/index.md)

##### Federal judge
#  A US federal judge issues a summary judgement against Christopher William Smith and awards AOL US$5.3 million in damages and US$287,000 in legal fees, after Smith refused to participate in the lawsuit filed against him by AOL under the CAN-SPAM Act of 2003. Smith is accused of sending millions of spam e-mails to AOL customers. ([http:--www.seattlepi.com-business-1700AP_Online_Pharmacy_Spam.html "The Seattle Post-Intelligencer"])
