### [2006-04-10](/news/2006/04/10/index.md)

##### Matthias Platzeck
#  Matthias Platzeck resigns from the Chair of Germany's Social Democratic Party (SPD) after suffering a major hearing loss in March 2006 because of occupational stress. The next SPD chairman will be Kurt Beck (Minister President of Rhineland-Palatinate).
