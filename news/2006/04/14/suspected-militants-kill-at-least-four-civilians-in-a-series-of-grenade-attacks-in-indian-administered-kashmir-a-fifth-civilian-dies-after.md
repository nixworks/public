### [2006-04-14](/news/2006/04/14/index.md)

##### Terrorism in Kashmir
#  Suspected militants kill at least four civilians in a series of grenade attacks in Indian-administered Kashmir. A fifth civilian dies after police opens fire on the suspected militants. At least 17 others are injured.
