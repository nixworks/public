### [2006-04-28](/news/2006/04/28/index.md)

##### Congress
#  The Congress of Mexico passes a bill which, if signed by the President, will decriminalize possession of small amounts of marijuana, cocaine, heroin, opium, LSD, hallucinogenic mushrooms, amphetamines, and peyote.
