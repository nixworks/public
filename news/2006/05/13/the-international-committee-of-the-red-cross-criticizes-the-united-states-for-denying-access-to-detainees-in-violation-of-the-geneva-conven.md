### [2006-05-13](/news/2006/05/13/index.md)

##### International Committee of the Red Cross
#  The International Committee of the Red Cross criticizes the United States for denying access to detainees in violation of the Geneva Convention. The US admits holding detainees secretly but claims they do not fall under that convention.
