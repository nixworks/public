### [2006-05-14](/news/2006/05/14/index.md)

##### Ahmat Mahamat Bachir
#  Ahmat Mahamat Bachir, the president of the Chadian Independent National Election Commission, announces that incumbent President of Chad Idriss Dby won the 2006 Chadian presidential election held on May 3 with 77.5% of the vote. The official turnout was 61%, though international observers estimated turnout at 4&ndash;10%.
