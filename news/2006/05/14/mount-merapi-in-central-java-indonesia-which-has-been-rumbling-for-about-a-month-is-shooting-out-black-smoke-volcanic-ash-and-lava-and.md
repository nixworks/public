### [2006-05-14](/news/2006/05/14/index.md)

##### Mount Merapi
#  Mount Merapi in Central Java, Indonesia, which has been rumbling for about a month, is shooting out black smoke, volcanic ash and lava, and a volcanic eruption appears to be imminent. Mandatory evacuations are under way.
