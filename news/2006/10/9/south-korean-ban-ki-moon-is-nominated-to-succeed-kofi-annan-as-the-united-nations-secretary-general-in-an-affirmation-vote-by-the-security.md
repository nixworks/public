### [2006-10-9](/news/2006/10/9/index.md)

##### South Korea
#  South Korean Ban Ki-moon is nominated to succeed Kofi Annan as the United Nations Secretary-General in an affirmation vote by the Security Council. A confirmation vote by the General Assembly is expected within the next fortnight.
