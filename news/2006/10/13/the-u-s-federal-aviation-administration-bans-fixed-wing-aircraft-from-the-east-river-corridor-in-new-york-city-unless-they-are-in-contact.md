### [2006-10-13](/news/2006/10/13/index.md)

##### U.S.
#  The U.S. Federal Aviation Administration bans fixed-wing aircraft from the East River corridor in New York City unless they are in contact with air traffic control. The change follows a crash of a plane into an apartment building earlier in the week.
