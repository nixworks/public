### [2006-10-30](/news/2006/10/30/index.md)

##### Chenagai airstrike
#  Chenagai airstrike: Pakistani helicopter gunships fired missiles and destroyed an al-Qaeda-linked training facility and killed 80 suspected terrorists in a northwestern tribal area near the Afghan border, in a madrassa near the town of Khar.
