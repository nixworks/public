### [2006-10-12](/news/2006/10/12/index.md)

##### 2005 election funding
#  The New Zealand Auditor-General's report into 2005 election funding is released. NZ$1.17 million dollars was unlawfully spent during the election by seven parties, more than half of it by Labour. Labour immediately promises to repay the money.
