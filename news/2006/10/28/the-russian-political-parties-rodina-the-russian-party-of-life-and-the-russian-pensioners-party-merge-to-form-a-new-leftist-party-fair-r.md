### [2006-10-28](/news/2006/10/28/index.md)

##### Russia
#  The Russian political parties Rodina, the Russian Party of Life and the Russian Pensioners' Party merge to form a new leftist party, Fair Russia, effectively making Sergey Mironov the new leader of the opposition in the Russian legislature.
