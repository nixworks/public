### [2006-07-25](/news/2006/07/25/index.md)

##### Interstate Abortion Bill
#  The Interstate Abortion Bill is passed by the United States Senate. The bill would make it illegal for non-parents to take a minor to another state to obtain an abortion without parental consent.
