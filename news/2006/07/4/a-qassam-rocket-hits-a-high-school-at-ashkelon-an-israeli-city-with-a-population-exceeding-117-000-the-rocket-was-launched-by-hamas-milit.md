### [2006-07-4](/news/2006/07/4/index.md)

##### Qassam rocket
#  A Qassam rocket hits a High school at Ashkelon, an Israeli city with a population exceeding 117,000. The rocket was launched by Hamas militants from the town of Beit Hanun in the northern Gaza strip.
