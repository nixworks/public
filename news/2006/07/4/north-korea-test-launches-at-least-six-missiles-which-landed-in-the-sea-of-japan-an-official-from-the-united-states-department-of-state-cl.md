### [2006-07-4](/news/2006/07/4/index.md)

##### North Korea
#  North Korea test-launches at least six missiles which landed in the Sea of Japan. An official from the United States Department of State claims that one of the missiles was a long-range Taepodong 2 missile which failed 35 seconds after takeoff.
