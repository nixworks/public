### [2006-07-19](/news/2006/07/19/index.md)

##### International Federation of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies
#  Independent reports say that over 100 people are dead and missing in North Korea as a result of floods and landslides, while the North's government-controlled media omitted mention of any casualties. More than 9,000 families are left homeless. There have been at least 25 deaths in South Korea. 

More than 100 people are dead or missing in North Korea due to floods and landslides, an aid group operating in the communist nation said.
