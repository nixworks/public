### [2006-07-7](/news/2006/07/7/index.md)

##### Typhoon Ewiniar
#  Typhoon Ewiniar nears landfall on the southern island chain of Okinawa, Japan. The typhoon packs winds 160 km, 99 mi, per hour. Japan's Meteorological Agency warns of flooding on the island and the storm to move across South Korea over the weekend.
