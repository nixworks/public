### [2006-07-11](/news/2006/07/11/index.md)

##### Mumbai train bombings
#  Eight explosions hit at least four commuter trains in the Indian city of Mumbai (Bombay). India's other major cities are all placed on high alert. At least 174 people are reported dead, and at least 460 are reported injured. A suspect has been arrested in central Delhi.
