### [2006-07-11](/news/2006/07/11/index.md)

##### Mujahideen Shura Council
#  The Mujahideen Shura Council, a group linked to Al-Qaeda, posts a tape claiming responsibility for killing three US soldiers in retaliation for the killing of an Iraqi girl Abeer Qassim Hamza, allegedly by members of the same unit that the soldiers belonged to.
