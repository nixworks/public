### [2006-07-18](/news/2006/07/18/index.md)

##### International Four Days Marches Nijmegen
#  On the first day of the 90th International Four Days Marches Nijmegen, two participants die of heart failure due to exhaustion caused by the exceptionally warm weather in the Netherlands. Because even higher temperatures are predicted for the second day, the organization decides to cancel the remainder of the event.
