### [2006-07-18](/news/2006/07/18/index.md)

##### Turkish
#  Turkey calls for Iraq and the United States to crack down on Kurdish guerrillas based in northern Iraq, and issued a veiled threat to attack the rebel bases if there was no progress. Meanwhile, the International Crisis Group releases a report entitled "Iraq and the Kurds: The Brewing Battle over Kirkuk" which examines the dangerously neglected looming conflict in and around the northern Iraqi city.
