### [2006-09-10](/news/2006/09/10/index.md)

##### Hurricane Florence
#  Hurricane Florence forces hurricane warnings for Bermuda as it threatens the island with rain of 13 to 20 cm (5 to 8 inches). Florence is also expected to become a Category 2 hurricane prior to affecting the island.
