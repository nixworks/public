### [2006-09-23](/news/2006/09/23/index.md)

##### France
#  French authorities are investigating reports that Osama bin Laden has died of typhoid after a leaked French secret service report. "Time" magazine also cites an unknown Saudi Arabian source that Bin Laden had a waterborne disease and may be dead. However, French and U.S. authorities are unable to confirm these reports.
