### [2006-09-14](/news/2006/09/14/index.md)

##### Mahon Airport
#  A partial roof collapse occurred in part of the Mahon Airport terminal building in Menorca, Spain while undergoing renovations-construction work. The collapse may have been caused by a build-up of heavy rain water. The debris temporarily trapped 20 and injured three workers
