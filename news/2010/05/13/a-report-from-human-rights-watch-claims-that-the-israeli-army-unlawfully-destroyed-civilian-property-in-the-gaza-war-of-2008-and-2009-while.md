### [2010-05-13](/news/2010/05/13/index.md)

##### Human Rights Watch
# A report from Human Rights Watch claims that the Israeli army unlawfully destroyed civilian property in the Gaza War of 2008 and 2009, while the Israeli army asserts they only targeted property used for combat or terrorist activities. 

IDF formulating response to report which claims property was sometimes damaged even without military necessity.
