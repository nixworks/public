##### Dublin Airport
# Dublin Airport, Amsterdam Airport Schiphol and Britain's two busiest airports all close due to the latest round of Icelandic volcanic disruption. 

The Irish Aviation Authority has said Dublin Airport will remain closed until at least midday tomorrow because of the volcanic ash cloud.
