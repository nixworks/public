##### Czech Social Democratic Party
# The Social Democrats gain the most votes, but 10 percentage points less than 2006, losing 18 seats. [http:--edition.cnn.com-2010-WORLD-europe-05-30-czech.elections-index.html (CNN)] [http:--www.google.com-hostednews-ap-article-ALeqM5jXQklBDkGopiAnFivptSWyL6nplgD9G15CB00 (AP)]Centre-right parties win 118 seats, with two of them--TOP 09 and Public Affairs--standing in the election for the first time. A centre-right coalition, with Petr Necas as new Prime Minister, is believed to be "almost certain". [http:--www.businessweek.com-news-2010-05-29-czech-voters-choose-spending-cuts-amid-european-debt-crisis.html (BusinessWeek)]Social Democrats leader Jiri Paroubek resigns as party leader following the result. [https:--www.reuters.com-article-idUSPRG00416620100529?type=marketsNews (Reuters)]The Communist Party, which was polling higher than 2006 before the election, fail to make any gains, remaining at 26 seats.
