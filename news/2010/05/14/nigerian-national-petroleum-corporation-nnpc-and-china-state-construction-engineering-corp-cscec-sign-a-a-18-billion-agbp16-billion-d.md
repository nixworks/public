# Nigerian National Petroleum Corporation (NNPC) and China State Construction Engineering Corp (CSCEC) sign a EUR18 billion (GBP16 billion) deal. 

Nigerian and Chinese authorities sign a $23bn deal to build three refineries and a fuel complex in the African state.
