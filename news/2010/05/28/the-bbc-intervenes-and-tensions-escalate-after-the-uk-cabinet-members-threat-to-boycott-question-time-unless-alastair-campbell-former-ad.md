# The BBC intervenes and tensions escalate after the UK cabinet members' threat to boycott "Question Time" unless Alastair Campbell, former adviser to Tony Blair, is removed from the panel. 

The UK government refused to field a cabinet minister on the BBC&#39;s Question Time because the panel featured Alastair Campbell.
