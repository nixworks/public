### [2010-05-2](/news/2010/05/2/index.md)

##### World number one
# The World number one snooker player John Higgins and his manager are reported by the "News of the World" to have been videoed accepting a GBP261,000 bribe in Ukraine to lose frames in four separate matches later this year; an investigation is launched and the player is immediately suspended on the final day of his reign as world champion ahead of the final of the 2010 World Snooker Championship. 

John Higgins, the snooker world champion, has allegedly been caught agreeing to take a &pound;261,000 bribe to fix matches.
