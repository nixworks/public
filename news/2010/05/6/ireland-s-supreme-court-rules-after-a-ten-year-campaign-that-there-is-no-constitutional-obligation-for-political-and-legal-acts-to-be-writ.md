# Ireland's Supreme Court rules, after a ten-year campaign, that there is no constitutional obligation for political and legal acts to be written in both the English and Irish languages 

The Supreme Court today ruled that there is no Constitutional obligation to provide Acts of the Oireachtas or Statutory Instruments simultaneously in Irish and English.
