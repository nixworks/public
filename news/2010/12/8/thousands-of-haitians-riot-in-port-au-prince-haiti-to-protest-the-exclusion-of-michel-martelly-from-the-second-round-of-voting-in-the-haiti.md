##### Port-Au-Prince
# Thousands of Haitians riot in Port-Au-Prince, Haiti to protest the exclusion of Michel Martelly from the second round of voting in the Haitian presidential election. 

Thousands of protesters rampage in Haitian towns, torching buildings in armed clashes that left four dead, after election results triggered bitter accusations of vote-rigging.
