### [2010-12-24](/news/2010/12/24/index.md)

##### Afghanistan
# A tripartite summit of Afghanistan, Pakistan and Turkey is held in Istanbul, aimed at boosting trust between Afghanistan and Pakistan. 

Three countries to hold joint military exercises in April, agree to cooperate on anti-narcotics operations.
