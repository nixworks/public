### [2010-12-31](/news/2010/12/31/index.md)

##### Annie Lennox
# Arts and cultural people Annie Lennox, Herbert Kretzmer, Sheila Hancock, David Suchet, Harriet Walter, Katharine Hamnett, Alice Temperley, Sandy Powell, John Lloyd, Steve McQueen and Trevor Horn are among those to receive a New Year Honour from the Queen of the United Kingdom. 

Singer Annie Lennox is recognised in the New Year Honours, along with actors David Suchet and Sheila Hancock and BA chairman Martin Broughton.
