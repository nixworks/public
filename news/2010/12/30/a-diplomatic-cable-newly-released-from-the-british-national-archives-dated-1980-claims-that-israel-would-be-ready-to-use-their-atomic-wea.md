##### British National Archives
# A diplomatic cable newly released from the British National Archives, dated 1980, claims that Israel would be "ready to use their atomic weapon" in any further war against its adversaries.

Former Ambassador Robinson told Foreign Office &#39;if Israelis are to be destroyed, they will go down fighting this time&#39;
