##### Saeb Erakat
# Palestinian negotiator Saeb Erakat asks the European Union to recognize "two states (Israel and Palestine) along the 1967 borders". 

Chief Palestinian negotiator Saeb Erakat formally asked the European Union in a letter to recognize an independent Palestinian state.
