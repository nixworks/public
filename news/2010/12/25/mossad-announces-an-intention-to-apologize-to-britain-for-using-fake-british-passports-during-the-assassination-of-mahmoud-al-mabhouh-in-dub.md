##### Mossad
# Mossad announces an intention to apologize to Britain for using fake British passports during the assassination of Mahmoud al-Mabhouh in Dubai in January 2010. 

Israel will apologize for using British passports in the assassination of a Hamas operative in Dubai, Mossad insiders said.
