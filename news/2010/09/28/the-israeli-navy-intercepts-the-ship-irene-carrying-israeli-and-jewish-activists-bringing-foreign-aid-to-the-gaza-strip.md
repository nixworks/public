# The Israeli Navy intercepts the ship "Irene" carrying Israeli and Jewish activists bringing foreign aid to the Gaza Strip. 

Ship is 20 miles from Gaza coast; activist aboard the boat: 2 Israeli Navy ships have escorted us for half an hour.
