##### United States Court of Appeals for the Ninth Circuit
# The United States Court of Appeals for the Ninth Circuit dismisses a lawsuit associated with the Central Intelligence Agency's practice of "extraordinary rendition". 

A sharply divided appeals court dismissed a lawsuit involving the C.I.A.’s “extraordinary rendition” program.
