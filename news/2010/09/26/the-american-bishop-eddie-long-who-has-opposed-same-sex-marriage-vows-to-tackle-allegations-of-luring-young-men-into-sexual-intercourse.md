##### Eddie Long
# The American bishop Eddie Long, who has opposed same-sex marriage, vows to tackle allegations of luring young men into sexual intercourse. 

The pastor of a hugely popular independent church in the US says he will fight claims that he coerced young men into sexual relationships.
