### [2010-09-1](/news/2010/09/1/index.md)

##### Australia
# A trade agreement between Australia and the European Union comes into effect stopping Australian wine being marketed using terms such as champagne and port. 

A new deal will ban Australian imitation champagne and sherry from the EU - but Australia welcomes simplified wine labels.
