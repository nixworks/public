##### 2010 Copiap mining accident
# The 33 miners involved in the 2010 Copiap mining accident in Chile receive their first hot meal in 26 days: it consists of meatballs, chicken and rice through a tube. 

Chilean miners trapped underground receive their first hot meal in 26 days, as Nasa experts arrive at the mine site.
