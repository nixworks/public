# Typhoon Fanapi (Inday) hits Taiwan with at least three people killed and dozens of people injured. The Peoples Republic of China steps up its emergency response in preparation for an impact on Monday. 

China raised its emergency response for Typhoon Fanapi ahead of the storm striking the country today, the China Meteorological Administration said.
