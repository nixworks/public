##### Palestine
# A Palestinian man is shot dead by an Israeli security guard in the East Jerusalem area of Silwan, during a clash between Israeli settlers and Palestinians. 

A Jewish settlement guard kills a Palestinian man in the East Jerusalem neighbourhood of Silwan after residents pelt his car with stones.
