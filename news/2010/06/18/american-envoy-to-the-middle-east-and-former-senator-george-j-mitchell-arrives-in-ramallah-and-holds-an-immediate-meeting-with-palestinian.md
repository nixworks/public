### [2010-06-18](/news/2010/06/18/index.md)

# American Envoy to the Middle East and former Senator George J. Mitchell arrives in Ramallah and holds an immediate meeting with Palestinian President Mahmoud Abbas. 

Palestinian president urges George Mitchell to press Israel to lift its blockade on Gaza.
