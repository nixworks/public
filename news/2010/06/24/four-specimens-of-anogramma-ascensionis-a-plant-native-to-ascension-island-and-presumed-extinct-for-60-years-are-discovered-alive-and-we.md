##### Anogramma ascensionis
# Four specimens of "Anogramma ascensionis", a plant native to Ascension Island and presumed extinct for 60 years, are discovered alive and well in Kew. 

Scientists from Kew Gardens have rediscovered a plant that has been presumed extinct for almost 60 years.
