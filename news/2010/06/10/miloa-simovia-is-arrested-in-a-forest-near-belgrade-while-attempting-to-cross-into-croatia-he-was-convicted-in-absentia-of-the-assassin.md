##### Milos Simovic
# Milos Simovic is arrested in a forest near Belgrade while attempting to cross into Croatia. He was convicted "in absentia" of the assassination of Serbian Prime Minister Zoran Dindic in 2003. 

Second man arrested as accomplice in the killing of Serbian PM Zoran Djindjic in 2003 is detained in Croatia.
