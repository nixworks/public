### [2010-06-11](/news/2010/06/11/index.md)

##### Chihuahua
# 40 people are killed and at least four others are wounded in an attack by at least 30 gunmen in Chihuahua, Mexico. 

Gun attacks in two Mexican cities leave at least 39 people dead, police say.
