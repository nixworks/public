# The Saville Inquiry into the events of Bloody Sunday (1972) in Northern Ireland is published, as a result of the longest-running public inquiry in British legal history. 

The Bloody Sunday killings were unjustified and unjustifiable, the Prime Minister has said, announcing the findings of the Saville Report.
