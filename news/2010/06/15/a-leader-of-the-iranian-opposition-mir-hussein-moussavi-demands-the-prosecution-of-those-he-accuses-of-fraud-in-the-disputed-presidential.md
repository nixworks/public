### [2010-06-15](/news/2010/06/15/index.md)

##### Iranian opposition
# A leader of the Iranian opposition, Mir Hussein Moussavi, demands the prosecution of those he accuses of fraud in the disputed presidential election of 2009 and of human rights abuses against opposition demonstrators over the past year in Iran. 

Mir Hussein Moussavi issued a defiant statement but urged his supporters to avoid confrontation in Tehran around the one-year anniversary of election protests.
