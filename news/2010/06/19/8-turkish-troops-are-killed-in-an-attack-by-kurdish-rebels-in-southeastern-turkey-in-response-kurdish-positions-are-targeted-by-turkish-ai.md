### [2010-06-19](/news/2010/06/19/index.md)

##### Turkey
# 8 Turkish troops are killed in an attack by Kurdish rebels in southeastern Turkey. In response, Kurdish positions are targeted by Turkish airstrikes in Northern Iraq. Twelve Kurdish rebels are killed. 

Ten Turkish soldiers have been killed in an attack by Kurdish rebels near the Iraqi border, reports say.
