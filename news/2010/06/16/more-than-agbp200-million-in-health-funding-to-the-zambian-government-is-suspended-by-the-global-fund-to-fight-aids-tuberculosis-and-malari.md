##### Zambia
# More than GBP200 million in health funding to the Zambian government is suspended by the Global Fund to Fight AIDS, Tuberculosis and Malaria, though some aid is given to non-government groups and the fund's director of communications says life-saving treatments remain unaffected. 

More than $300m of HIV/Aids health funding to Zambia is being suspended over corruption concerns, aid officials say.
