### [2010-06-16](/news/2010/06/16/index.md)

# The Pacific-10 Conference, after being turned down by several Texas and Oklahoma schools in its effort to expand to 12 or more members, invites the University of Utah to become its 12th member. 

Utah will join the Pac-10 Conference as its 12th team, with an announcement coming as soon as Friday, a source tells ESPN.
