##### CE-2
# Flying in an orbit 100 kilometers above the moon, the CE-2 is tasked to collect data for a soft-landing designed for its successor, the Chang'e-3. China Aerospace Science and Technology Corporation (CASC), said at the Global Lunar Conference held in Beijing.
