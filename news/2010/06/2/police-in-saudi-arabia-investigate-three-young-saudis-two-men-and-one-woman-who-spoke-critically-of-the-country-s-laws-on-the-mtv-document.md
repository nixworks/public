##### Saudi Arabia
# Police in Saudi Arabia investigate three young Saudis, two men and one woman, who spoke critically of the country's laws on the MTV documentary, "Resist the Power! Saudi Arabia". 

Three Saudi youths who appeared in a recent broadcast of the MTV documentary series “True Life” could be brought to court by the Saudi Arabian religious police.
