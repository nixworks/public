# The right's Civic Democratic Party (ODS), TOP 09 and Public Affairs sign a coalition agreement in the wake of last Saturday's general election in the Czech Republic, even though the left Czech Social Democratic Party (CSSD) won more votes. 

Three centre-right parties pledge to work together following weekend election.
