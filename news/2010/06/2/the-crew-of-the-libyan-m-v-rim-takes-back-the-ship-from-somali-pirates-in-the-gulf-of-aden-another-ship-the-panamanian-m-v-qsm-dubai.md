### [2010-06-2](/news/2010/06/2/index.md)

##### Libya
# The crew of the Libyan M-V "Rim" takes back the ship from Somali pirates in the Gulf of Aden. Another ship, the Panamanian M-V "QSM Dubai", is captured. 

The crew of a cargo ship seized by Somali pirates in the Gulf of Aden reportedly retake control of the vessel.
