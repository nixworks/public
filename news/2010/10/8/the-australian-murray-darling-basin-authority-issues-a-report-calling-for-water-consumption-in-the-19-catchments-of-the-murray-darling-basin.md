# The Australian Murray-Darling Basin Authority issues a report calling for water consumption in the 19 catchments of the Murray-Darling basin to be cut by up to half. 

The Murray-Darling Basin Authority calls for water consumption from some of the basin&#039;s river systems to be slashed by almost half.
