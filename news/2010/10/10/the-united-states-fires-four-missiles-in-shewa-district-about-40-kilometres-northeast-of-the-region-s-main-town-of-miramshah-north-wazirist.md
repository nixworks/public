### [2010-10-10](/news/2010/10/10/index.md)

##### U.S.
# The United States fires four missiles in Shewa district about 40 kilometres northeast of the region's main town of Miramshah, North Waziristan, Pakistan, killing seven and destroying two cars, as the country increases its attacks on the tribal area.
