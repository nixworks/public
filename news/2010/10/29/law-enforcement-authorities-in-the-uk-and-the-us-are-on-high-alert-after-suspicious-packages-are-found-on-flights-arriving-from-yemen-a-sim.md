# Law enforcement authorities in the UK and the US are on high alert after suspicious packages are found on flights arriving from Yemen. A similar package is discovered in Dubai. 

A &quot;suspicious object&quot; found to contain explosives at East Midlands Airport was on a plane flying from Yemen to Chicago.
