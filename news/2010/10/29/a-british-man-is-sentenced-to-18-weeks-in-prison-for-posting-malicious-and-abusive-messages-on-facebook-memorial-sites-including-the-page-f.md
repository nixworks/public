# A British man is sentenced to 18 weeks in prison for posting malicious and abusive messages on Facebook memorial sites, including the page for deceased reality TV star Jade Goody. 

An &quot;internet troll&quot; who posted obscene and offensive messages on Facebook sites dedicated to the memories of dead people is jailed.
