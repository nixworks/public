##### Physicist
# Physicists Andre Geim and Konstantin Novoselov at the University of Manchester win the 2010 Nobel Prize in Physics "for groundbreaking experiments regarding the two-dimensional material graphene". 

Two Russian-born physicists honoured for work on graphene, which is both the thinnest and toughest material known.
