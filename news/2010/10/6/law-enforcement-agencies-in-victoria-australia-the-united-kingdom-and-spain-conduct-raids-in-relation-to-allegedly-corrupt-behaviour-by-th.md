# Law enforcement agencies in Victoria, Australia, the United Kingdom and Spain conduct raids in relation to allegedly corrupt behaviour by the currency subsidiary of the Reserve Bank of Australia. 

Law enforcement agencies in Melbourne, Britain and Spain have launched raids over claims of corrupt conduct by the currency subsidiary of the Reserve Bank of Australia.
