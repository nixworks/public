##### MAXXI - National Museum of the 21st Century Arts
# The MAXXI - National Museum of the 21st Century Arts in Rome wins the Stirling Prize, a British architectural award given by the Royal Institute of British Architects (RIBA). 

The British architect Zaha Hadid has finally won the RIBA Stirling Prize on her fourth attempt.
