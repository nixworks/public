##### Indonesia
# In Indonesia, two trains are involved in a rear-end collision at Petarukan, killing 43 and injuring 50. Another train crash at Solo kills one person. 

Two trains collide in a station near the Indonesian city of Pemalang, killing at least 36 people, with officials suspecting a signalling error.
