# Riot police and dogs clash with protesters as demonstrations lead to violence in England. There are injuries and 5 men are arrested in the country's largest police operation for a quarter of a century (25 years). 

Thirteen men are arrested as demonstrators stage two protests in Leicester, sparking the biggest police operation in the county for 25 years.
