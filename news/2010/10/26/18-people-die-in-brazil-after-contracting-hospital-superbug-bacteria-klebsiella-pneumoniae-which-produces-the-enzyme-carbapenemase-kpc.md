# 18 people die in Brazil after contracting hospital "superbug" bacteria "Klebsiella pneumoniae"; which produces the enzyme carbapenemase (KPC), which renders most modern antibiotics ineffective. 

An outbreak of a drug-resistant strain of bacteria worries doctors in Brazil after a rising number of infections leads to 18 deaths in the capital, Brasilia.
