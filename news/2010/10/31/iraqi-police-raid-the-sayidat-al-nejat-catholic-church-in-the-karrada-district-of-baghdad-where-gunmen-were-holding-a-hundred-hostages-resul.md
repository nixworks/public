##### Iraqi police
# Iraqi police raid the Sayidat al-Nejat Catholic Church in the Karrada district of Baghdad where gunmen were holding a hundred hostages resulting in seven hostages being killed and twenty injured. 

At least 52 people are killed after Iraqi troops storm a Catholic church in Baghdad to free dozens of hostages being held by gunmen.
