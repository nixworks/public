##### Robert Geoffrey Edwards
# Robert Geoffrey Edwards of the United Kingdom wins the Nobel Prize in Physiology or Medicine for his role in developing in vitro fertilisation. 

The man who devised the fertility treatment IVF is awarded the Nobel prize for medicine.
