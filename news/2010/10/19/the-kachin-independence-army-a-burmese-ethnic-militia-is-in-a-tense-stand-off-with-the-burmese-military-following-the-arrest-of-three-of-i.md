##### Kachin Independence Army
# The Kachin Independence Army, a Burmese ethnic militia, is in a tense stand-off with the Burmese military following the arrest of three of its members. 

The Kachin Independence Army in northern Burma says it is involved in a tense stand-off with the Burmese army.
