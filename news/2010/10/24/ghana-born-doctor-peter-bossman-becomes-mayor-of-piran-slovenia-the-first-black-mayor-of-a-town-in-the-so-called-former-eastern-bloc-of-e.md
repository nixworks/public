# Ghana-born doctor Peter Bossman becomes Mayor of Piran (Slovenia), the first black mayor of a town in the so-called former Eastern Bloc of Europe. 

A Ghana-born doctor is elected mayor of a town in Slovenia - apparently breaking new ground for the former Yugoslavia.
