##### Wang Qishan
# Chinese Vice Premier Wang Qishan welcomes U.S. Treasury Secretary Timothy Geithner to the port of Qingdao as the United States objects to what it labels the "artificial" value of China's own currency, the yuan. 

US Treasury Secretary Timothy Geithner says he believes China is now &quot;committed&quot; to allowing the yuan to go up in value.
