##### Joe Glenton
# Joe Glenton, a British soldier who went AWOL in 2007 and became a figurehead of the anti-war movement, is demoted and sent to jail in Colchester. 

A soldier who went absent without leave rather than serve a second tour in Afghanistan has been jailed for nine months.
