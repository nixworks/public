##### Qaim
# 6 die and 33 are injured in five co-ordinated bombings targeting militia leader Sheikh Turki Hamad Mikhlif in Qaim, Iraq. 

Six people were killed and 15 others wounded in a roadside bomb attack in the Iraqi city of Qaim.
