# Turkey recalls its ambassador to Sweden and cancels the Turkey - Sweden summit planned for March 17 after the Riksdag votes in favour of calling the Armenian Genocide a genocide. 

Turkey has recalled its ambassador from Sweden after its parliament voted to brand the WW1 killing of Armenians by Ottoman forces as genocide.
