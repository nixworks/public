##### Israel
# Israel fires its first missiles into Gaza this month injuring several civilians, following a rocket attack on a kibbutz in southern Israel which causes damage but no injuries.

In response to rocket fired at Eshkol Regional Council, Israeli jets strikes smuggling tunnel, weapons lab in southern Strip. Gaza eyewitnesses tell AFP several people injured
