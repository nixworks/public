# President of France Nicolas Sarkozy gives EUR3 million to areas devastated by France's worst storm for over a decade, calling the storm a "national disaster". 

The storms which battered France, leaving nearly 60 people dead, have been declared a &#034;national disaster&#034;, by Nicolas Sarkozy.
