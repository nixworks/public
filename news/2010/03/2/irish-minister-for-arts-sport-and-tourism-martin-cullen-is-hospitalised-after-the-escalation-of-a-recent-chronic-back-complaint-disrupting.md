# Irish Minister for Arts, Sport and Tourism Martin Cullen is hospitalised after the escalation of a recent chronic back complaint, disrupting some of his work, including a visit to the United States. 

Arts, Sports &amp; Tourism Minister Martin Cullen has been admitted to hospital for treatment for a chronic back complaint.
