##### Boris Berezovsky
# Boris Berezovsky is awarded GBP150,000 in England's High Court and wins his libel case in relation to the 2006 poisoning to death of Alexander Litvinenko. 

Boris Berezovsky won &pound;150,000 High Court libel damages over claims on a Russian TV broadcast about the radiation poisoning murder of Alexander Litvinenko.
