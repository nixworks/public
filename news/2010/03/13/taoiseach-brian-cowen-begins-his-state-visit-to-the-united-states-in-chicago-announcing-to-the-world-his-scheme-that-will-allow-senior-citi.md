##### Taoiseach
# Taoiseach Brian Cowen begins his state visit to the United States in Chicago, announcing to the world his scheme that will allow senior citizen tourists aged 66 and above to travel free on Iarnrod Eireann in the Republic of Ireland. 

The Taoiseach was the guest of honour at today&#39;s St Patrick&#39;s parade in Chicago.
