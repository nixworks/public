### [2010-07-5](/news/2010/07/5/index.md)

##### Saidnaya
# Human rights groups say at least 52 prisoners have disappeared from Saidnaya Military Prison in Syria, following disturbances in 2008 that led to the deaths of 22 people. 

Two years after unrest in Saydnaya jail, relatives of 52 inmates await news of their fate.
