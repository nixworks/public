##### Guantanamo Bay
# Two men imprisoned for nearly eight years in Guantanamo Bay are sent to Algeria and Cape Verde, according to the United States Pentagon, against their will. 

Two men held in Guantanamo Bay for nearly eight years have been released to Algeria and Cape Verde, the Pentagon has announced.
