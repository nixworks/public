### [2010-07-21](/news/2010/07/21/index.md)

##### Palestinian
# Two Palestinian militants of the Islamic Jihad Movement in Palestine are killed and another six wounded by Israeli shelling as they approached the Gaza Strip-Israel border near Beit Hanoun. A ten-year-old girl is also wounded. 

Top Islamic Jihad militant killed as Israeli tanks fire at militants after receiving reports they intended to attack a nearby IDF unit.
