# Three plainclothes Chinese police officers beat up, bruise and concuss a provincial official's wife by accident; the police are punished, while the woman is hospitalised. 

The wife of a senior Chinese government official was hospitalised after being beaten up by plain clothes policemen who mistook her for a protestor.
