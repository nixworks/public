##### Alan Shadrake
# 75-year-old author Alan Shadrake is arrested in Singapore while promoting his book about the use of the death penalty. He is charged with criminal defamation and faces two years imprisonment. 

The British author of a book that seeks to expose &quot;disturbing truths&quot; about Singapore&#039;s use of the death penalty has been arrested on charges of criminal defamation.
