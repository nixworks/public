# Authorities at Mexico City International Airport arrest a man who had flown from Lima and was attempting to smuggle 18 little monkeys into the country inside items of clothing. 

A man is arrested in Mexico trying to smuggle 18 monkeys into the country by hiding them in a girdle around his waist.
