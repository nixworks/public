# David Cameron opts not to meet four United States senators to discuss allegations BP lobbied for the release of the terminally ill Abdelbaset al-Megrahi, though they may meet the ambassador instead. 

UK Prime Minister David Cameron insists BP should not be blamed for the &quot;completely wrong&quot; decision to release the Lockerbie bomber.
