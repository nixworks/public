# Poland and the United States sign a deal in Krakow allowing the United States to position an American anti-missile shield in Poland to defend Europe from the perceived threat of Iran and other countries: Russia objects. 

Clinton cites Iranian threats to counter Russian objections to anti-missile shield deployment.
