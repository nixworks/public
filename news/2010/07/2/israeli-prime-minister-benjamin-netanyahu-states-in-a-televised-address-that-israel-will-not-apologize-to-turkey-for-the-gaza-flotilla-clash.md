# Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu states in a televised address that Israel will not apologize to Turkey for the Gaza flotilla clash and that no compensation will be paid to the injured. 

Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu has ruled out any apology from his country for a raid on a Turkish Gaza-bound aid ship that killed nine Turks. &quot;
