### [2010-07-4](/news/2010/07/4/index.md)

# Polish voters head to the polls for the second round of presidential voting.Jaroslaw Kaczynski initially concedes defeat after exit polls show Bronislaw Komorowski winning, but later withdraws his concession as exit polls show the result narrowing. 

Bronislaw Komorowski is declared the winner in a dramatic race for the Polish presidency, securing 53% of the vote.
