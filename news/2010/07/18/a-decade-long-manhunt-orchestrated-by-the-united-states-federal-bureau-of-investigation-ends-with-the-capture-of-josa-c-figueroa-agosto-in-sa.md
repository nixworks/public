# A decade-long manhunt orchestrated by the United States Federal Bureau of Investigation ends with the capture of Jose Figueroa Agosto in San Juan, Puerto Rico. 

An alleged drug kingpin, known as the Pablo Escobar of the Caribbean, has been arrested after a decade-long chase marked by his narrow escapes and taunts.
