##### President of Zimbabwe
# President of Zimbabwe Robert Mugabe, speaking in Marange, says he will ignore anyone who desires mention of LGBT rights in a draft of the next constitution of Zimbabwe. 

The Zimbabwe president has said the country will not listen to those who want gay rights mentioned in a draft of the new constitution.
