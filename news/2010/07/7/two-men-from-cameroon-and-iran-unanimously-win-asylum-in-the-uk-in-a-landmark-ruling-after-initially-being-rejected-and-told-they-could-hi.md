# Two men from Cameroon and Iran "unanimously" win asylum in the UK in a landmark ruling after initially being rejected and told they could hide their homosexuality by "behaving discreetly". 

Homosexuals who cannot live an open life in their own countries without fear of persecution should be entitled to asylum in Britain, the Supreme Court said on Wednesday.
