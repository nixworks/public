# Hundreds of people wearing pink wigs and pink clothing walk out of Catholic Mass in 's-Hertogenbosch in the Netherlands in protest of a gay Dutch man being refused communion in a nearby town. 

Hundreds of people walked out of a Catholic church in the Netherlands Sunday to protest church policy against giving communion to homosexuals, organizers said.
