# Conference of the German Bishops chairman Archbishop Robert Zollitsch formally apologises to those who went through sexual abuse as children after fresh controversy surrounding the issue at the beginning of this year. 

The head of the German Bishop&#39;s Conference Archbishop, Robert Zollitsch, has made a formal apology to the victims of clerical abuse at some Catholic schools.
