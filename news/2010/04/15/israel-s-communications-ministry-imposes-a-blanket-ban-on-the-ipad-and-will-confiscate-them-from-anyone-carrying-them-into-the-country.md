# Israel's Communications Ministry imposes a blanket ban on the iPad and will confiscate them from anyone carrying them into the country. 

Don&#039;t count on bringing the iPad on any trips to the Holy Land, as Israeli Customs says it will confiscate Apple&#039;s tablets because of the Wi-Fi standards it uses.
