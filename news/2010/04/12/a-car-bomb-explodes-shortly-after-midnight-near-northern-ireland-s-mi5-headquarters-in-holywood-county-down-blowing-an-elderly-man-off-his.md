### [2010-04-12](/news/2010/04/12/index.md)

##### Northern Ireland
# A car bomb explodes shortly after midnight near Northern Ireland's MI5 headquarters in Holywood, County Down, blowing an elderly man off his feet and sending him to hospital. 

The Real IRA has claimed responsibility for the car bomb that exploded close to the British Army base in Co Down this morning.
