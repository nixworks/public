##### Indian Premier League
# The semi-finals of the Indian Premier League (IPL) cricket tournament are moved from Bangalore to Mumbai following the discovery of further explosive devices after yesterday's bombing. 

Discovery of two more devices sees Bangalore lose hosting of both cricket semi-finals.
