##### Florence Green
# Previously unknown Florence Green, at 108, is believed to be Britain's oldest surviving UK-resident First World War veteran, and the last known female First World War veteran, having served with the Women's RAF (WRAF) in 1918 

A 108-year-old woman is believed to be Britain&rsquo;s oldest surviving First World War veteran.
