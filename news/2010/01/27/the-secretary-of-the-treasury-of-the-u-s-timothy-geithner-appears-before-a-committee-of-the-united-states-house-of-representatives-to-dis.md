### [2010-01-27](/news/2010/01/27/index.md)

##### Secretary of the Treasury
# The Secretary of the Treasury of the U.S., Timothy Geithner, appears before a committee of the United States House of Representatives to discuss his actions in 2008, when he was the President of the Federal Reserve Bank of New York, in the rescue of troubled insurance industry giant AIG. 

In questioning, Treasury Secretary Timothy F. Geithner said the A.I.G. bailout helped prevent a depression.
