# The MV "Mavi Marmara" arrives in Turkey; the remaining three non-Turkish flotilla vessels plus the MV "Rachel Corrie" cargo ship, which followed days later, remain in Israeli custody. 

Mavi Marmara will be inspected by Turkish authorities and a UN delegation.
