# Morocco is to close 1,250 mosques deemed to be unsafe following the collapse of a minaret at the Bab Berdieyinne Mosque in February. 

Morocco will close 1,256 unsafe mosques to avoid a repeat of the collapse of a minaret that killed 41 people.
