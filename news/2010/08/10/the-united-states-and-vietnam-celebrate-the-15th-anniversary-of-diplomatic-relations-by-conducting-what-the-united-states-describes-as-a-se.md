### [2010-08-10](/news/2010/08/10/index.md)

##### United States
# The United States and Vietnam celebrate the 15th anniversary of diplomatic relations by conducting what the United States describes as a "series of naval engagement activities" in the South China Sea, risking a disagreement with China. 

The US and Vietnam stage joint naval activities in the South China Sea, a sign of increasing military ties between the former enemies.
