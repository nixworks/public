##### Edelmiro Cavazos
# The corpse of mayor Edelmiro Cavazos of the Mexican town of Santiago, Nuevo Len, is found handcuffed and blindfolded after his abduction on Sunday night. 

Police in Mexico find the body of the kidnapped mayor of the northern city of Santiago three days after he was seized, local media say.
