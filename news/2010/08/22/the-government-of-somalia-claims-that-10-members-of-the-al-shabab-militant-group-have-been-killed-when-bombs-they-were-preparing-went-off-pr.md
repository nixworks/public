### [2010-08-22](/news/2010/08/22/index.md)

##### Transitional Federal Government
# The Government of Somalia claims that 10 members of the al-Shabab militant group have been killed when bombs they were preparing went off prematurely in Mogadishu. 

Government says the men died while preparing or planting bombs in two separate incidents.
