### [2010-08-22](/news/2010/08/22/index.md)

##### Julian Assange
# Supporters of Julian Assange of Wikileaks credit American intelligence agencies with recent smears against his character and express surprise that it has not happened sooner. 

Wikileaks founder says Australian intelligence warned him on August 11 to expect personal attacks.
