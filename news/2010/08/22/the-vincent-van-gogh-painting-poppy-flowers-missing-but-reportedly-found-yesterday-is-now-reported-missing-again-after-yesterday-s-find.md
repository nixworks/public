##### Vincent van Gogh
# The Vincent van Gogh painting "Poppy Flowers", missing but reportedly found yesterday, is now reported missing again after yesterday's find is proven false. 

A painting by Vincent Van Gogh, stolen from a Cairo museum, is still missing, says the culture minister, hours after he said it had been found.
