# President of the United States Barack Obama states that America's "commitment to religious freedom must be unshakable" as he declares his support for plans to build a mosque in New York City. 

President Obama defends controversial plans to build a mosque near Ground Zero site saying commitment to religious freedom &quot;must be unshakeable&quot;.
