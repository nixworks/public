### [2010-08-9](/news/2010/08/9/index.md)

##### International Assistance Mission
# The International Assistance Mission suspends medical expeditions in Afghanistan following loss of personnel in the 2010 Badakhshan massacre. 

The head of a relief group whose workers were killed had been worried there were too many foreigners on the trip.
