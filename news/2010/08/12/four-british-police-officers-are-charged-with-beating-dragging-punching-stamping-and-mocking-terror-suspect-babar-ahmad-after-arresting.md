### [2010-08-12](/news/2010/08/12/index.md)

# Four British police officers are charged with beating, dragging, punching, stamping and mocking "terror suspect" Babar Ahmad after arresting him in Tooting, South London in 2003; the suspect, a 36-year-old IT worker, was later deemed innocent. 

Four policemen face prosecution for actual bodily harm on a terror suspect in London in December 2003.
