# Relatives accuse authorities of not doing enough to save 33 miners who have spent the past 15 days trapped in a collapsed mine near Copiap in the Atacama Desert. 

Relatives of 33 miners trapped for 16 days in a mine in Chile say rescue efforts are not proceeding fast enough.
