### [2010-08-21](/news/2010/08/21/index.md)

##### Mogadishu
# At least 10 anti-government protesters are killed by early morning bombs in Mogadishu; the dead include people from Afghanistan, Algeria, India and Pakistan. 

At least 10 anti-government militants are believed to have died in Mogadishu when their bombs exploded prematurely.
