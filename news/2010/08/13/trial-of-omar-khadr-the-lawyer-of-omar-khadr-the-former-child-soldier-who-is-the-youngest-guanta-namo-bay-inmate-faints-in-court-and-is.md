### [2010-08-13](/news/2010/08/13/index.md)

# Trial of Omar Khadr:. The lawyer of Omar Khadr, the former child soldier who is the youngest Guantanamo Bay inmate, faints in court and is rushed to hospital. The trial subsequently gets suspended.

The first trial at Guantanamo Bay under US President Barack Obama is suspended after the defence lawyer faints in court.
