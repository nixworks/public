##### Dublin Zoo
# Dublin Zoo upsets some people with red hair by offering free entry to young redheads in honour of Orangutan Awareness Week. 

Dublin Zoo is offering free entry to &quot;any type of red haired child under 16 years of age&quot; this weekend.
