# Cuba - United States relations: A Cuban website questions the release of "Call of Duty: Black Ops", an American shooter video game in which United States forces try to murder Fidel Castro. 

Cuba has condemned the release of a new video game in which United States special forces try to kill a young Fidel Castro.
