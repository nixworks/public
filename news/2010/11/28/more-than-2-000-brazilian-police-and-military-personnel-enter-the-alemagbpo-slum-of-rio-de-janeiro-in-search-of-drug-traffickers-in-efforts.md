##### Brazil
# More than 2,000 Brazilian police and military personnel enter the AlemGBPo slum of Rio de Janeiro in search of drug traffickers, in efforts to boost security well ahead of the 2016 Olympic Games. 

Security forces seized control of a violent slum, claiming victory in a weeklong battle against drug gangs that has claimed dozens of lives.
