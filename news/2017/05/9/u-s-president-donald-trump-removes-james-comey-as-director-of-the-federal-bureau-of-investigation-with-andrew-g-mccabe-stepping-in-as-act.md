### [2017-05-9](/news/2017/05/9/index.md)

##### Dismissal of James Comey
# U.S. President Donald Trump removes James Comey as Director of the Federal Bureau of Investigation, with Andrew G. McCabe stepping in as acting director. 

The Trump administration says Mr Comey made &quot;serious mistakes&quot; in the Clinton email investigation.
