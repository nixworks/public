### [2017-05-28](/news/2017/05/28/index.md)

##### Tiburcio Caras Andino Stadium
# Four people, including a pregnant woman, are killed and dozens injured when a rush of fans try to enter an already-at-capacity Tiburcio Caras Andino Stadium before the Honduran Clausura final in Tegucigalpa, Honduras. Police are investigating possible overselling of tickets. 

A tragic stampede ahead of the Clausura final between Motagua and Progreso cost four fans their lives amid fears the capacity was exceeded
