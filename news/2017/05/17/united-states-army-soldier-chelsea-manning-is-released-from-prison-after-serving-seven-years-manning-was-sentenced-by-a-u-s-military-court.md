# United States Army soldier Chelsea Manning is released from prison after serving seven years. Manning was sentenced by a U.S. military court to 35 years in prison, but her sentence was commuted by President Barack Obama. 

American army private is free after serving seven years of 35-year sentence for leaking classified documents and videos downloaded to WikiLeaks
