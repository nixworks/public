##### Norwegian parliamentary election, 2017
# The right-wing coalition led by Erna Solberg wins its second term in office in the Norwegian parliamentary election held late yesterday. 

The conservative coalition led by Erna Solberg is projected to win 89 of 169 seats in parliament.
