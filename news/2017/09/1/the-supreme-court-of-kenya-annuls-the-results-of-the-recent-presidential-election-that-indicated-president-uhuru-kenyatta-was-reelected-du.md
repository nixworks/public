### [2017-09-1](/news/2017/09/1/index.md)

##### Kenyan general election, 2017
# The Supreme Court of Kenya annuls the results of the recent presidential election, that indicated President Uhuru Kenyatta was reelected, due to irregularities, and orders a new election. 

The Supreme Court orders a new vote citing irregularities, sparking joy among opposition supporters.
