##### Journalists killed in India
# Prominent Indian journalist and "Lankesh Patrike" editor Gauri Lankesh is shot dead by unidentified men outside her house in the city of Bengaluru. 

A senior Indian journalist was shot dead on Tuesday in the southern city of Bengaluru by unidentified assailants, police said.
