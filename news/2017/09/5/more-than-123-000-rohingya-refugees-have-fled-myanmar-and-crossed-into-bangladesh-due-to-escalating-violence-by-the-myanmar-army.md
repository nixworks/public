### [2017-09-5](/news/2017/09/5/index.md)

##### 2016-17 Rohingya persecution in Myanmar
# More than 123,000 Rohingya refugees have fled Myanmar and crossed into Bangladesh due to escalating violence by the Myanmar Army. 

More than 35,000 new arrivals have been identified in 24 hours, the United Nations says.
