### [2017-07-14](/news/2017/07/14/index.md)

##### Marco Polo condo fire
# At least three people are killed and two hospitalized, one in serious condition, from a fire in a condominium tower in the U.S. city of Honolulu, Hawaii. An unknown number of residents are reportedly still trapped in their apartments. 

At least three people died Friday in a fire in a high-rise apartment building in Honolulu and authorities fear some residents may be trapped in surrounding units, fire officials said.
