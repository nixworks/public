##### Floods in India
#  Following heavy rain in July 2017, the Gujarat state of India was affected by a severe flood resulting in more than 200 deaths.

The flood toll in Gujarat has risen to 218 this monsoon, with 61 people killed in worst-hit Banaskantha district, an official said on Monday.
