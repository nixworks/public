##### Crime in New York City
# A police shooting occurs in the Bronx, New York, killing one police officer and injuring a bystander. Two officers confront the suspect and shoot him dead. 

Miosotis Familia, a 12-year veteran of the force, was shot in the head while sitting in a vehicle. The gunman was killed, the police said.
