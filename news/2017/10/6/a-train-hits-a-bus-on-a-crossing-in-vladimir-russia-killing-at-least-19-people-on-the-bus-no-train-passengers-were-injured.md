### [2017-10-6](/news/2017/10/6/index.md)

##### Vladimir
# A train hits a bus on a crossing in Vladimir, Russia, killing at least 19 people on the bus. No train passengers were injured. 

About 55 bus passengers were reported to be sleeping on board when it stalled on a crossing.
