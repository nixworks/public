### [2017-10-12](/news/2017/10/12/index.md)

##### Trumpcare Executive Order
# U.S. President Donald Trump signs an executive order allowing the sale of health insurance plans that are exempt from some Obamacare regulations including purchases effective across state lines. 

The new action would make it easier for Americans to buy stripped-down, cheaper polices.
