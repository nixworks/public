### [2017-10-13](/news/2017/10/13/index.md)

##### Human rights in the Philippines
# A group of civil society organizations, "among them groups working on human rights, youth and drug policy reform," have demanded that Apple Inc. remove from its App Store games it said violated the tech firm's guidelines and promoted violence and killings commonplace in the Philippine Drug War.
