### [2017-11-22](/news/2017/11/22/index.md)

# A U.S. Navy C2-A Greyhound, operating off the aircraft carrier USS "Ronald Reagan", crashes in the Philippine Sea. Eight sailors are rescued, while three remain missing from the 11-person crew. 

The U.S. Navy said the cause of the crash is not known at this time.
