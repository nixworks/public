##### Honduran general election, 2017
# Voters in Honduras go to the polls to elect a president for the country, with current President Juan Orlando Hernndez running for a second term, marking the first time a sitting president sought reelection. 

Amid allegations of fraud, President Juan Orlando Hernandez seeks re-election despite constitutional ban.
