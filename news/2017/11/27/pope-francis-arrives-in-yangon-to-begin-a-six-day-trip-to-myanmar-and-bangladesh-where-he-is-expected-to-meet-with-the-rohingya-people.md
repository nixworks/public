##### International reactions to the Rohingya crisis in Myanmar
# Pope Francis arrives in Yangon to begin a six-day trip to Myanmar and Bangladesh, where he is expected to meet with the Rohingya people. 

Officials have warned him not to use the term &quot;Rohingya&quot; to describe Muslims who have fled violence.
