### [2017-11-9](/news/2017/11/9/index.md)

##### Spanish constitutional crisis
# The Supreme Court sends to preventive prison the Speaker of the Parliament of Catalonia, Carme Forcadell. She leaves the prison the next day, after the payment of a bail. Four of the members of the Bureau of the Parliaments are condemned to pay a fine, while one of them not receives any precautionary measure. 

Carme Forcadell and others are accused of rebellion over the Catalan bid for independence.
