### [2017-08-25](/news/2017/08/25/index.md)

# Riots erupt in Panchkula and later spread to other parts of the Northern Indian state of Haryana, as well as the state of Punjab and the Indian capital of New Delhi, after a special CBI court found the Dera Sacha Sauda chief Gurmeet Ram Rahim Singh guilty of rape. At least 28 people have been killed and 250 others injured. 

Violence in Panchkula and Sirsa in Haryana. Punjab and Delhi too hit; HC orders recovery of losses from Dera.
