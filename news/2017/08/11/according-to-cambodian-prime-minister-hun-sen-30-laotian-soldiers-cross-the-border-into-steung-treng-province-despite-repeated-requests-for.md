### [2017-08-11](/news/2017/08/11/index.md)

##### Prime Minister of Cambodia
# According to Cambodian Prime Minister Hun Sen, 30 Laotian soldiers cross the border into Steung Treng Province despite repeated requests for them to leave. Hun Sen gives Laos a six-day ultimatum to withdraw the soldiers or face a military response. 

Hun Sen accuses Laotian troops of trespassing into border area and threatens military response if they fail to withdraw.
