### [2017-12-16](/news/2017/12/16/index.md)

##### Chile
# Five people are killed and at least 15 missing in a mudslide in Villa Santa Lucia, a remote village in southern Chile, after more than 4.5 inches (11.4 cm) of rain the previous 24 hours. President Michelle Bachelet declares a state of emergency. Sunday is the run-off election for Chile's next president. 

A search continues for 15 people missing, after five died in a village in the south of the country.
