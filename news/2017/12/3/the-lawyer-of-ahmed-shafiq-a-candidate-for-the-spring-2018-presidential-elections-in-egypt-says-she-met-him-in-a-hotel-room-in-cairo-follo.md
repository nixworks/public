### [2017-12-3](/news/2017/12/3/index.md)

##### Ahmed Shafiq
# The lawyer of Ahmed Shafiq, a candidate for the spring 2018 presidential elections in Egypt, says she met him in a hotel room in Cairo following his arrest the day before in Dubai, U.A.E., after saying earlier today that his family didn't know his whereabouts. 

The family of Ahmed Shafiq had voiced fears he was kidnapped after returning to Cairo.
