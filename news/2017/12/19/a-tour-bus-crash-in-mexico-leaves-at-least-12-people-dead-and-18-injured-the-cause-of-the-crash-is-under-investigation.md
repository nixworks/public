### [2017-12-19](/news/2017/12/19/index.md)

##### Mexico
# A tour bus crash in Mexico leaves at least 12 people dead and 18 injured. The cause of the crash is under investigation. 

The majority of the passengers came from two ships belonging to Miami-based Royal Caribbean Cruises.
