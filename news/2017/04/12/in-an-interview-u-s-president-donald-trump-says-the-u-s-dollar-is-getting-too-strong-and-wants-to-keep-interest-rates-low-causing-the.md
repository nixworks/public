##### History of the United States dollar
# In an interview, U.S. President Donald Trump says the U.S. dollar is "getting too strong" and wants to keep interest rates low, causing the U.S. Dollar Index to fall. 

The dollar fell broadly on Wednesday after U.S. President Donald Trump said the dollar was &quot;getting too strong&quot; and that he would prefer that the Federal Reserve keep interest rates low.
