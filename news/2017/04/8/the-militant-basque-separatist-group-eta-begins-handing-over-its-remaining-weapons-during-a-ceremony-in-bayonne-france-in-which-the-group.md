### [2017-04-8](/news/2017/04/8/index.md)

#  The militant Basque separatist group ETA begins handing over its remaining weapons during a ceremony in Bayonne, France, in which the group turned over to authorities an inventory of weapons and their locations. 

Weapons caches laid down by the Basque separatist group Eta are now being searched by French police.
