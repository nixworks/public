##### Australian nationality law
# Australia adds stricter requirements to its citizenship application process, including harder tests on English language skills and the requirement that a migrant be able to demonstrate "Australian values". 

Applicants will face stricter tests on their English and &quot;Australian values&quot;, the government says.
