# Twenty people have died and more than a dozen missing after a ferry capsizes in Myanmar's Irrawaddy River delta. 

A ferry boat has capsized in Myanmar&#39;s Ayeyarwaddy delta, killing 20 people and leaving more than a dozen missing, police said.
