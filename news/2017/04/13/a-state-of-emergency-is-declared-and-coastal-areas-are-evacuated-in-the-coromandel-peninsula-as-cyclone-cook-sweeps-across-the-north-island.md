##### 2016-17 South Pacific cyclone season
# A state of emergency is declared and coastal areas are evacuated in the Coromandel Peninsula as Cyclone Cook sweeps across the North Island of New Zealand. 

A storm forecast to be the worst in decades leaves floods, power cuts and downed trees in its wake.
