##### 2017 Sukma attack
# Suspected Maoist insurgents kill at least 24 Central Reserve Police Force (CRPF) officers in the Sukma district of Chhattisgarh, India. 

The police were guarding road workers in Chhattisgarh when they came under attack themselves.
