### [2017-03-12](/news/2017/03/12/index.md)

##### Haiti
# A bus driver in the Haitian city of Gonaives ploughs into crowds of people celebrating Rara while speeding away from an earlier accident which left one person dead, killing at least 38 people, including a group of street musicians, and injures 17 others. 

The bus mows down a group of musicians as the driver tries to flee the scene of a fatal accident in Haiti.
