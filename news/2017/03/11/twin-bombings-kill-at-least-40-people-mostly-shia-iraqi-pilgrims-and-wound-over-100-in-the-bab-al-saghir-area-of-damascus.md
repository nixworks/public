### [2017-03-11](/news/2017/03/11/index.md)

##### Syrian Civil War
# Twin bombings kill at least 40 people, mostly Shia Iraqi pilgrims, and wound over 100 in the Bab al-Saghir area of Damascus. 

The attack targeted buses carrying Shia pilgrims to the Bab al-Saghir cemetery site.
