##### United States immigration ban
# A second U.S. federal judge, Theodore D. Chuang of Maryland, grants that state's motion for a temporary restraining order on President Trump's revised travel ban. 

Judge Theodore D. Chuang in Maryland said there was a &ldquo;convincing case&rdquo; to view the order as a &ldquo;Muslim ban.&rdquo;
