### [2017-03-5](/news/2017/03/5/index.md)

# The Sudan People's Liberation Movement-North releases 127 prisoners from captivity, mostly troops of the Sudanese military. 

Most of those freed by SPLM-N described by army as troops captured in fighting in Blue Nile and South Kordofan states.
