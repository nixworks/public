##### East Timorese presidential election, 2017
# Elections are held in East Timor to vote for the nation's 4th President. Francisco Guterres, runner-up in the 2007 and 2012 elections, is expected to win due to the support of Xanana GusmGBPo. 

Francisco Guterres, who is favoured by key powerbroker Xanana Gusmao, told Fairfax Media there are now &#034;better prospects&#034; for an agreement on sea borders.
