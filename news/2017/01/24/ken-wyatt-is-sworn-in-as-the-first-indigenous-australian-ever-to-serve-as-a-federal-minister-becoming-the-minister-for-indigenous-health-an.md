##### Ken Wyatt
# Ken Wyatt is sworn in as the first Indigenous Australian ever to serve as a Federal minister, becoming the Minister for Indigenous Health and Aged Care. 

Ken Wyatt is the country&#039;s first Indigenous person into the Commonwealth ministry after being sworn in as Minister for Indigenous Health and Aged Care.
