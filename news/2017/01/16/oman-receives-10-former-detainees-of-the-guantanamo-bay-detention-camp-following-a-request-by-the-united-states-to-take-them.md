### [2017-01-16](/news/2017/01/16/index.md)

##### Oman
# Oman receives 10 former detainees of the Guantanamo Bay detention camp following a request by the United States to take them. 

The move means fewer than 50 prisoners remain at the Guantanamo Bay detention centre in Cuba.
