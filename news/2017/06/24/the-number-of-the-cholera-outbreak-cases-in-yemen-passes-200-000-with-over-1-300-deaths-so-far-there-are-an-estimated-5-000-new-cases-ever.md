##### 2016-17 Yemen cholera outbreak
# The number of the cholera outbreak cases in Yemen passes 200,000, with over 1,300 deaths so far. There are an estimated 5,000 new cases every day. 

So far more than 1,300 people have died, 25% children, with numbers expected to rise, the UN says.
