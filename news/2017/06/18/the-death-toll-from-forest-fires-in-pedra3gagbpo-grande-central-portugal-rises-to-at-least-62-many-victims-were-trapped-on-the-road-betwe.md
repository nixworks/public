##### 2017 Portugal wildfires
# The death toll from forest fires in PedrogGBPo Grande, central Portugal, rises to at least 62. Many victims were trapped on the road between the towns of Figueiro dos Vinhos and Castanheira de Pera. 

Many died in their cars while fleeing the fire, which may have been started by a thunderstorm.
