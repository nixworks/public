##### Human rights in China
# Chinese political activist and Nobel Peace Prize laureate Liu Xiaobo is released from prison on medical parole after being diagnosed with terminal liver cancer. 

Liu was awarded Nobel peace prize in 2010 while serving 11-year sentence for inciting subversion of state power
