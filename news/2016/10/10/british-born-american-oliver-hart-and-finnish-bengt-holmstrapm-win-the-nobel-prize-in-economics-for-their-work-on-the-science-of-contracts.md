# British-born American Oliver Hart and Finnish Bengt Holmstrom win the Nobel Prize in Economics for their work on the science of contracts. 

UK-born Oliver Hart and Bengt Holmstrom of Finland have won the Nobel Economics Prize for work on contract theory.
