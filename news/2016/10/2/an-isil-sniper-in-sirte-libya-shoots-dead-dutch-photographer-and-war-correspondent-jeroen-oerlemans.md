##### Libyan Civil War (2014-present)
# An ISIL sniper in Sirte, Libya, shoots dead Dutch photographer and war correspondent Jeroen Oerlemans. 

A Dutch journalist has been killed while covering a government-backed offensive against the Isis jihadist group in the city of Sirte, a medical official has said. Photojournalist Jeroen Oerlemans was shot in the chest by an Isis militant, according to the AFP news agency.
