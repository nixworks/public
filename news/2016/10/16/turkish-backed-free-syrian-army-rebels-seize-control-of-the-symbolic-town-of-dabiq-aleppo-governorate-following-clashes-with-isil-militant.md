##### Syrian Civil War
# Turkish-backed Free Syrian Army rebels seize control of the symbolic town of Dabiq, Aleppo Governorate, following clashes with ISIL militants. 

Syrian rebels said they captured Dabiq, forcing ISIS from a stronghold where it had promised a final, apocalyptic battle with the West.
