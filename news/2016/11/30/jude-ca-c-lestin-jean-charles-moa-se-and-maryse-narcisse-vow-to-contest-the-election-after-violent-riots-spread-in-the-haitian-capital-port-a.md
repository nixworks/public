##### Haitian presidential election, round 2
# Jude Celestin, Jean-Charles Moise and Maryse Narcisse vow to contest the election after violent riots spread in the Haitian capital Port-au-Prince. 

Violent protests erupted in Haiti on Tuesday as losing candidates rejected the preliminary results of an election that indicated Jovenel Mo&iuml;&#8203;se would be the next president.
