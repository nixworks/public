##### Accession of Turkey to the European Union
# Turkish President Recep Tayyip Erdogan threatens to open Turkey's borders to allow refugees and migrants to reach Europe after the European Parliament votes to temporarily freeze talks of Turkey's accession to the EU. 

Calls to halt negotiations with Ankara angers the country&#039;s leader, who warns of triggering a fresh influx of refugees.
