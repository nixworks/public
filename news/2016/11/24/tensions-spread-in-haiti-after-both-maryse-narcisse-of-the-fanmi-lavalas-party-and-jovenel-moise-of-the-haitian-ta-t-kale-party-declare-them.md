##### Haitian presidential election, 2016-17
# Tensions spread in Haiti after both Maryse Narcisse of the Fanmi Lavalas party and Jovenel Moise of the Haitian Tet Kale Party declare themselves winners. 

Police clashed with supporters of presidential candidate Maryse Narcisse in Port-au-Prince as the nation awaits elections results.
