# At least 25 migrants, including 10 children, drown after their wooden boat capsizes in the Aegean Sea near the seaside town of Didim, Turkey. The Turkish Coast Guard was able to rescue 15 people. 

At least 25 people drowned Sunday after a boat carrying migrants capsized off the Turkish coast. Ten of the dead were children.
