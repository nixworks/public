### [2016-03-14](/news/2016/03/14/index.md)

##### March 2016 Ankara bombing
# Turkey's Interior Minister Efkan Ala says the results of the investigation into yesterday's bombing in Ankara will be made public later today. Health Minister Mehmet Muezzinoglu says 125 people were transported to a hospital, with 19 in critical condition. Two Kurdish militants have been blamed for the bombing. 

Turkey begins operations against PKK Kurdish rebels at home and in Iraq, a day after a suicide car bomb in Ankara killed at least 37 people.
