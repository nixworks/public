### [2016-03-23](/news/2016/03/23/index.md)

# Nigerien President Mahamadou Issoufou wins re-election in a second round of voting, receiving 92 percent of the vote in an election that was boycotted by the opposition. 

Incumbent wins 92 percent of runoff vote after opposition calls for boycott.
