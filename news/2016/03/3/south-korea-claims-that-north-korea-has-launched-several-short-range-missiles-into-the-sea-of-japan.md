# South Korea claims that North Korea has launched several short range missiles into the Sea of Japan. 

Seoul (AFP) - North Korea fired short-range projectiles into the sea off its eastern coast on Thursday, South Korea&#x27;s defence ministry said, hours after the UN Security Council imposed tough new sanctions on Pyongyang.
