### [2016-03-26](/news/2016/03/26/index.md)

##### Flint water crisis
#  The U.S. Federal Emergency Management Agency extends the federal disaster declaration for Flint, Michigan, so water deliveries can continue through mid-August. 

FEMA extended its federal disaster declaration in the wake of the Flint water crisis through mid-August so water deliveries can continue.
