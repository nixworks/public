# Thousands of people take to the streets of Warsaw and other cities in Poland in protest against the government after it refused to publish the Constitutional Court's ruling that the government cannot change how the court works. 

Thousands of people are protesting in the Polish capital Warsaw as a constitutional crisis between the government and the top court deepens.
