### [2016-12-6](/news/2016/12/6/index.md)

##### President-elect
#  After a meeting with President-elect of the United States Donald Trump, Masayoshi Son of SoftBank Group pledges to invest $50 billion in the United States and create 50,000 jobs. 

Masayoshi Son, the founder of SoftBank, says a previously announced fund will create some 50,000 jobs by investing in American start-ups.
