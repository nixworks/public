##### Yemeni Civil War (2015-present)
# An explosion outside a military camp in southern Yemen kills at least 49 people and injures many more. The explosion is believed to have been caused by a suicide bomber wearing an explosive vest. 

IS says one of its suicide bombers killed at least 48 Yemeni soldiers queuing for salaries in Aden.
