##### Israel
#  Israel's Foreign Ministry announces that it is "reducing" ties with some of the UN Security Council countries that voted to pass United Nations Security Council Resolution 2334. 

Israel curbed relations with countries that voted in favor of a recent U.N. Security Council resolution condemning the country&#x27;s West Bank settlements.
