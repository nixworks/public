##### Gambian presidential election, 2016
# Opposition candidate Adama Barrow defeats Gambian President Yahya Jammeh, bringing an end to Jammeh's 22-year-rule in the West African nation. 

A former security guard at an Argos catalogue store in London has defeated one of Africa&#39;s most feared strongman leaders to become the new president of Gambia.
