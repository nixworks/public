##### Battle of Aleppo
#  Russia and the United States will continue talks on Aleppo after talks between the U.S. Secretary of State John Kerry and Foreign Minister Sergey Lavrov. 

US State Department confirms Kerry and Lavrov met but no agreement on ceasefire or civilian evacuation was reached.
