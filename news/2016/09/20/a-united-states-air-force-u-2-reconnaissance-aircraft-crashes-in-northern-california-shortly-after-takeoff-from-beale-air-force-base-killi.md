### [2016-09-20](/news/2016/09/20/index.md)

##### U.S. Air Force
# A United States Air Force U-2 reconnaissance aircraft crashes in Northern California, shortly after takeoff from Beale Air Force Base, killing one pilot and injuring another. 

Two pilots ejected safely after a U-2 spy plane went down in Sutter County, just north of Sacramento, according to the U.S. Air Force.
