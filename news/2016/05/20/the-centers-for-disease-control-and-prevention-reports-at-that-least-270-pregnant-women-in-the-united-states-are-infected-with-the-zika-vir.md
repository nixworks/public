##### Zika virus outbreak (2015 - present)
#  The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention reports at that least 270 pregnant women in the United States are infected with the Zika virus. 

More than 270 pregnant women in the U.S. are also infected with the Zika virus, and are worrying about whether their babies will suffer birth defects.
