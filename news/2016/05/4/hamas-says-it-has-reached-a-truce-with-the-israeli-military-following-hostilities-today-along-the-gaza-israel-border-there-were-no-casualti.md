### [2016-05-4](/news/2016/05/4/index.md)

# Hamas says it has reached a truce with the Israeli military following hostilities today along the Gaza-Israel border. There were no casualties. 

The Palestinian Islamist group claimed that Egypt mediated a truce with Israel after 24 hours of escalation and tit-for-tat attacks, Arab press reports said.
