##### Europe
# Europe and the United States lift sanctions imposed on Iran regarding its nuclear programme, and the Joint Comprehensive Plan of Action begins to be implemented. 

International sanctions on Iran are lifted after its compliance with obligations under the nuclear agreement with world powers was certified.
