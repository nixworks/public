### [2016-01-7](/news/2016/01/7/index.md)

# In the United States, a swarm of 30 ("and counting") earthquakes of magnitude 2.5 or greater struck Oklahoma from Wednesday evening through Thursday mid-afternoon. The biggest, a magnitude 4.8, happened yesterday at 10:27 p.m. CST, 32 kilometers (20 miles) northwest of Fairview, Oklahoma. 

Two of the stronger tremors in state history shook northwest Oklahoma. We have the latest.
