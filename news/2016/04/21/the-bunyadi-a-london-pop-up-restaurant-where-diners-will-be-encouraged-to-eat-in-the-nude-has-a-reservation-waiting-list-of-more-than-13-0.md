# The Bunyadi, a London pop-up restaurant where diners will be encouraged to eat in the nude, has a reservation waiting list of more than 13,000 people for its June opening. The restaurant will operate for three months. 

The creators of a planned pop-up restaurant in London where diners will be encouraged to eat in the nude conceded it might not be &quot;a first date venue.&quot;
