##### Amnesty International
# Amnesty International says the Nigerian Army killed 350 members of the Islamic Movement and secretly buried them in mass graves in the city of Zaria in Kaduna State. 

Amnesty International report says military shot and burned members of Islamic Movement in Nigeria last December and secretly buried the bodies
