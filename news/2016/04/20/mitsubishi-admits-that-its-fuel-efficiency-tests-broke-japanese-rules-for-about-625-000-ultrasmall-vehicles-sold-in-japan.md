# Mitsubishi admits that its fuel efficiency tests broke Japanese rules for about 625,000 "ultrasmall" vehicles sold in Japan. 

Mitsubishi Motors admits falsifying fuel economy test data for more than 600,000 vehicles sold in Japan.
