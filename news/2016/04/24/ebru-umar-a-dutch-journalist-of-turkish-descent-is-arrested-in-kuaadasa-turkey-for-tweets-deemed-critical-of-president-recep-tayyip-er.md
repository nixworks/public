### [2016-04-24](/news/2016/04/24/index.md)

##### Ebru Umar
# Ebru Umar, a Dutch journalist of Turkish descent, is arrested in Kusadasi, Turkey, for tweets deemed critical of President Recep Tayyip Erdogan. This comes as a political storm erupted this week over reports that the Turkish consulate asked Turkish organizations in the Netherlands to forward emails and social media posts which insult Erdogan or Turkey. 

Ebru Umar blocked from leaving country after facing judge over tweets deemed critical of president
