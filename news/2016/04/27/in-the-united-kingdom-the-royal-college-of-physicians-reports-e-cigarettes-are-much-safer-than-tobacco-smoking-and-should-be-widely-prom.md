##### United Kingdom
# In the United Kingdom, the Royal College of Physicians reports e-cigarettes are "much safer" than tobacco smoking, and should be widely promoted as an option for smoking cessation. 

Smokers should be offered and encouraged to use e-cigarettes to help them quit, says a leading medical body.
