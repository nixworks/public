##### Iran-European Union relations
# EU Foreign policy chief Federica Mogherini holds the highest-level talks between the EU and Iran in decades to mark a new phase in relations between the two. Both sides are keen to focus on trade, energy and the environment as possible areas of cooperation. 

EU foreign policy chief Federica Mogherini says they have &quot;turned a new page&quot; with Iran, as she visits Tehran for highest-level talks in more than a decade.
