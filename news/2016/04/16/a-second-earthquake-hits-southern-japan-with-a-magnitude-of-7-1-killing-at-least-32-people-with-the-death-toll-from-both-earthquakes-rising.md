##### 2016 Kumamoto earthquakes
# A second earthquake hits southern Japan with a magnitude of 7.1 killing at least 32 people with the death toll from both earthquakes rising to 42. The new quake traps even more people. In Kumamoto a landslide occurs as well as a bridge collapse. 

OZU, Japan (AP) — The latest on the earthquakes in Japan (all times local): 1:30 p.m. Japanese media say the number of people taking shelter after the two earthquakes in southern Japan has doubled to more than 180,000.…
