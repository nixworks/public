### [2016-04-25](/news/2016/04/25/index.md)

##### Saudi Arabia
# Saudi Arabia's Council of Ministers announces a broad-based economic reform plan, known as Vision 2030, which will help the oil-reliant state to diversify its economy over the next 14 years. 

Vision 2030 envisages forming public investment fund, boosting affordable housing and giving expats long-term residence.
