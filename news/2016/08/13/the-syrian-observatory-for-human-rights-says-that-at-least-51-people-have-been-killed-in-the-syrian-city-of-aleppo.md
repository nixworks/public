# The Syrian Observatory for Human Rights says that at least 51 people have been killed in the Syrian city of Aleppo. 

Beirut (AFP) - At least 51 civilians, including four children, were killed in air raids and shelling attacks on Saturday in Syria&#x27;s battleground city Aleppo and the neighbouring countryside, a monitoring group said.
