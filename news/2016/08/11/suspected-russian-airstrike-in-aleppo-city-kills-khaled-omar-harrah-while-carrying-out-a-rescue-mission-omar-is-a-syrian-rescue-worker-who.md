# Suspected Russian airstrike in Aleppo city kills Khaled Omar Harrah while carrying out a rescue mission. Omar is a Syrian rescue worker who became famous around the world after saving a baby buried for 16 hours under rubble. 

A Syrian rescue worker who became famous around the world after saving a baby buried for 16 hours under rubble has been killed in an airstrike.
