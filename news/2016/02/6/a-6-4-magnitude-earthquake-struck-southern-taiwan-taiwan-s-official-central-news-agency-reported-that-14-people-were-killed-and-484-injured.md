##### 2016 Taiwan earthquake
# A 6.4 magnitude earthquake struck southern Taiwan. Taiwan's official Central News Agency reported that 14 people were killed and 484 injured. 

A powerful magnitude 6.4 earthquake strikes southern Taiwan near the city of Tainan, toppling a 17-storey building and trapping people beneath the rubble.
