##### Didcot A power station
# At least one person has been killed and three people remain missing following the partial collapse of a decommissioned turbine hall at Didcot A Power Station in Oxfordshire, England. 

One person dies, three are unaccounted for and five are injured after the collapse of a building at a power station in Oxfordshire.
