### [2016-02-26](/news/2016/02/26/index.md)

##### Air Kasthamandap
#  Air Kasthamandap Flight 9N-AJB, from Nepalgunj headed to the Jumla District, crashes in a field in the Kalikot District mountains in northern Nepal, killing the two pilots and injuring all nine passengers, one seriously. 

Two days after the fatal Tara Air crash, a plane belonging to Air Kasthamandap crash landed at Chilkhaya in Kalikot district on Friday.
