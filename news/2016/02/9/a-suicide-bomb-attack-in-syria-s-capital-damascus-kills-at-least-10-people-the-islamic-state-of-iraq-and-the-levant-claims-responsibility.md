##### Syrian Civil War
# A suicide bomb attack in Syria's capital Damascus kills at least 10 people. The Islamic State of Iraq and the Levant claims responsibility. 

A car bomb tore up a vegetable market and a police officers’ club in the capital of Syria, striking an area that had been quiet for about two years.
