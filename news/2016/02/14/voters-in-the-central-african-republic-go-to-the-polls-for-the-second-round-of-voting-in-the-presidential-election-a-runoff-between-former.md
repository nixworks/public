# Voters in the Central African Republic go to the polls for the second round of voting in the presidential election, a runoff between former prime ministers Anicet-Georges Dologuele (1999-2001) and Faustin-Archange Touadera (2008-13). 

People in the violence-hit Central African Republic vote in the second round of presidential elections.
