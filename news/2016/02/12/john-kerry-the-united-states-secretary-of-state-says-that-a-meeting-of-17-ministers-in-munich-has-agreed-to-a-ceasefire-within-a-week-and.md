# John Kerry, the United States Secretary of State, says that a meeting of 17 ministers in Munich has agreed to a ceasefire within a week and delivery of humanitarian assistance beginning immediately. 

Ministers holding crisis talks over Syria agree to cease hostilities within a week&#039;s time, US Secretary of State John Kerry said.
