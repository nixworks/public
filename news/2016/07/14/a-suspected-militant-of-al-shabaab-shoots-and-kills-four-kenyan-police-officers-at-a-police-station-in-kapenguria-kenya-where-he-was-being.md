##### War in Somalia
# A suspected militant of Al-Shabaab shoots and kills four Kenyan police officers at a police station in Kapenguria, Kenya, where he was being held. The militant was later killed following a shootout with police. 

A rogue officer who &quot;went berserk&quot; was responsible for an attack on a police station in western Kenya on Thursday in which seven people died, a police spokesman said.
