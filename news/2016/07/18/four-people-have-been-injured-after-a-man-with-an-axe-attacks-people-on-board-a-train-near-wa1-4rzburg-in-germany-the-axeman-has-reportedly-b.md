##### 2016 Wurzburg train attack
# Four people have been injured after a man with an axe attacks people on board a train near Wurzburg in Germany. The axeman has reportedly been shot by police and is reportedly a 17-year-old Afghan man who pledged allegiance to ISIL before the attack. 

A teenage Afghan refugee attacks passengers on a train in Germany with an axe and a knife, wounding four, before being shot dead by police.
