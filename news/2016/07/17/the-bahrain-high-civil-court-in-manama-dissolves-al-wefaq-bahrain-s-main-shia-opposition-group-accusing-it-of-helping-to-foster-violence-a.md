##### Bahrain High Civil Court
# The Bahrain High Civil Court in Manama dissolves Al Wefaq, Bahrain's main Shia opposition group, accusing it of helping to foster violence and terrorism in the Sunni-led, Shia-majority island country. The Bahraini court previously issued a three-month suspension of the group last month. 

Manama dissolves main Shia opposition group Al-Wefaq, accusing it of fostering violence in the island kingdom.
