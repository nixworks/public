##### Cambodia
# Cambodia continues to block consensus in the Association of Southeast Asian Nations meeting. The country has political and economical dependencies with China. 

South East Asian nations have released a watered-down statement on the South China Sea that avoids mention of a recent court ruling against Chinese claims.
