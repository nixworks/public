### [2016-07-2](/news/2016/07/2/index.md)

##### Aftermath of the United Kingdom European Union membership referendum, 2016
#  In pro-EU rallies, thousands of people protest in London against the United Kingdom withdrawal from the European Union and ask that Article 50 not be invoked. 

Tens of thousands take to the streets of London to demonstrate against the vote to leave the EU.
