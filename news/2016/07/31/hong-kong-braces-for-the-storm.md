##### Typhoon Nida (2016) 
# Hong Kong braces for the storm. 

China issued an &quot;orange alert&quot; for Typhoon Nida on Sunday, predicting the storm would hit the southern province of Guangdong on Tuesday, state news agency Xinhua reported.
