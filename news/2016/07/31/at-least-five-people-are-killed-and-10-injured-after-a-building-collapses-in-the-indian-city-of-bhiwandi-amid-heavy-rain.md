##### India
# At least five people are killed and 10 injured after a building collapses in the Indian city of Bhiwandi amid heavy rain. 

At least five persons were killed and ten others injured when a building collapsed in the powerloom town of Bhiwandi this morning amid heavy rains. The two-storey structure at Gaibi Nagar under Shanti Nagar police station limits in the town crashed
