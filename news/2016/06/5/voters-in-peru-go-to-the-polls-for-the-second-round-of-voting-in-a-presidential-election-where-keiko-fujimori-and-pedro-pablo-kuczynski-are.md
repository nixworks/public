# Voters in Peru go to the polls for the second round of voting in a presidential election where Keiko Fujimori and Pedro Pablo Kuczynski are the remaining candidates. 

Sunday&#039;s runoff for leadership of the South American nation will pit Pedro Pablo Kuczynski against Keiko Fujimori.
