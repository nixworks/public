# Police open fire on University of Papua New Guinea students in Port Moresby protesting against the government of Prime Minister Peter O'Neill. Four people have been killed and seven injured. However, according to the police, nobody was killed while a university spokesperson claims police only fired tear gas. 

Police in Port Moresby open fire on university students protesting against Papua New Guinea&#039;s Government, injuring at least 17 people.
