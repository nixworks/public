##### 2016 UCLA shooting
# A second victim of the UCLA shooter is found in Minnesota; the shooter is identified as a former Ph.D. candidate in mechanical engineering that claimed his first victim had "cleverly stolen his code." 

The gunman who killed a professor and himself may have also killed a woman in Minnesota, and may have intended to kill another professor, the authorities said.
