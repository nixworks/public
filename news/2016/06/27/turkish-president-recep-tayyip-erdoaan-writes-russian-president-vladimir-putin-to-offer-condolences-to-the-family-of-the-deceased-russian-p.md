### [2016-06-27](/news/2016/06/27/index.md)

##### Russia-Turkey relations
# Turkish President Recep Tayyip Erdogan writes Russian President Vladimir Putin to offer condolences to the family of the deceased Russian pilot of the Sukhoi Su-24 warplane shot down last November. The Kremlin has insisted on a personal apology for months. A Turkish spokesman says this is a step toward improving bilateral relations between the two countries. 

Statement cites Turkish president as saying Ankara ‘never had the desire and the intention’ to down warplane
