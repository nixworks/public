##### Spillover of the Syrian Civil War
# The Syrian Observatory for Human Rights reports at least eight people, including four children, are shot and killed, and another eight wounded by Turkish border guards. The group was trying to cross from northwestern Syria into Turkey's Hatay Province. 

4 children among those reported killed attempting to cross border in northwestern Syria
