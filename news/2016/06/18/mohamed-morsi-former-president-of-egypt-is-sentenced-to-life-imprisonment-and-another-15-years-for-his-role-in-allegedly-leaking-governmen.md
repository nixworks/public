### [2016-06-18](/news/2016/06/18/index.md)

##### Mohamed Morsi
# Mohamed Morsi, former President of Egypt, is sentenced to life imprisonment and another 15 years for his role in allegedly leaking government secrets to Qatar. Six people, including two Al-Jazeera employees, are sentenced to death. 

Death sentences upheld for six defendants, as ex-president and aides receive 25-year jail term in controversial trial.
