##### Iraqi Civil War (2014-present)
# In the Third Battle of Fallujah, ISIL militants attack a military barracks east of Fallujah, killing more than 50 government soldiers and militia members. At least 12 militants were also killed in the assault. 

Government forces and allied Shia militias targeted east of Iraqi city, leaving at least 50 dead.
