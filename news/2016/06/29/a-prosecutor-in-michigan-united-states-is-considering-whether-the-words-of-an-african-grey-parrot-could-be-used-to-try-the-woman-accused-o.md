# A prosecutor in Michigan, United States, is considering whether the words of an African grey parrot could be used to try the woman accused of killing the pet parrot's owner. 

A prosecutor is considering whether Bud, a 19-year-old African grey parrot, could be used in trial of woman accused of shooting his owner, Martin Duram
