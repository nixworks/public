##### CFBDSIR 2149-0403
# Astronomers announce the discovery of CFBDSIR 2149-0403, the closest known rogue planet to Earth at approximately 100 light-years away. 

Astronomers spot a planet wandering the cosmos without a host star about 100 light-years away, finding it to have a mass a few times that of Jupiter.
