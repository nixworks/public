##### Richard O'Dwyer
# British student Richard O'Dwyer reaches an agreement with authorities in the United States to avoid being extradited from the United Kingdom to stand trial over infringement of U.S. copyright laws over his TVShack website which hosted links to pirated films and television shows. 

A British student facing trial and possible imprisonment in the United States strikes a deal to avoid extradition, the High Court hears.
