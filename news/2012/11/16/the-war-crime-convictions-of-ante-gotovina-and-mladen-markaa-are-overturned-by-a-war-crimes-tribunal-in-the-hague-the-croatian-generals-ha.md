### [2012-11-16](/news/2012/11/16/index.md)

##### Ante Gotovina
# The war crime convictions of Ante Gotovina and Mladen Markac are overturned by a war crimes tribunal in The Hague. The Croatian generals had been convicted and sentenced last year for atrocities against Serbs during the 1990's Yugoslav Wars.
