### [2012-11-16](/news/2012/11/16/index.md)

# Labour wins all three by-elections contested in the UK on Thursday, including gaining Corby from the Conservatives. Andy Sawford is elected MP for Corby, Lucy Powell for Manchester Central and Stephen Doughty for Cardiff South and Penarth. 

Labour wins a seat from the Conservatives in a by-election for the first time in 15 years, taking Corby in Northamptonshire.
