##### Syrian civil war
# Syrian rebels kill 28 soldiers in attacks on military checkpoints near the town of Saraqeb in northern Idlib province, hours after a wave of bombings hit Damascus and its outskirts, according to a report from the Syrian Observatory for Human Rights; five rebels also died in gun battles following the attacks. 

Observatory for Human Rights confirms ambush on three checkpoints around northern province of Idlib, bordering Turkey.
