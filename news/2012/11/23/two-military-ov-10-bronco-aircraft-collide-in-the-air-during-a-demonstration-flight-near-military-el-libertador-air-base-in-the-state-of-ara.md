### [2012-11-23](/news/2012/11/23/index.md)

##### OV-10 Bronco
# Two military OV-10 Bronco aircraft collide in the air during a demonstration flight near military El Libertador Air Base in the state of Aragua, Venezuela. One of the pilots, guiding his aircraft away from a populated area, is killed. Three soldiers are also injured in the crash. 

Two military aircraft collided in the air in Venezuela during a demonstration flight. One of the pilots was killed
