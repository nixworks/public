##### UK
# Russia lifts a 16-year ban on the import of British beef and lamb. British farmers had been stopped from exporting to Russia following the 1996 BSE outbreak in the UK, despite the lifting of a worldwide ban in 2006. 

A 16-year-old Russian ban on importing British beef and lamb is to be lifted.
