### [2012-09-14](/news/2012/09/14/index.md)

##### Chinese Marine Surveillance
# Six Chinese Marine Surveillance ships are reported near the disputed Senkaku Islands by the Japanese Coast Guard. China says "two surveillance ship fleets" are in the waters "around" the islands for "patrols and law enforcement".
