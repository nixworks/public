### [2012-09-14](/news/2012/09/14/index.md)

##### HTV-3
# HTV-3 detaches from the ISS for a burial in the Pacific Ocean. ATV 3 thrusts the ISS' orbit two kilometers up. TMA-04M will land on 17 September. ATV-003, named "Edoardo Amaldi", is planned to disconnect on September 25th and burn up itself and a load of trash in the atmosphere over the same ocean.
