### [2012-09-15](/news/2012/09/15/index.md)

##### Irish Daily Star
# Following the decision of "Irish Daily Star" editor Michael O'Kane to publish topless photographs of The Duchess of Cambridge, Northern & Shell chairman Richard Desmond immediately closes down his joint venture with the tabloid. 

Media boss Richard Desmond tonight began &quot;immediate steps&quot; to close down his joint venture with the Irish Daily Star after the newspaper published topless photographs of Kate Middleton.
