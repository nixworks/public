# Singer George Michael cancels the Australian leg of his tour due to "major anxiety", resulting from his recovery from life-threatening pneumonia last November. 

Singer George Michael cancels the Australian leg of his tour due to &quot;major anxiety&quot; following his battle with pneumonia at the end of last year.
