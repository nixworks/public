##### North Korea
# Multiple reports suggest that North Korea is to introduce reforms allowing farmers to keep more of their produce, rather than handing it to the state. 

Reports from North Korea suggest reforms that will see farmers keep more of their crops instead of handing them to the state are to be introduced.
