### [2012-09-11](/news/2012/09/11/index.md)

##### Istanbul
# A suicide bombing in front of a police station in the western Istanbul suburb of Sultangazi kills a policeman, the bomber, and injures several others. The leftist group Dev Sol claims responsibility. 

A Turkish leftist group says it carried out a suicide bombing outside a police station in Istanbul which killed a policeman.
