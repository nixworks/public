##### AntiSec
# The AntiSec hacker operation publishes 1 million edited Apple iOS unique device identifiers of the 12 million it claims to have "obtained" from a FBI laptop. 

Group publishes Unique IDs of 1 million devices online, which they claim were being stored on FBI computer.
