##### News Limited
# News Limited makes a formal bid for James Packer's Consolidated Media valuing the company at 1.94 billion Australian dollars. 

News Ltd has lowered its offer for Pay-TV operator Consolidated Media to $1.94 billion in a formal takeover bid.
