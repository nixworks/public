# The tour bus of American heavy metal band Baroness falls off a 30-foot viaduct in Monkton Combe, near Bath. All nine passengers on board are injured, two seriously. 

Two people on a bus used by the US band Baroness were injured when it fell 30ft from a viaduct near Bath, their agents confirm.
