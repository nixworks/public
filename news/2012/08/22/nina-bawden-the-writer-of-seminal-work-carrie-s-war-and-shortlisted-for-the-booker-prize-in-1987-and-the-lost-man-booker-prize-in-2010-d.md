##### Nina Bawden
# Nina Bawden, the writer of seminal work "Carrie's War" and shortlisted for the Booker Prize in 1987 and the Lost Man Booker Prize in 2010, dies at her London home. 

Nina Bawden, who wrote 48 novels including Carrie&#039;s War, and whose husband died in the Potter&#039;s Bar rail crash, has died at the age of 87.
