##### Tony Scott
# British-born film director Tony Scott dies after leaping 200ft from the Vincent Thomas Bridge in Los Angeles. 

Tony Scott, the British-born Hollywood director behind hits including Top Gun and Crimson Tide, has died after jumping off a Los Angeles bridge, authorities said on Monday.
