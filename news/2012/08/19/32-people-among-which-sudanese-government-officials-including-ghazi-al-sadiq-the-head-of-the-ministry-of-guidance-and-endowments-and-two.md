### [2012-08-19](/news/2012/08/19/index.md)

##### Sudan
# 32 people, among which Sudanese government officials, including Ghazi al-Sadiq, the head of the ministry of guidance and endowments, and two state ministers, are killed in a plane crash in Talodi. 

A Sudanese government minister and other officials die as a plane carrying some 30 people crashes in the south of the country, Sudanese TV says.
