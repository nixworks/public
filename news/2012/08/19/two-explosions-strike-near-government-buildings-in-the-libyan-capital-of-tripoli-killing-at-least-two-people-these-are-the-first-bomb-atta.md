# Two explosions strike near government buildings in the Libyan capital of Tripoli, killing at least two people. These are the first bomb attacks since the end of the Libyan Civil War.

Two people are killed in twin car bomb attacks close to security force buildings in the Libyan capital, Tripoli, officials say.
