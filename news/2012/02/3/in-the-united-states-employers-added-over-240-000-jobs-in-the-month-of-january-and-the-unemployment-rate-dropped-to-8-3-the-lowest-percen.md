### [2012-02-3](/news/2012/02/3/index.md)

##### United States
# In the United States, employers added over 240,000 jobs in the month of January and the unemployment rate dropped to 8.3%, the lowest percent in three years. 

American employers substantially stepped up their hiring in January, bringing the unemployment rate down for the fifth month in a row.
