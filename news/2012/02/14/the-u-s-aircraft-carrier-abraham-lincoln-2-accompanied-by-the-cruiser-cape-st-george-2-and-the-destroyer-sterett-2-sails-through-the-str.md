##### Strait of Hormuz
# The U.S. aircraft carrier Abraham Lincoln 2, accompanied by the cruiser Cape St. George 2 and the destroyer Sterett 2, sails through the Strait of Hormuz, close to the coast of Iran, for the second time in recent weeks. 

A US aircraft carrier passes through the Strait of Hormuz for the second time in recent weeks, amid heightened tension with Iran.
