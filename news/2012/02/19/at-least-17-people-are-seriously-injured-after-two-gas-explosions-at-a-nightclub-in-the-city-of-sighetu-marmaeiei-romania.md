##### Sighetu Marmatiei
# At least 17 people are seriously injured after two gas explosions at a nightclub in the city of Sighetu Marmatiei, Romania. 

Two explosions injured 17 people at a nightclub in the northern Romanian town of Sighetu Marmatiei, emergency services said Sunday.
