# South Korea proceeds with live-fire drills near the disputed Korean sea border despite threats from North Korea of "merciless" strikes in retaliation. 

NORTH Korea has threatened to launch &quot;merciless&quot; strikes if South Korea goes through with planned live-fire drills near their disputed sea border.
