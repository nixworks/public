# U.S. Secretary of State Hillary Clinton making "excellent progress" at NewYork-Presbyterian Hospital after a blood clot in a vein was found in a sinus between her brain and skull. 

US Secretary of State Hillary Clinton is making &quot;excellent progress&quot; after a blood clot was found between her brain and skull, doctors say.
