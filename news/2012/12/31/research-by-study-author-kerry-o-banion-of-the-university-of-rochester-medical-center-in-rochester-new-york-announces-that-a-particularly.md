### [2012-12-31](/news/2012/12/31/index.md)

##### University of Rochester Medical Center
# Research by study author Kerry O'Banion, of the University of Rochester Medical Center in Rochester, New York, announces that a particularly harmful type of space radiation may accelerate the development of Alzheimer's disease. 

Space radiation exposure could accelerate Alzheimer&#039;s disease in the brains of astronauts, a new study suggests. A single two-year mission to Mars could be enough to create the effect, scientists say.
