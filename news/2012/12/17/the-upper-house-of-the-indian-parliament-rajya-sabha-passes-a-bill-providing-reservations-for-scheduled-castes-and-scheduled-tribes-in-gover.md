##### Rajya Sabha
# The upper house of the Indian parliament Rajya Sabha passes a bill providing reservations for Scheduled castes and Scheduled tribes in government job promotions. 

The Rajya Sabha on Monday passed the constitution amendment bill to provide reservations in promotions for the scheduled castes (SCs) and the scheduled tribes (STs).
