##### NASA
# NASA's twin GRAIL probes have revealed the surface of the Moon in unprecedented detail, showing unexpectedly-deep cracks, craters and tectonic structures. 

The scale of the battering the Moon received early in its history is revealed in remarkable new data from two Nasa satellites.
