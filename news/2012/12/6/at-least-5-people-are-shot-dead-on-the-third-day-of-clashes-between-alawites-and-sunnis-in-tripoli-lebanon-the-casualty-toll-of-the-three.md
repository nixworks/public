### [2012-12-6](/news/2012/12/6/index.md)

# At least 5 people are shot dead on the third day of clashes between Alawites and Sunnis in Tripoli, Lebanon. The casualty toll of the three days is at least 10 killed and 73 injured. 

* Ten people killed since clashes started on Tuesday* Tensions high after Lebanese gunmen killed in Syria* Syrian FM says Lebanese go
