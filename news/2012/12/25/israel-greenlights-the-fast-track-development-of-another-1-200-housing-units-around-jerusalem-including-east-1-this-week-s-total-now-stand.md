### [2012-12-25](/news/2012/12/25/index.md)

##### Israel
# Israel greenlights the fast-track development of another 1,200 housing units around Jerusalem, including East 1. This week's total now stands at 5,500, the largest in some time. 

Plan includes 930 apartments for immediate construction and around 300 that could be built at a later time.
