# Banking giant UBS is fined $1.5 billion for attempting to manipulate the Libor interbank lending rate, becoming the second international bank, after Barclays, to be fined over the Libor scandal. 

Swiss banking giant is given $1.5bn fine for attempting to manipulate the Libor interbank lending rate.
