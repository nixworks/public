### [2012-12-5](/news/2012/12/5/index.md)

##### European Commission
# The European Commission fines Philips, LG Electronics, Samsung SDI, Panasonic, Toshiba and Technicolor a total of EUR1.47 bn for fixing prices of TV cathode-ray tubes. 

Two Panasonic joint ventures were also fined. Taiwanese firm Chunghwa Picture Tubes blew the whistle on the cartel and was escaped a fine.
