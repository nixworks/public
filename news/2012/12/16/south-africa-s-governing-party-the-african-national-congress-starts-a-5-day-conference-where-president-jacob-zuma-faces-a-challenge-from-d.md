# South Africa's governing party, the African National Congress, starts a 5-day conference where President Jacob Zuma faces a challenge from Deputy President Kgalema Motlanthe. 

South African President Jacob Zuma defends his record at the African National Congress (ANC) conference as he faces a leadership challenge.
