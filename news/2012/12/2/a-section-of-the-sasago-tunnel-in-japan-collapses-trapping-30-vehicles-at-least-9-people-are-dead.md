### [2012-12-2](/news/2012/12/2/index.md)

##### Sasago Tunnel
# A section of the Sasago Tunnel in Japan collapses trapping 30 vehicles; at least 9 people are dead. 

At least seven people are missing after a major road tunnel collapses west of Tokyo, trapping a number of vehicles, Japanese media say.
