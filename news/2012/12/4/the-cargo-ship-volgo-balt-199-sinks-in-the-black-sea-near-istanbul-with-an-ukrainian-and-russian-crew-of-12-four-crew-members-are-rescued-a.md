### [2012-12-4](/news/2012/12/4/index.md)

##### Black Sea
# The cargo ship Volgo Balt 199 sinks in the Black Sea near Istanbul with an Ukrainian and Russian crew of 12. Four crew members are rescued and one is found dead, while the rest are missing. Two rescuers are killed and two others left missing after their boat hit rocks during the search operations.
