##### Parliament
# The Parliament of New South Wales officially appoints Bob Carr as a member of the Australian Senate filling a casual vacancy left by the resignation of Mark Arbib. 

Despite earlier heckling from a protester, Bob Carr is officially endorsed by the NSW Parliament to fill the Senate vacancy left by Mark Arbib.
