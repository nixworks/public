# Fuel tanker drivers belonging to the Unite union in the United Kingdom vote overwhelmingly to take strike action in a dispute over terms and conditions. 

Fuel tanker drivers have voted overwhelmingly to go on strike in a row over terms and conditions, says the Unite union.
