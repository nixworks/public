##### Nike, Inc.
# U.S. sportswear company Nike, Inc. controversially gives its new Saint Patrick's Day runner the name "The Black and Tan", prompting comparisons from concerned netizens with the controversial British paramilitary unit, which was known as the Black and Tans. 

Sports wear company Nike has apologised for a controversy that arose over the name of its new runner - &quot;The Black and Tan&quot;.
