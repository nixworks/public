# Heavy rains in the Australian state of New South Wales leads to evacuations or warnings in the towns of Cooma, Goulburn, Queanbeyan and near the town of Kiama. 

Residents in parts of Goulburn are being ordered to leave their homes and businesses immediately because of flooding.
