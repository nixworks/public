### [2012-03-12](/news/2012/03/12/index.md)

# Gaza-Israel clashes continue as Palestinian militants fire more Qassam rockets into Israel overnight. 2 people are killed, including a militant, and 25 are injured during Israeli Air Force strikes on the Gaza Strip. 

Terrorists launch 31 rockets into Israel, causing damages, lightly injuring 2 in Ashdod; Palestinians: 5 killed by IAF.
