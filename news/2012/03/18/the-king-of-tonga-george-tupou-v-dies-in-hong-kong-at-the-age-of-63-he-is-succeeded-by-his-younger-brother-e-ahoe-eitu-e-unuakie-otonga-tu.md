##### King of Tonga
# The King of Tonga George Tupou V dies in Hong Kong at the age of 63. He is succeeded by his younger brother, `Aho`eitu `Unuaki`otonga Tuku`aho. 

The King of Tonga has died in Hong Kong, sparking an outpouring of grief in the Pacific nation. - New Zealand Herald
