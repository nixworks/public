### [2012-03-8](/news/2012/03/8/index.md)

# British Prime Minister David Cameron announces that two engineers - Briton Chris McManus and Italian Franco Lamolinara - who were abducted by Islamic extremists in Nigeria in May 2011 were killed during a rescue attempt by British and Nigerian forces. 

A British engineer and his Italian colleague, who were abducted in Nigeria last year, have died in a failed rescue attempt, David Cameron says.
