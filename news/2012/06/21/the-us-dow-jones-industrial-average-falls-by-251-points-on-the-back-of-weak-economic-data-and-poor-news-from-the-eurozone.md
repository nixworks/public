##### Dow Jones Industrial Average
# The US Dow Jones Industrial Average falls by 251 points on the back of weak economic data and poor news from the Eurozone. 

Stocks traded mostly higher at the open Thursday, with traders shrugging off a fresh batch of economic reports and a rising dollar as Europe&#039;s debt woes continued to intensify.
