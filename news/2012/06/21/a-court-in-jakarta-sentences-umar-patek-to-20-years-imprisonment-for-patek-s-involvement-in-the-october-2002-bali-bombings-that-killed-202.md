### [2012-06-21](/news/2012/06/21/index.md)

##### Jakarta
# A court in Jakarta sentences Umar Patek to 20 years' imprisonment for Patek's involvement in the October 2002 Bali bombings that killed 202 people. 

Indonesian court finds Umar Patek, accused of assembling explosives for 2002 attack on nightclub, guilty on all counts.
