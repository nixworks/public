##### Venezuela
# The Venezuelan government outlaws the commercial sale of guns and ammunition, the latest in a series of initiatives to improve security and cut crime. 

Venezuela brings in a new law banning the commercial sale of guns and ammunition, in an attempt to control worsening crime rates.
