### [2012-06-16](/news/2012/06/16/index.md)

##### Egypt
# Voters in Egypt go to the polls for the second round of a presidential election, with ex-Prime Minister Ahmed Shafiq and Mohamed Morsi of the Muslim Brotherhood competing for the presidency. 

High turnout reported in some governorates as Egyptians turn out to cast ballots amid deep political uncertainty.
