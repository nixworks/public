##### Elizabeth II
# Elizabeth II attends a lunch at Westminster Hall following the Jubilee thanksgiving service at St Paul's Cathedral. Celebrations ends with a flypast by the Red Arrows. 

Four days of Diamond Jubilee events culminate in an appearance by the Queen on the Buckingham Palace balcony in front of huge crowds and an RAF flypast.
