##### Sikorsky H-34
# Search crews locate the wreckage of a missing Sikorsky H-34 helicopter near Mt. Mamarosa in southern Peru, with all 14 on board confirmed dead. 

The wreckage of a helicopter missing in southern Peru has been found with all 14 on board, who include foreign nationals, confirmed dead.
