##### Gaza Strip
# Rocket attacks from the Gaza Strip are followed by Israeli airstrikes in response, resulting in at least one death. 

Fresh air raid leaves one Palestinian dead, after Hamas fighters fired 45 rockets across the border in 24 hours.
