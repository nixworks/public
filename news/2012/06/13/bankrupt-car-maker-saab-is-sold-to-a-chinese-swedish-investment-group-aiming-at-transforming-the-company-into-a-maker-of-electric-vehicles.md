##### Saab Automobile
# Bankrupt car maker Saab is sold to a Chinese-Swedish investment group, aiming at transforming the company into a maker of electric vehicles. 

Bankrupt Swedish carmaker Saab has been sold to an investment group which aims to develop the company into a maker of electric cars.
