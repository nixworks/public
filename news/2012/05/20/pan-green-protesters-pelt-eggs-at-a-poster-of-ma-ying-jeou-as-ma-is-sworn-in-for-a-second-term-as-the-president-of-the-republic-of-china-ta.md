# Pan-Green protesters pelt eggs at a poster of Ma Ying-jeou as Ma is sworn in for a second term as the President of the Republic of China (Taiwan), after winning the presidential election in January. 

Ma Ying-jeou begins second term amid protests over living costs and policies seen by critics as too China-friendly.
