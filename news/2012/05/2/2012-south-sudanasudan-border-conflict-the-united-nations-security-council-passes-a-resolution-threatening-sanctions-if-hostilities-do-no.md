# 2012 South Sudan-Sudan border conflict: The United Nations Security Council passes a resolution threatening sanctions if hostilities do not end within 48 hours. 

Security Council passes resolution that gives Sudan and South Sudan just 48 hours to halt hostilities or face sanctions.
