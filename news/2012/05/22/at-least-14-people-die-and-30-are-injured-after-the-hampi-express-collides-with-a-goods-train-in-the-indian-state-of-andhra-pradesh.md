##### Hampi Express
# At least 14 people die and 30 are injured after the Hampi Express collides with a goods train in the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. 

AT LEAST 14 people have been killed and about 30 injured when a passenger train collided with a stationary goods train in southern India.
