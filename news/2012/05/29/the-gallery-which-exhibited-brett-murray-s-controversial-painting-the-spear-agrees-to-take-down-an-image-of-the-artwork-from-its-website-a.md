# The gallery which exhibited Brett Murray's controversial painting "The Spear" agrees to take down an image of the artwork from its website as thousands of people march in protest through the streets of Johannesburg. 

Thousands march through the streets of Johannesburg as outrage and tension over controversial painting continue.
