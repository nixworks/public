### [2012-05-31](/news/2012/05/31/index.md)

##### Petrol
# A nationwide strike against rising petrol prices closes shops and disrupts public transport in India, with the government facing widespread criticism over its economic management. 

Cities disrupted as opposition parties and trade unions call nationwide protest after 11 per cent rise in fuel costs.
