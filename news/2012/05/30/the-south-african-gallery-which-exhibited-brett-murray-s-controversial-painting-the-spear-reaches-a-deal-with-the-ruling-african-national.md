##### South Africa
# The South African gallery which exhibited Brett Murray's controversial painting "The Spear" reaches a deal with the ruling African National Congress (ANC), which opposes its display. 

A South African art gallery agrees not to display a painting of President Jacob Zuma with his genitals exposed after reaching a deal with the ANC.
