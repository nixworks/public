##### Prince Willem-Alexander
# Crown Prince Willem-Alexander, the heir to the Dutch throne, buys a villa for EUR4.5 million beside the Greek town of Kranidi near a home owned by Russian President-elect Vladimir Putin. 

The heir to the Dutch throne, Crown Prince Willem-Alexander, buys a villa in southern Greece where Vladimir Putin is said by Dutch media to own a home.
