##### Prime Minister
# British Prime Minister David Cameron makes a historic visit to Burma (Myanmar), becoming the first British Prime Minister to visit that country. 

The Prime Minister David Cameron today called for sanctions against Burma to be eased as he made a historic visit to the country.
