# The U.S. Secret Service dismisses three more employees over the prostitution scandal involving U.S. agents at last weekend's 6th Summit of the Americas in Colombia. 

The US Secret Service says three more employees are resigning over the prostitution scandal at an international summit in Colombia last weekend.
