##### 2012 Northern Mali conflict
# 2012 Northern Mali conflict: The United Nations Security Council unanimously passes a resolution approving an African-led force to assist the army of Mali in ousting Islamist militants who have seized the north of the country. 

The U.N. Security Council unanimously approved a plan to back an African-led military force to help the Malian army oust Islamic militants who seized the northern half of the country.
