##### Paju
# Activists descend on the city of Paju to float 50,000 propaganda leaflets on balloons into North Korea, despite protests from local South Korean residents concerned at provoking a military response. 

Activists in South Korea float propaganda leaflets to the North, a week after Pyongyang threatened military action over a similar launch.
