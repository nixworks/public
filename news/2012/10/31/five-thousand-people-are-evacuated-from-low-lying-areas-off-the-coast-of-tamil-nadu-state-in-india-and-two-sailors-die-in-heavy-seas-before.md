### [2012-10-31](/news/2012/10/31/index.md)

##### Tamil Nadu
# Five thousand people are evacuated from low-lying areas off the coast of Tamil Nadu state in India and two sailors die in heavy seas before Cyclone Nilam hits the coast. 

Cyclone Nilam today swept across north Tamil Nadu coast near Mahabalipuram leaving two people dead, including a crew member of an oil tanker.
