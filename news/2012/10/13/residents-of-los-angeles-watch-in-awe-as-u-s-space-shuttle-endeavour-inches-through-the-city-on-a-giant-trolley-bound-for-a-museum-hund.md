# Residents of Los Angeles watch in awe as U.S. Space Shuttle "Endeavour" inches through the city on a giant trolley, bound for a museum. Hundreds of trees in its path are chopped down. 

The retired US space shuttle Endeavour finally reaches its new home in a Los Angeles museum after long delays caused by trees along the route.
