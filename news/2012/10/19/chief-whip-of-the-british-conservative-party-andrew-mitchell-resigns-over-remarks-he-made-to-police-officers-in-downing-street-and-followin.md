# Chief Whip of the British Conservative Party Andrew Mitchell resigns over remarks he made to police officers in Downing Street, and following a lengthy political row over the issue. 

Chief Whip Andrew Mitchell resigns over rude remarks he made to police officers in Downing Street and will be replaced by Sir George Young.
