##### Google
# Trading of Google stock on NASDAQ is temporarily suspended after it drops 9% following an inadvertent early release of its quarterly report showing a 20% decline in profits. 

Trading in Google shares was suspended for over two hours after it released financial results early by mistake, showing a 20% profit fall.
