### [2012-10-5](/news/2012/10/5/index.md)

##### Anglo American Platinum
# Anglo Platinum Limited&mdash;the world's biggest platinum producer&mdash;fires 12,000 people in South Africa after a strike over working conditions. The corporation has stated that the strikes have cost it 39,000 ounces in output - equivalent to 700 million rand ($82.3 million; GBP51 million) in revenue.
