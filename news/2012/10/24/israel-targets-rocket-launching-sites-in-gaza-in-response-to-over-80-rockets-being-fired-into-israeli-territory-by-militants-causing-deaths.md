##### Israel
# Israel targets rocket launching sites in Gaza in response to over 80 rockets being fired into Israeli territory by militants, causing deaths and injuries. 

Israel carries out air strikes in the Gaza Strip, killing four Hamas militants, after rocket and mortar-fire wounds five people in southern Israel.
