### [2004-07-6](/news/2004/07/6/index.md)

##### Senate Select Committee on Intelligence
#  The U.S. Senate Select Committee on Intelligence uncovers that, before the War on Iraq, the Central Intelligence Agency (CIA) was told by relatives of Iraqi scientists that Iraq's programs to develop unconventional weapons had been abandoned.
