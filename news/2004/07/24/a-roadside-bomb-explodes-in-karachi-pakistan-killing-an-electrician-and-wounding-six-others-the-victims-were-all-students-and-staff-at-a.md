### [2004-07-24](/news/2004/07/24/index.md)

##### Karachi
#  A roadside bomb explodes in Karachi, Pakistan, killing an electrician and wounding six others. The victims were all students and staff at an Islamic seminary, and appear to be the targets of the attack.
