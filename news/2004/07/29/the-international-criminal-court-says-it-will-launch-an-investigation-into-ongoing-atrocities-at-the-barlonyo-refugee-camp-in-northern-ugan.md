### [2004-07-29](/news/2004/07/29/index.md)

##### International Criminal Court
#  The International Criminal Court says it will launch an investigation into ongoing atrocities at the Barlonyo refugee camp in northern Uganda. Reports say that more than 200 people have killed by rebels from the Lord's Resistance Army since the beginning of the year.
