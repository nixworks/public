### [2004-07-22](/news/2004/07/22/index.md)

##### Prosecutor
#  Ignacio Carrill, the Special Prosecutor for Past Social and Political Movements in Mexico, presents the findings of the investigation into the "dirty war", where it classifies the killings by government forces as genocide, and requests warrants be issued for the arrest of former president Luis Echeverra and 11 other ex-government figures.
