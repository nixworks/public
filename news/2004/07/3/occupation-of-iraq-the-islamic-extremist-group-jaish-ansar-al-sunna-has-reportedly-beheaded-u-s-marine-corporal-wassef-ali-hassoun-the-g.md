### [2004-07-3](/news/2004/07/3/index.md)

##### Occupation of Iraq
#  Occupation of Iraq: The Islamic extremist group Jaish Ansar al-Sunna has reportedly beheaded U.S. Marine Corporal Wassef Ali Hassoun. The group claims that it will release a videotape recording of the execution in the coming days.
