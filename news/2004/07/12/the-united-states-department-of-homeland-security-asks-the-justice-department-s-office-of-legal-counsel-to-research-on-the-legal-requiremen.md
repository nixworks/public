### [2004-07-12](/news/2004/07/12/index.md)

##### Department of Homeland Security
#  The United States Department of Homeland Security asks the Justice Department's office of legal counsel to research on the legal requirements for postponing the November elections, stating that they are concerned that terrorists might disrupt the elections.
