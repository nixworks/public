### [2004-04-10](/news/2004/04/10/index.md)

##### Saddam Hussein
#  Iraq marks the anniversary of Saddam Hussein's fall with fighting and bloodshed. Kurds, in the relatively stable north, celebrate with parties and the melting of an ice statue of the ousted dictator.
