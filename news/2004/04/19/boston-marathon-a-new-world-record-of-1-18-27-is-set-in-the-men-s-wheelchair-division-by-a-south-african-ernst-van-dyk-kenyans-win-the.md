### [2004-04-19](/news/2004/04/19/index.md)

##### Boston Marathon
#  Boston Marathon: A new world record of 1:18:27 is set in the men's wheelchair division by a South African (Ernst Van Dyk). Kenyans win the women's race and the men's for the 13th time in fourteen years.
