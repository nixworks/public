### [2004-04-18](/news/2004/04/18/index.md)

#  The new Prime Minister of Spain, Jos Luis Rodrguez Zapatero, orders that all Spanish troops should be brought home from Iraq "in as short a time as possible", in accordance with "the will of the Spanish people".
