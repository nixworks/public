### [2004-04-2](/news/2004/04/2/index.md)

##### Michael Grade
#  The BBC announces that Michael Grade will become its new Chairman on May 17, following the resignation of its previous Chairman Gavyn Davies in the fall-out from the Hutton Inquiry report.
