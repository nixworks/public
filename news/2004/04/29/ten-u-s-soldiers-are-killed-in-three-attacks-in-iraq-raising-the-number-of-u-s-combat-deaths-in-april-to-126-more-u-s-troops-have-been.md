### [2004-04-29](/news/2004/04/29/index.md)

##### Iraq
#  Ten U.S. soldiers are killed in three attacks in Iraq, raising the number of U.S. combat deaths in April to 126. More U.S. troops have been killed this month than during the six weeks of "major combat" in 2003.
