### [2004-04-20](/news/2004/04/20/index.md)

##### Mortar fire
#  12 mortars were fired on Abu Ghraib Prison by insurgents. 22 detainees were killed and 92 wounded. Iraqi security forces who fled the fighting in Fallujah begin to return to the city.
