### [2004-10-23](/news/2004/10/23/index.md)

##### 2004 Chuetsu earthquake
#  A powerful 2004 Chuetsu earthquake 6.7 measuring and six aftershocks of similar scale occur in the Tokamachi area. A huge landslide occurs on the outskirts of Nagaoka. Both area is southern and central Niigata Prefecture in Japan. According to Japanese officials, 68 people are killed, 4,085 are injured, and 103,000 are rendered homeless.
