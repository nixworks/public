### [2004-10-3](/news/2004/10/3/index.md)

##### Pope
#  Pope John Paul&nbsp;II beatifies five persons, including Anne Catherine Emmerich, a German nun, and Karl&nbsp;I, last emperor of the Austro-Hungarian Empire, in a ceremony in Rome. John Paul&nbsp;II has made a total of 1,340 beatifications (including today's), more than all previous popes combined.
