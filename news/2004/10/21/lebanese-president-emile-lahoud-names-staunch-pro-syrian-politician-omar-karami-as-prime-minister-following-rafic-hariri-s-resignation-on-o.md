### [2004-10-21](/news/2004/10/21/index.md)

##### Lebanon
#  Lebanese President mile Lahoud names staunch pro-Syrian politician Omar Karami as Prime Minister following Rafic Hariri's resignation on October 20, 2004. Karami, Prime Minister from 1990 to 1992, was forced to resign in 1992.
