### [2004-10-1](/news/2004/10/1/index.md)

##### Denmark
#  The government of Denmark is investigating whether it can claim ownership of the North Pole, by studying how far the underwater portion of Greenland, a Danish territory, extends. Canada and Russia are already undertaking similar investigations regarding their own claims.
