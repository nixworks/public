### [2004-10-27](/news/2004/10/27/index.md)

##### Guantanamo Bay
#  Four British citizens, who were detained at the US military installation in Guantanamo Bay for almost three years, sue the U.S. government for GBP5.5 million ($10 million) each, alleging torture and other human rights violations. The principal defendants are Secretary of Defense Donald Rumsfeld and Chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff, General Richard Myers.
