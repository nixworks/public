### [2004-10-22](/news/2004/10/22/index.md)

##### Kyoto Treaty
#  The Kyoto Treaty on climate change is ratified by Russia's State Duma, the lower house of parliament. The treaty will now go to the upper house and President Vladimir Putin for their approval.
