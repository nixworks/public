### [2004-11-3](/news/2004/11/3/index.md)

##### Charles Robert Jenkins
#  Sgt. Charles Robert Jenkins, who crossed over the DMZ to North Korea in 1965, pleads guilty to desertion and aiding the enemy at his court martial in Japan. He is sentenced to 30 days in prison and given a dishonorable discharge.
