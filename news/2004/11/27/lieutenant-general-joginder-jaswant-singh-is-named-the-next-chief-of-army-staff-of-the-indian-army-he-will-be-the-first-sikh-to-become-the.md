### [2004-11-27](/news/2004/11/27/index.md)

##### Lieutenant General
#  Lieutenant General Joginder Jaswant Singh is named the next chief of army staff of the Indian Army. He will be the first Sikh to become the chief. 

NEW DELHI: The Lt-Gen will be the first Sikh to lead the 1.13 million-strong Indian Army.
