### [2004-11-30](/news/2004/11/30/index.md)

##### International Committee of the Red Cross
#  A report from the International Committee of the Red Cross, recently leaked to the "New York Times", describes the treatment of prisoners at the U.S. base in Guantnamo Bay, Cuba as being "tantamount to torture". The U.S. military disputes this.
