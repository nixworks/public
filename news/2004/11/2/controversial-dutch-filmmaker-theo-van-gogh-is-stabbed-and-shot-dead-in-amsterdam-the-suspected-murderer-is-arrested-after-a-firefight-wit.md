##### Theo van Gogh
#  Controversial Dutch filmmaker Theo van Gogh is stabbed and shot dead in Amsterdam; the suspected murderer is arrested after a firefight with police. van Gogh had received numerous death threats after his movie "Submission" elicited criticism among the Dutch Muslim community.
