### [2004-11-1](/news/2004/11/1/index.md)

##### Sinai Peninsula
#  An inquiry by the Egyptian Interior Ministry into last month's bombings of hotels in the Sinai concludes that the perpetrators received no external help, contradicting assertions by Israeli officials that the blasts were linked to al-Qaeda.
