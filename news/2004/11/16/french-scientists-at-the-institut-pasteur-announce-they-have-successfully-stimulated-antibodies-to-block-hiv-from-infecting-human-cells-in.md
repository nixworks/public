### [2004-11-16](/news/2004/11/16/index.md)

##### Institut Pasteur
#  French scientists at the Institut Pasteur announce they have successfully stimulated antibodies to block HIV from infecting human cells "in vitro". The achievement is a significant breakthrough towards the goal of an HIV vaccine.
