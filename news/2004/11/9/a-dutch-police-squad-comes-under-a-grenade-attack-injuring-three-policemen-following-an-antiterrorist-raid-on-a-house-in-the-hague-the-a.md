### [2004-11-9](/news/2004/11/9/index.md)

##### Netherlands
#  A Dutch police squad comes under a grenade attack, injuring three policemen, following an antiterrorist raid on a house in The Hague. The area's airspace is closed as a precaution. Two arrests have been made.
