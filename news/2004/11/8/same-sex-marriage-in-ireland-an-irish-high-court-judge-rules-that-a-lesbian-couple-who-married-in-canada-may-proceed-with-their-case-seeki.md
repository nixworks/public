#  Same-sex marriage in Ireland: An Irish High Court judge rules that a lesbian couple who married in Canada may proceed with their case seeking to have their marriage recognized in Ireland. 

Lesbian couple, married in Canada, can ask to have their marriage legally recognized in Ireland.
