### [2004-03-9](/news/2004/03/9/index.md)

##### Pakistan
#  Pakistan announces a successful first flight test of its Hatf&nbsp;VI - Shaheen&nbsp;II long-range nuclear-capable ballistic missile. The missile has a range of 2000 km and can carry a payload of 1000 kg.
