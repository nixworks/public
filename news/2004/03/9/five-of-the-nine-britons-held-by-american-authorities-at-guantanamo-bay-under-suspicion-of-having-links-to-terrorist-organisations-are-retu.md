### [2004-03-9](/news/2004/03/9/index.md)

##### Guantnamo Bay
#  Five of the nine Britons held by American authorities at Guantanamo Bay under suspicion of having links to terrorist organisations are returned to Britain. They are to be questioned by British anti-terrorism police on arrival.
