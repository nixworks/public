### [2004-03-15](/news/2004/03/15/index.md)

##### Haiti
#  Haiti recalls its ambassador of neighbouring Caribbean state Jamaica, where ousted president Jean-Bertrand Aristide is said to be making a personal visit. Haiti also threatens to boycott a 2-day Caricom meeting.
