### [2004-09-25](/news/2004/09/25/index.md)

##### 2004 Atlantic hurricane season
#  2004 Atlantic hurricane season: Hurricane Jeanne strengthens slightly as it passes over the northern Bahamas and makes landfall at 11:50 p.m. local time at Hutchison Island, just east of Stuart, Florida, as a Category Three storm with maximum sustained winds of 120&nbsp;mph (195 km-h). About 3 million people are ordered to evacuate vulnerable areas in Florida.
