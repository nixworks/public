### [2004-09-15](/news/2004/09/15/index.md)

##### UTC
#  As of 13:00 local time (1800 UTC September 15), the center of Hurricane Ivan is located about 275 km south of the coast of Alabama and is moving northward at about 23 km-h (14&nbsp;mph). The hurricane is now projected to make landfall along the Gulf Coast of the United States very early on Thursday. Forecasters now predict that there is little risk that the hurricane will pass over New Orleans. A hurricane warning is in effect for the Gulf Coast from New Orleans to the Florida Panhandle.
