### [2004-09-14](/news/2004/09/14/index.md)

##### Tal Afar
#  The United States lifts its siege of the northern Iraqi city of Tal Afar after Turkey threatens to end all cooperation with the U.S. in Iraq if the attacks, which had killed many civilians in the largely Turkmen city, continue.
