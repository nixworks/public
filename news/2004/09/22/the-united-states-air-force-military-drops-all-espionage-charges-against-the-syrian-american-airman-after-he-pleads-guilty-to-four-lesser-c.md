### [2004-09-22](/news/2004/09/22/index.md)

##### Espionage
#  The United States Air Force military drops all espionage charges against the Syrian-American airman after he pleads guilty to four lesser charges. The judge criticizes the prosecution for improperly handling key evidence and for failing to correct the mistranslation of a crucial document.
