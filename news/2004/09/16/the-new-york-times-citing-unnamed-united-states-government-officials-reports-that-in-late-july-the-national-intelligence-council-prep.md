### [2004-09-16](/news/2004/09/16/index.md)

##### The New York Times
#  "The New York Times," citing unnamed United States government officials, reports that, in late July, the National Intelligence Council prepared a pessimistic classified report for President George W. Bush that predicted three likely outcomes for Iraq by the end of 2005, the best of which is "tenuous stability", and the worst of which is a descent into civil war.
