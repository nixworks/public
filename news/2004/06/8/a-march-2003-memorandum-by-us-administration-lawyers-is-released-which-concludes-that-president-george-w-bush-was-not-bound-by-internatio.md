### [2004-06-8](/news/2004/06/8/index.md)

##### George W. Bush
#  A March 2003 memorandum by US administration lawyers is released, which concludes that President George W. Bush was not bound by international treaty or by federal law against torture because the commander-in-chief had the authority to protect national security.
