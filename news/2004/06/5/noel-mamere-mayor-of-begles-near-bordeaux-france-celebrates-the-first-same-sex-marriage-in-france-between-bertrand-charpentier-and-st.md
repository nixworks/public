### [2004-06-5](/news/2004/06/5/index.md)

##### Nol Mamre
#  Nol Mamre, mayor of Bgles (near Bordeaux), France, celebrates the first same-sex marriage in France, between Bertrand Charpentier and Stphane Chapin. Interior minister Dominique de Villepin states that the wedding is illegal and announces that the mayor will face censure. Mamre claims he is interpreting French law, inspired by similar actions in San Francisco and other US cities.
