### [2004-08-12](/news/2004/08/12/index.md)

##### San Francisco, California
#  Unanimously determining that San Francisco's same-sex marriages are illegal, the California Supreme Court votes 5-2 to annul all 4,000 such marriages performed in San Francisco this year.
