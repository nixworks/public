### [2004-08-2](/news/2004/08/2/index.md)

##### Iraq
#  The Iraqi government blames Al Qaeda ally Abu Musab al-Zarqawi for a series of church bombings that killed at least 11 people, saying the aim was to spark religious strife and drive Christians out of the country.
