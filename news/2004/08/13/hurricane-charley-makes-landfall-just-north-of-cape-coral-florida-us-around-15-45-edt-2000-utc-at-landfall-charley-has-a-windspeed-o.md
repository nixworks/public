### [2004-08-13](/news/2004/08/13/index.md)

##### 2004
#  Hurricane Charley makes landfall just north of Cape Coral, Florida, US, around 15:45 EDT (2000 UTC). At landfall, Charley has a windspeed of 150&nbsp;mph (241&nbsp;km-h), air pressure 941 mbar, Category 4 storm on the Saffir-Simpson hurricane scale. There are multiple fatalities.
