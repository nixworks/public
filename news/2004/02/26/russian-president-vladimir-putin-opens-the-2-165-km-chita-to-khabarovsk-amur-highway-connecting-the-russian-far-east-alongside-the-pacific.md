### [2004-02-26](/news/2004/02/26/index.md)

##### Chita
#  Russian President Vladimir Putin opens the 2,165 km Chita-to-Khabarovsk Amur Highway connecting the Russian Far East alongside the Pacific to the rest of the country. Construction of the highway was begun in 1978.
