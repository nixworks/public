### [2004-02-15](/news/2004/02/15/index.md)

##### Caltech
#  Scientists at the California Institute of Technology announce the discovery of a galaxy which is the farthest known object in the universe. The galaxy was found with the help of the magnification effect from the Abell 2218 galaxy cluster.
