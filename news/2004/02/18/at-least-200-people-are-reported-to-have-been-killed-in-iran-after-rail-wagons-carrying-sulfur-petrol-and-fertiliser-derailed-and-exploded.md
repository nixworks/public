### [2004-02-18](/news/2004/02/18/index.md)

##### Iran
#  At least 200 people are reported to have been killed in Iran after rail wagons carrying sulfur, petrol and fertiliser derailed and exploded. The accident happened near the town of Nishapur in Khorasan province.
