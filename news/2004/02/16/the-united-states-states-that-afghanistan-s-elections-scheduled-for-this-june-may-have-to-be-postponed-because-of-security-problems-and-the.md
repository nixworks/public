### [2004-02-16](/news/2004/02/16/index.md)

##### Afghanistan
#  The United States states that Afghanistan's elections scheduled for this June may have to be postponed because of security problems and the failure to register enough voters. Only 8% of eligible Afghan voters have been enrolled to date.
