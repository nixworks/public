### [2004-02-27](/news/2004/02/27/index.md)

##### International Space Station
#  International Space Station crew Michael Foale and Aleksandr Kaleri perform the first ever spacewalk involving the station's entire crew; the spacewalk is cut short by a malfunction in Kaleri's spacesuit.
