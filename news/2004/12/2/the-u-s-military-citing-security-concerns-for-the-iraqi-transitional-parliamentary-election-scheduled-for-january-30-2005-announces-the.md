### [2004-12-2](/news/2004/12/2/index.md)

##### Iraq War
#  The U.S. military, citing security concerns for the Iraqi transitional parliamentary election scheduled for January 30, 2005, announces the deployment of 1,500 additional troops to Iraq and tour extensions bringing the number to an all-time high of 150,000.
