### [2004-12-27](/news/2004/12/27/index.md)

##### Israeli-Palestinian Conflict
#  Israeli-Palestinian Conflict: Israeli police arrest Palestinian presidential candidate Mustafa Barghouti. A Jerusalem police spokesman says Mr Barghouti was "detained for questioning because he has the right to transit through Jerusalem but not be in Jerusalem itself".
