### [2004-12-3](/news/2004/12/3/index.md)

##### Brazil
#  Brazilian paleontologists of the Federal University of Rio de Janeiro announce a find of a new dinosaur species, "Unaysaurus tolentinoi". The find also shows links to Europe when both continents were part of Pangaea.
