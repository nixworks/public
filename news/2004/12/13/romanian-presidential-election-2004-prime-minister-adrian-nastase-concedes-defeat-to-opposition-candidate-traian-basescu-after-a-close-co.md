### [2004-12-13](/news/2004/12/13/index.md)

##### Romanian presidential election, 2004
#  Romanian presidential election, 2004: Prime Minister Adrian Nastase concedes defeat to opposition candidate Traian Basescu after a close contest. With 99% of the vote counted, Basescu took 51.23% of the vote to Nastase's 48.77%.
