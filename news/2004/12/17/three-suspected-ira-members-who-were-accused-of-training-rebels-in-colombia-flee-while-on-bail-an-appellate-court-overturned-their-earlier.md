### [2004-12-17](/news/2004/12/17/index.md)

##### Three suspected
#  Three suspected IRA members who were accused of training rebels in Colombia flee while on bail. An appellate court overturned their earlier acquittals yesterday. They had been sentenced to prison for 17 years.
