##### Inter-American Court of Human Rights
#  The Inter-American Court of Human Rights orders Guatemala to pay USD $7.9 million in compensation to 317 survivors of the 1982 Plan de Snchez massacre in which soldiers and paramilitaries killed 268 villagers.
