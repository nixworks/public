### [2004-12-21](/news/2004/12/21/index.md)

##### J.K. Rowling
#  Author J.K. Rowling announces that the sixth book in her Harry Potter series, "Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince", will be published on 16 July 2005. The share price of her British publisher, Bloomsbury, rises 7.5% on the news.
