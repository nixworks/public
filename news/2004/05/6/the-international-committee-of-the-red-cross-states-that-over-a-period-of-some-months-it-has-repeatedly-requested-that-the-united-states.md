### [2004-05-6](/news/2004/05/6/index.md)

##### Abu Ghraib prison
#  The International Committee of the Red Cross states that, over a period of some months, it has repeatedly requested that the United States take action on alleged prisoner abuse at Iraq's Abu Ghraib prison.
