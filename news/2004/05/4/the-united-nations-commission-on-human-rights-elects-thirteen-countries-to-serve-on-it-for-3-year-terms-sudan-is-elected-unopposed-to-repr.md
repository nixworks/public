### [2004-05-4](/news/2004/05/4/index.md)

##### United Nations Commission on Human Rights
#  The United Nations Commission on Human Rights elects thirteen countries to serve on it for 3-year terms. Sudan is elected unopposed to represent the African bloc, prompting a walk-out by the U.S. delegation.
