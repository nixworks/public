##### Shieik Abdul-Satar al-Bahadli
#  Shieik Abdul-Satar al-Bahadli, a senior aide to Muqtada al-Sadr, is offering a reward of 250,000 dinars (~&nbsp;USD 170) to any Iraqi who captures a British woman soldier; he says the captive will be kept as a concubine.
