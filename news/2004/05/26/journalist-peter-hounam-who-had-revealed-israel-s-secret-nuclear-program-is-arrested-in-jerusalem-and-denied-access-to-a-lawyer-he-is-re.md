### [2004-05-26](/news/2004/05/26/index.md)

##### Peter Hounam
#  Journalist Peter Hounam, who had revealed Israel's secret nuclear program, is arrested in Jerusalem and denied access to a lawyer. He is released and expelled from the country the following day.
