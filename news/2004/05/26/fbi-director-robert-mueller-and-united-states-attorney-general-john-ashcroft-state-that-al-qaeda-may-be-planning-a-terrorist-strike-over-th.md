### [2004-05-26](/news/2004/05/26/index.md)

##### FBI
#  FBI Director Robert Mueller and United States Attorney General John Ashcroft state that Al Qaeda may be planning a terrorist strike over the coming months. Multiple FBI officials contend that there is no recent intelligence to suggest a significant change in the US's security situation, and critics question the validity and timing of the public warning.[https:--www.nytimes.com-2004-05-27-politics-27terror.html?th (NYT)] Seven people wanted for questioning are also named.
