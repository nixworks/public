### [2004-05-17](/news/2004/05/17/index.md)

##### Heathrow
#  Police in London foil an armed robbery at the Heathrow Cargo Centre, which attempted to steal GBP40 million (some USD 70 million) in gold and GBP30-GBP40 million in cash. Six men are arrested and another is being sought by police.
