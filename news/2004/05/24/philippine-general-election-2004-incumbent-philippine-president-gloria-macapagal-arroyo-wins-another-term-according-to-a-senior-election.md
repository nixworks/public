### [2004-05-24](/news/2004/05/24/index.md)

##### 2004 elections
#  Philippine general election, 2004: Incumbent Philippine President Gloria Macapagal Arroyo wins another term according to a senior election official who leaks the narrow winning margin of about 3% or 900,000 votes. An independent watchdog group confirms the figures.
